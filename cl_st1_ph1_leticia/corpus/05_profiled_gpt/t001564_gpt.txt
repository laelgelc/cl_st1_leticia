It’s become a really long‑term thing now, this India project, more like a community than just a series of retreats. There’s a huge amount of admin attached to it, on top of all the things I’m already doing – the books, the website, the texts, videos, all the information stuff that has to go out. It’s all grown very organically over the years, so there are a lot of people involved now in one way or another, but in practice the three of us are still carrying most of the work.

We’ve started to realise that’s not really sustainable, so we’re trying to bring more people into it properly, so that it’s more shared. Not in some grand democratic way or anything, just so that the load isn’t always falling on the same three shoulders and other people can take real responsibility for bits of it.
