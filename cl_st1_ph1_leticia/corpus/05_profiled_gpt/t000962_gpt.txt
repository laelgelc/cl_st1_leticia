I decided I really ought to crack down and get something done, rather than just sit about, so I went out into the garage and got on with it. I was cutting up those great big boards, you know, for the big Norfolk, and once I’d got going it felt quite good to be doing something practical. The next thing I’ve got in mind is to make a banner, that’s the next job on the list. I even went up to the church to pick up the church banner, but it was just far too wet. Still, doing all that has made me feel properly, fully alert.
