the legend goes that these village children in Woolpit were out in the fields and they found this boy and girl, brother and sister, and their skin was actually green. They didn’t speak English, nobody could understand them, and they were terrified. At first they would only eat green beans, nothing else at all, and the boy was so sad and unsettled that in the end he just faded and died. The girl, though, gradually started eating other food, and as she did, her green colour disappeared. Over time she learned to speak, and she explained they’d come from a place where it was always dark. They’d followed the sound of music, gone through a sort of tunnel into the sunlight, and ended up here. Eventually she grew up and married someone local.
