He’s such a character, that robin. He’ll actually follow us from window to window, watching what we’re doing, and as soon as we head outside he’s there, flying along beside us. When we go down the garden to put the food out, he comes right in close, almost under our feet while we’re putting it down, and then he’ll just sit and eat quite happily near us as if he owns the place. He used to be even tamer, you know – we could touch him and he’d take food from our hands without a fuss. He doesn’t let us do that anymore, though. I’m sure it’s because he’s got a nest somewhere now, and he’s that bit more cautious, looking after things.
