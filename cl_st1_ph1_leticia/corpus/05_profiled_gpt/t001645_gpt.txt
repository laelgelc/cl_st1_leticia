I just find it desperately depressing, really, that after all those years of sex education and women’s liberation, young women still seem to be missing out on what should be an important and joyful part of life. It feels as though, somewhere along the line, we’ve completely failed them. I can’t help thinking it’s tied up with ignorance about what healthy sex actually is, and not knowing their own bodies properly. In that sense, it’s almost as if sex education has just collapsed, hasn’t it?
