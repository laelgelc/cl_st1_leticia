Aye, that robin shot was actually without any zoom at all. I’d pulled into this wee snowy car park at the start of Loch Luneck, just north of Callander. I walked down to the waterfront to set the tripod up, getting ready to take a few landscape shots. While I was faffing about with the gear, this robin just appeared out of nowhere.

It landed right beside me on the wall, practically next to my camera bag. I just stayed absolutely still, didn’t move a muscle, and it seemed quite happy to sit there in the snow. Because it was so close in, I didn’t need a zoom lens – just framed it carefully and fired off a couple of close‑up shots. After a wee minute or so it eventually flew off again, but it gave me enough time to get the photos you saw.
