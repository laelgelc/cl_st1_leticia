So the dragon went up to them and said, “Is iron and steel the same thing?” and they all said, “Yes.” But the dragon shook his head and said, “No, you’re wrong.” Then he started to explain it all to them. He said, “Iron is old and rusty and black and it’s natural, it comes from the ground.” Then he told them, “Steel is smooth and shiny and it’s man-made, people have to make it.” The dragon kept talking and told them lots more about iron and steel and even about silk and other things as well.
