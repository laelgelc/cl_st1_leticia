I was up early, about half six in the morning, going out to a car boot sale. As I was leaving, I noticed this car pull into our street and just sit there, didn’t move while I went off. Then I saw another police car come in at the bottom of the street. I thought to myself, something serious must’ve happened here. I kept watching for a long time, expecting to see something or someone brought out of the house, but nothing came out at all.
