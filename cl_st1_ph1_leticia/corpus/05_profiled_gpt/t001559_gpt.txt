We went to the Comedy Store and it was just really awkward from the start. I went up to the bloke on the door and asked if there were any seats or, you know, any free tickets going, and he barely responded. He just sort of shut it down straightaway, really brusque, and that was that. No effort to be friendly or anything, which you’d kind of expect from a comedy place. I came away feeling quite disappointed, thinking, well, this is meant to be a comedy club, but it doesn’t feel welcoming at all. It just didn’t have the fun atmosphere I’d hoped for.
