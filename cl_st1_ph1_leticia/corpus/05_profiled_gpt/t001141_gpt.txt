Before I went to uni I literally just wrote to loads of publishing companies, like properly blanket-emailed them, and most of them either rejected me or just didn’t reply. A couple did say yes though, so I ended up doing a bit of work experience with them back then. 

Recently I went back through all those old contacts and re-emailed them with my updated CV and everything I’ve done since, just asking if there were any opportunities going. One of the women I’d worked with before replied and said they were actually looking to fill a New Year internship. She asked to see my CV, had a look over it, and then a few days later she emailed again and invited me to come in for an interview.
