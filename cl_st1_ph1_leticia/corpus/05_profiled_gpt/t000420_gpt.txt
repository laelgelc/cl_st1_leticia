I woke up one morning and the skin on the palms of me hands was just suddenly peeling off, like it wasn't there the day before and then it was all coming away. It was really like having a dead man's hand, just all this skin flaking off, and it was so frightening and freaky to look at. I went to the doctor and they said it was contact dermatitis, and they gave us some cream for it, and luckily that made it go away.
