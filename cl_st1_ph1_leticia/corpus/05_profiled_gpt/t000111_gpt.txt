When my older one was very little, and I probably had the younger in a sling, I actually let her wander off in British Home Stores on purpose. I stayed just behind her, hiding behind the clothes rails, watching. I was waiting for that moment when she’d suddenly think, “Oh, mummy,” turn round and not be able to see me. You could see the fear starting to come over her face. Just before it tipped into absolute terror, I came out from behind her and said, “Did you lose mummy?” She spun round and threw her arms round my neck. It really taught her a lesson. I did feel quite mean, but it worked.
