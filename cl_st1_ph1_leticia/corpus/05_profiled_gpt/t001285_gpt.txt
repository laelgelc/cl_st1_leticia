On the ship where I’ve been teaching, I got to know one of the Filipino barmen who’s very keen on learning languages. We ended up having quite a lot of fun with it. I taught him a bit of Greek, but especially some Turkish. Every morning I’d greet him with “gunaydin”, and he picked it up very quickly and enjoyed using it back at me. He really seems to relish trying out new phrases. The ship itself is due to go around Southern Africa in the spring, which adds another interesting dimension to it all.
