He is a bit better than he used to be, I’ll give him that, but honestly, the other day was ridiculous. I went in and his room was just a huge mess again – clothes all over the floor, stuff scattered all over his desk, you couldn’t see much carpet at all. I told him to tidy it up, and when I looked back in later it seemed fine from the doorway, like he’d actually done what I’d asked. 

It looked as though he’d made a proper effort, everything off the floor, bed looking neat, desk cleared. I even said to myself, oh, that looks much better. But then my other half pointed out that I hadn’t actually checked properly, just had a quick glance from the landing. So we went back in and had a closer look. 

Turned out he’d literally just scooped all the clothes up, shoved them into the corner of the bed up by his pillow, and pulled the quilt over the top so you couldn’t see a thing. From the doorway it looked immaculate, but everything was just hidden in the bed.
