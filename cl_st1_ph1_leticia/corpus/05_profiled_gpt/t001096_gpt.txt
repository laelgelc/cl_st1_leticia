Some of them just can’t really understand the music, so we’ve had to make it very simple for them. We colour-code their notes so they can see straightaway which ones are theirs. On top of that, we actually mark the music to show them which hand they’re supposed to use for each note, so they don’t get muddled up when they’re picking up the bells. We also mark whether they should damp the sound quickly or let it ring on for a long time.
