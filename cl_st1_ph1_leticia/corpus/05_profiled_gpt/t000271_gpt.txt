Ray was the one who really helped me with the bell-ringing, but he had no close family round here. He was never married, a bachelor, and in the end he moved down to Winchester, where his nearest relatives are, and of course there’s the cathedral there as well. Another chap who helped me was a fireman, but he’s got three jobs on the go, so he’s often busy and can’t always be around. The third one’s a doctor, and he’s actually connected to a retired colleague from our maths department, who comes out walking with us now. I was saying to him I wanted to do something really different in retirement, not just sit in the pub or watch things.
