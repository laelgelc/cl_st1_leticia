when I first moved to Spain I didn’t speak a word of Spanish and they put me straight into teaching this little English class of six and seven year olds. at the beginning they were actually really well behaved, all sat there very neatly, and I thought oh this is going to be fine. but then they cottoned on to the fact that I didn’t understand any Spanish at all, and that was it, they just went completely wild because they realised they could say anything and I’d have no idea. it only settled down again once they understood that, whatever they did, I was still only going to respond in English, which they didn’t understand either.
