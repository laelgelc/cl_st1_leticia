I did always work in the holidays, but not all the way through. I’d usually do about a week at the beginning of the summer and a week at the end, getting things sorted for the new term. Then there’d be a few days before term started again at Christmas, and roughly a week at Easter as well. Even with that, I still ended up with about six weeks’ proper holiday a year, which I think is actually a really decent amount of leave, especially compared to someone working in business.
