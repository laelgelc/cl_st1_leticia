So on Valentine’s Day me and my sister were trying to pick a Nando’s. The one near her house is tiny and always rammed, so we didn’t bother with that. There’s another branch we just call too crusty, so that was a no as well. Then there’s a really big one, but we knew that would be absolutely full of people, especially with all the couples out. In the end we decided to go to this more remote Nando’s in an almost empty area, basically nothing there, because we thought it would be quiet.
