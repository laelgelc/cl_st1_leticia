I remember saying to him, look, this just isn’t right. He’d been offered this employment project that was supposedly starting in April, and they were talking about paying a full year’s grant up front in November and even holding the space for him until then. I said to him, that’s too good to be true, it doesn’t sound right at all. But he was absolutely adamant it was all above board, purely because the woman who’d put it to him seemed so utterly convinced herself. He kept insisting it must be genuine, and I kept saying I simply didn’t believe it.
