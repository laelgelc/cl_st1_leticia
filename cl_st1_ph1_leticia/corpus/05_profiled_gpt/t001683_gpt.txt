I really don’t fancy doing another ten years, no. It’s quite hard being the oldest one in the office, actually. You get the sense some of the younger ones are just willing the older people to retire and move out of the way, and I don’t want to hang on until I’m that person everyone’s hoping will go. I’d much rather stop when it starts to feel like a trial, when it all gets a bit samey and I’m struggling to come up with new ideas, than just keep plodding on for the sake of it.
