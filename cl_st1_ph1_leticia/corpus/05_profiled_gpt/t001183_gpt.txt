At work when I was asking people for ideas, loads of them had done all these little trips and were saying how easy it is. They were like, if you don’t go to the obvious big touristy places and you pick somewhere a bit more random, it can be really cheap. You can just fly out for the weekend for like forty or fifty quid, and then stay in a little B&B or something. It doesn’t have to be some massive, expensive holiday at all, just a quick, cheap weekend away.
