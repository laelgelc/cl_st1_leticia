Well, I think they must have been the norm, really. There’s evidence for at least six of these places, and it’s believed there were as many as twelve along the high street. People used to talk about the Primpt Restaurant under the Angel Hotel, but that’s actually an undercroft, not a restaurant in origin at all.

Ours was built as a shop, sometime between about twelve‑fifty and thirteen hundred. You can still see parts of the original stairs, which is rather nice, and there’s an original window surviving as well. There’s also a little arched doorway, very small, and it’s thought that would have led to a spiral staircase going up to the house above. That staircase is ruined now, of course, but you can imagine how it must have looked when it was all in one piece.
