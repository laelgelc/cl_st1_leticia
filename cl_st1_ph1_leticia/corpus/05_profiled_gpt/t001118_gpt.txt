and he was showing us how so much of it is just where you look, really. Like with the coin trick – if you pretend to take the coin from one hand but actually just drop it, as long as you keep your eyes fixed on the hand that’s “got” the coin, everyone else will stare at that hand too. Then when you open it and there’s nothing there, they’re amazed and think it’s vanished. So he was saying proper stage magic is mostly about body language and your gaze, rather than anything actually supernatural.
