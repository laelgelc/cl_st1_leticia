I can actually imagine myself spending a few years in Northern Ireland again, you know. It really is so beautiful, and being back there reminded me of that. If I did move back – and I might, I’m not ruling it out – there are all these things I never took advantage of before. 

For example, there’s this local sailing club where you can just sign up as crew on Wednesday nights. You go out with them, sail up and down the loch, and learn about boats as you go. It sounds brilliant, really, and exactly the sort of thing I’d love to try if I were living there again.
