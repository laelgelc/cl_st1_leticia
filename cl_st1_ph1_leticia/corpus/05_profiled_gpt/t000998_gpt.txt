rendang it’s like an Indonesian or Malaysian curry, honestly I reckon it’s the best curry in the world. You do these big chunks of beef, sear them off first, and then you cook them really, really slowly. The flavour comes from tamarind, kaffir lime, lemongrass, a little bit of brown sugar, and loads of coconut cream. You just keep it going until the sauce reduces right down to this really thick, dark paste that’s surprisingly sweet for a curry. Then, right at the end, you turn the heat up really high so it sort of caramelises in the beef fat, or lamb fat if you use lamb instead.
