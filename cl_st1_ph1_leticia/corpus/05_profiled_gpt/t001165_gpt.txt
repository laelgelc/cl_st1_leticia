She’s got loads of food issues, so everything has to be just so, even down to this special bread she has. For years she relied on the Co‑op’s fine cut lemon marmalade, absolutely loved it, got through about a jar every week or two. Then of course they discontinued it, and that was a real problem for her. I decided I’d try to sort it, so I contacted the Co‑op and then the original producer. I asked them to send me the full ingredient list, because I thought if I knew exactly what was in it, I could hunt around and find another maker doing something as close as possible.
