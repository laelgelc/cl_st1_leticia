I just think, like, first year at uni is more about getting your life together than anything else. You’re basically starting a new life and learning a whole new way of living, so it’s more about getting used to the experience. It’s such a big change, so meeting people and joining in with different activities is a huge part of it as well. It doesn’t have to be all about being amazing academically straightaway. You can focus more on properly excelling in your work in later years, once you’ve settled in and you’re more used to how everything works here.
