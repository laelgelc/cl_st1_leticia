For my birthday he did this really elaborate surprise, even though he’d already left the country. First the doorman came up with this box, and inside was a freeze-dried rose and a card. The card told me to go to my computer. On the computer there was this Word document I couldn’t open because it was password-protected.  

He’d hidden the password in the last love letter he’d sent me. So I had to sit there and work it out, going through the letter, and when I finally figured it out the password was “I love you,” which was really sweet.  

When I opened the document there were more instructions, telling me what to do next and where to look. It basically sent me on this continued search, like a little treasure hunt he’d set up from afar, each step leading to another clue. It was all very romantic and kind of mad, considering he wasn’t even in the country anymore.
