At work we had a stock transfer where loads of council properties went over, and it really changed things. There was this big house, proper valuable, with a family of eight living in it. After the transfer, the chief executive was desperate to get them out so he could flog the place. He pushed and pushed for an eviction, purely because of what the house was worth. I stepped in and said, hang on, you can’t just do that, it’s not legal, and that stopped it. But it showed me once a chief exec’s in charge instead of an elected councillor, it’s all about the bottom line.
