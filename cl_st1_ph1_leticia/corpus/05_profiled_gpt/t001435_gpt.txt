A few weeks ago I was in a lift and this man stepped out and I thought, I know you from somewhere. It bothered me that he looked so familiar, so I asked him where he lived, and then I said, “Did you used to drink at the ex-servicemen’s club?” When he said yes, it all came back. I told him he used to drink Manns Brown, and I’d remembered it exactly right. He was absolutely amazed that I could recall his drink from all that time ago.
