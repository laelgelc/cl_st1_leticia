I was watching this thing, it was like one of those BBC bits where the reporter’s walking along talking to the camera, then stopping people in the street. They were asking everyone about the EU, and what struck me was how most people were just openly saying they didn’t really know that much about it. But then as soon as immigration came up, nearly everyone suddenly had really strong opinions. It was people of all different ages and both men and women, and they all reacted like that. I actually found it really interesting to see.
