I honestly don’t know how he survived. He looked like he’d been on the street for quite a while, so maybe he just moved on somewhere else, but I’ve no idea. What really got to me was that we actually had this spare apartment, completely unused, that I was responsible for. I came very, very close to just offering it to him, because it felt awful seeing him out there every day. But in the end I held back, because I knew I was responsible for that place.
