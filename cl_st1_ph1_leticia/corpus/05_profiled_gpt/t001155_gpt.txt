We all sat down and brainstormed it together in the end. I’d started off with this idea of “we care” being shown by the world, and I was really keen on getting hands into all of the images somehow. But it turned out to be quite hard to work hands into every single one of the values. So once we hit that problem, everyone chipped in with more ideas and different ways of doing it. I then went away and did some rough sketches, we had another meeting to go through and refine them, and after that I finished the final versions and sent them in.
