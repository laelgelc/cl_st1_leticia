I was absolutely fine at the start of the night, just out as normal, and then it was like suddenly I was completely gone. One minute I remember being okay, and then after that it’s all a blur. I barely remember how I even got home. Apparently at some point I must’ve gone through my phone and called basically everyone in my contacts. He was one of the people who actually picked up. The next day, when I finally checked my phone properly, there were loads of missed calls and messages from people. They were all asking where I was, if I was okay, even if I was in hospital.
