I’d had this little travel alarm clock in the bedroom for years, a tiny thing, and it finally gave up the ghost. So I went and bought another one, very similar – small, neat, just what I wanted. But when I actually tried to use it, the ticking was absolutely unbearable. I put it by the bed that first night and I just couldn’t cope with it at all, it was so loud. I didn’t even manage one night with it in the bedroom before deciding I couldn’t tolerate it.
