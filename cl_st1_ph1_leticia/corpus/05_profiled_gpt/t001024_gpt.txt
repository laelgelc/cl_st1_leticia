yeah I was listening this morning to Johnnie Walker’s “Sounds of the Seventies,” and the first hour was all about David Bowie. They had these interviews with people talking about how he sort of invented himself, you know, and became this character. And sometimes you got the impression even he wasn’t entirely sure if he really was that character or just playing it. But that’s what struck me – that you can just decide who you want to be and perform that, and it doesn’t have to be fake. It’s not like it’s some big lie, it’s just you choosing a role and living it. So I think that’s the thing, really: you can choose whatever character you want in life, and rather than agonising over whether it’s “real” or not, just do it.
