well people in offices use Tippex, it’s this white liquid, to cover up little mistakes on documents. You sort of paint it over the error, let it dry, and then when you photocopy the page the mistake doesn’t show up anymore, it just looks blank or corrected. It’s quite a strong smell actually, you notice it as soon as someone opens the bottle. Some silly students at school used to sniff it to get themselves all over‑excited, and then, being daft, they’d end up pouring it all over their textbooks as well.
