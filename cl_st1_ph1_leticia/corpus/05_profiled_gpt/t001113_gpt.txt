Last night we were talking about how scary it would be to have a daughter and just be constantly worrying about her safety. It really stuck with me, that idea of always having to think about what might happen to her, what situations she’s in, what she’s wearing, how late it is, all of that. And I just don’t think it should be on her to have to learn all these ways to protect herself, as if it’s her job to stop bad things happening. It really should be about educating young men not to attack people in the first place, not to behave like that at all. I feel really strongly about that.
