well one of the ways you could look at it is if you got yourself a one bedroom place to begin with, then if she doesn’t come over you’re still only classed as a single person, so your benefit situation stays the same. but if she does come, and you’re on DHSS at that point, then you’d be classed as having a dependent, and that changes how they look at you for housing. you might then be considered for rehousing into a two bedroom, because you’d officially have a child living with you.

it’s more likely to work in the other area, where there isn’t the same pressure on housing, they’re not as oversubscribed as here.
