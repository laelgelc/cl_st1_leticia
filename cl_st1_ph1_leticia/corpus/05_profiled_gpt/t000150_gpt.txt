Sometimes when I’m sat there in traffic having this really stressful internal monologue, I’m like, if I had something really important to do, if my job really mattered, then I could sort of justify it. Like, if I had loads of responsibility, proper meetings to get to, people relying on me, then yeah, maybe the horrible commute would feel worth it. But instead I’m just sat there thinking, realistically, it probably doesn’t even really matter if I get to work that day or not, and that just makes the whole thing feel even more pointless.
