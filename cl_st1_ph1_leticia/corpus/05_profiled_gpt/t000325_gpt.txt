On twenty-five grand here I’m losing about twenty percent straightaway in tax, then another ten percent in National Insurance, and another ten percent on student loans. On top of that there’s council tax and all the other bits, plus twenty percent VAT on what I buy. By the time you add it all up I’m basically paying around fifty percent in tax overall. It just doesn’t feel cost‑effective compared to going somewhere else, earning more and actually saving. Once I’ve got my PhD I can go into lecturing on about forty grand a year, and that’s really the income bracket I need.
