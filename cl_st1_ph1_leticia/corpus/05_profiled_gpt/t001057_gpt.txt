Nowadays I really do strongly disapprove of servicewomen having babies while they’re serving. If you’re a young mother you simply can’t put the service first, and that means someone else has to pick up the slack. It’s not fair on your colleagues if they’re constantly covering for you because your child quite rightly comes first. In my day there was a rule that you couldn’t join if you had children under fifteen, and I always thought that was fair. Younger children need a parent to prioritise them; the career should come second.
