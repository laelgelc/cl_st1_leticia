yeah so we’d all actually got on the plane to come back to Heathrow, and then they suddenly told us to get off, didn’t say why, just that there’d be a delay. They herded everyone into this tiny little waiting room and then basically abandoned us there. No toilets we were allowed to use, no water, no food, and nobody telling us what was happening. We could still see all these men in orange overalls crawling all over the outside of the plane, like Guantanamo jumpsuits, just carrying on working while we sat there.

After a few hours people just started to lose patience, and it turned into this huge mutiny. Everyone started demanding water, food, some sort of information, anything really. We were in there something like twenty hours in the end. Only then did the airline finally let us out, and suddenly they were all apologetic, giving us free food and so on – and then they just cancelled the flight anyway. I’m convinced they only did any of that because we all kicked up such a massive fuss together.
