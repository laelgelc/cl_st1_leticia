well for me it comes down to what sort of society you actually want to live in. There are some things that feel like they should be basic, shared foundations rather than just another thing you can buy more of if you’ve got the cash. I’m thinking particularly of good healthcare and good education. Those are kind of fundamental requirements of life, really, where everyone ought to have an equal start and a fair footing. If you don’t, then everything else is already tilted before you even begin. So it’s less about communism and more about agreeing that, at that basic level, everyone should be on the same platform.
