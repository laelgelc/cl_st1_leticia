I mean, my whole slightly mad memorisation thing does work for me, even if it sounds a bit obsessive. Some stuff you just have to learn by rote, there isn’t really a way round it, and I’d honestly rather just do endless recitation than faff about with gimmicky techniques. With A level English Lit, when we had those closed‑book exams, the teachers only wanted us to memorise a few key quotes from the set poems. But because they were poems, I couldn’t bear only knowing bits, so I decided I might as well memorise the entire poems instead and just have the whole lot in my head.
