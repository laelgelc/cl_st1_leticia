we went to Pompeii on the Sunday and we ended up getting a taxi from our hotel to the train station because we were worried about missing the train and Italian public transport is actually shocking. the driver had the meter on, and when we got there it said one price, but then he tried to charge us more, saying there was some kind of Sunday surcharge. we did challenge him on it, because it was obvious he was trying it on, and I remember thinking that if we’d had a man with us we probably would’ve argued it a lot more. in the end though, we just paid the extra two euros rather than get into a massive confrontation.
