I was watching this programme about Britain in the 1940s, like after the war when there was a proper shortage of workers, and it was really different then. People who were on benefits and classed as disabled didn’t just stay at home, they actually got assessed. There was this bloke who’d been in a wheelchair from birth, and he really wanted to work. So they’d send people like him to a doctor first, and the doctor would look at what they could actually do. Then they’d be placed into jobs that were suited to whatever abilities they still had.
