there was this woman on the train, she was in one of those electric wheelchairs, and as soon as we were in the boarding area she just reclined it completely flat. she had all her bags piled around her as well, so she ended up taking up basically the whole space. everyone getting on at Gatwick had to kind of squeeze past her and her stuff while she just lay there reading. it was really awkward, because she didn’t move or anything, just stayed stretched out, and the rest of us were trying to shuffle round her in this really cramped bit of the carriage.
