I’d rung her the week before and suddenly realised, oh, it’s bank holiday Monday, so I thought, right, I’ll see if she’s free. I rang and asked how she was fixed, and she said she was busy Monday afternoon but free in the morning, so we arranged that I’d go over and we’d go out for coffee. Then on the Sunday she rang me back and said, could we make it lunch instead of coffee, because she was on antibiotics that had to be taken an hour before a meal, and lunch would fit in better with her routine.
