Someone asked me the other day if I’d got my Garmin ready for the run, and I had to say I don’t even have one. I’m honestly fine just knowing my finish time at the end. Like, [friend] has a Garmin that tells her her pace and distance every kilometre, and it’s all very precise, but that kind of thing just doesn’t appeal to me. I don’t really care about all those detailed stats; I don’t feel like I need them to enjoy running or feel satisfied with how I’ve done.
