I’ve actually seen a banded sea krait three times in the Andaman Isles. The first time, it was literally swimming around my feet and I had no idea what it was. Later I found out it’s supposed to be the most poisonous snake in the world. I saw it again curled up while I was scuba diving, and once more when it came up to the surface for air. It’s really beautiful. According to David Attenborough, its bite can kill you in about ten seconds to a minute, because it’s got a really powerful neurotoxin to stop fast‑swimming prey quickly.
