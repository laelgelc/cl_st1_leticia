I think I’d actually be more up for having a child if it worked differently, you know? Like, imagine if parenthood was only for, say, five years. You’d look after the child, really bond with them, teach them everything you can in that time, and then they’d move on to live with someone else. You’d still have some kind of role in their life, but you wouldn’t be responsible for them forever. That kind of system would also mean we could control how many children there are. It’d help keep population growth in check, especially when we’ve only got a finite amount of resources to go round.
