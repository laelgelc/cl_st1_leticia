So the whole point of this fake language thing is to see how your brain copes with structure when you’ve got absolutely nothing to go on, no Latin roots, no Germanic hints, nothing. They’ve literally invented a full language between the three forces, with a dictionary and everything, but it’s completely fabricated, so you can’t revise for it or learn it in advance. It’s just a straight pass or fail based on how you handle the listening and reading passages they give you. And apparently it’s not about how educated you are either – loads of really highly qualified people fail it. But there was this bloke who’d only got his GCSEs, and he’d just sat at home and taught himself eleven languages, seven of them fluently, and he’s gone and passed it.
