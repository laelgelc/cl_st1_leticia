I've never sold this house, and I’ve no intention of selling it, I don’t need to. If I ever did decide to sell, I’d have to do a lot of work on it first. Over the years I’ve had bits done by proper tradesmen – the roof was done, I had double glazing put in, some of the floorboards sorted, an outside wall done, and the chimney seen to. But I’m no DIY expert, never have been, so there’s only so much I can realistically tackle myself. I’ve just kept things going as best I can, getting people in when it’s needed, rather than constantly doing it up as if I’m preparing it for the market.
