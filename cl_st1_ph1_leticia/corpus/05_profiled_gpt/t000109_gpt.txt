We had the film Beethoven’s Fifth on over New Year, I think it was. It’s the one where the big male St Bernard meets a female St Bernard and then they have puppies, and the puppies are staying in the house. Holly was sat on the sofa between us while it was on. Once she realised that the little whining puppy noises were actually coming from the telly, she became absolutely fascinated. She just fixated on the screen, properly watching it, and she didn’t look away until the film finished and the puppy sounds stopped. It was really sweet to see her like that.
