I honestly thought the whole adenoids thing was just this special gas and a little sleep. That’s exactly how they explained it to me when I was three – you know, “you’ll have a mask, breathe in this funny gas, then have a nice nap.” So in my head that was the entire operation, nothing else to it. I didn’t question it at all, I just carried that version around as fact. It wasn’t until I was about nine or ten that I realised there was more involved, that they’d actually done anything beyond knocking me out.
