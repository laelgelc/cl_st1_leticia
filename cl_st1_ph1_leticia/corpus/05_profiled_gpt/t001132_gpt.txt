Over Easter the museum got in touch with some families about doing a bit of filming for a new CBeebies programme, and I straightaway thought of you two. They were looking for families with children aged eight to eleven, so I said you were eight, because you’re nearly eight. We’ve arranged for you to be filmed in the armoury with Schloppy, the puppet character, for about half an hour. 

You’ll be talking to Schloppy on camera, taking part in some family activities they’ve organised, and you’ll be on the receiving end of some bad jokes. It’s all part of this new show they’re making. There’s a chance you might end up being in a bit of the actual TV programme when it’s on CBeebies, which is exciting. We’ll be able to see it when it’s broadcast, and we can record it as well so we can watch it back.
