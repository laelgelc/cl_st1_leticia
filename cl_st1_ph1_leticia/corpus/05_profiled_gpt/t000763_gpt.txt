I remember one of the lads at work saying to me, “You wanna slow down, they’ve put average speed cameras in,” and I just thought, oh Jesus. I suddenly realised I’d been belting through there every day since about the second of January, straight after Christmas, totally oblivious. It’s my regular route home, you get into a habit, don’t you, especially outside the school holidays when it’s quieter. I’d just been driving like I always did, not thinking at all that there were new cameras tracking me the whole way.
