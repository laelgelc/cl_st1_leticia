There was this plenary with the new chair or chief executive of English UK, and at first he was just speaking in a fairly typical, nothing-particularly-special way. Then this incredibly pompous bloke in the audience stood up and asked this ridiculously long, stupid question. It was so obviously a trap, and he was even citing research from 1985 as if it proved something devastating. And then the plenary speaker just absolutely took him apart – very polite, very measured, but completely demolished his argument. It was brilliant to watch, really good, and totally changed the feel of the session.
