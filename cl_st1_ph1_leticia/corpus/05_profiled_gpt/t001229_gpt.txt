I told him about this other friend, from when we were acting together, who’d confided all sorts of personal problems in me. We’d got really close during the rehearsals because he literally had no one else he felt he could talk to, so I ended up being that person for him. Then out of the blue his wife confronted him, asking why he was discussing their marital issues with me. He was absolutely stunned because he’d assumed she knew nothing about me at all. That confrontation completely blew everything up and, in the end, it finished the friendship between me and him.
