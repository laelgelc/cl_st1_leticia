There were technically five places I could’ve gone to for Spanish and Chinese, but it didn’t really feel like that many in the end. I ruled one out straightaway because I just didn’t want to live there at all, and there was another where I had family, but I kind of just ignored that option for some reason. So it basically came down to choosing between two. I remember sitting in my friend’s living room with prospectuses everywhere, feeling totally stuck, not knowing where I wanted to go, what I actually wanted to study, or what I should be prioritising.
