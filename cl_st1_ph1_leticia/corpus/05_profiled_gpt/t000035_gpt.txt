Sometimes it’s even just the little things, like when I’ve had a rubbish day at work and I just want to come home and stay in a bad mood for a bit. I want to stew on it, sit and be grumpy, and not have to put on a face for anyone. But when you’re living in someone else’s family home, in a house full of people, you can’t really do that. You can’t just sit and be, or be openly miserable, because you’re always kind of “on” around everyone.
