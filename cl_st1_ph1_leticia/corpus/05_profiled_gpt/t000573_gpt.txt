I reckon part of the problem is I always end up buying the cheap computers and consoles, you know, the ones that never seem to work properly. It’s like they’re made just to wind you up so you’ll cave in and buy the really expensive version. My old Xbox was like that – it just stopped loading discs. I kept threatening it, saying I’d chuck it out the window if it didn’t start working. Went on holiday, came back, it still wouldn’t load, so I actually lobbed it out of a two-storey window.
