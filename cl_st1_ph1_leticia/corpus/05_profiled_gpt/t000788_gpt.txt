right so now when you get back in lane you wanna focus on using your Q and E at the right times, yeah? Q is kind of your main damage poke, so use that when the enemy steps a bit too close or when you’re safe behind your minions. Don’t just spam it randomly, wait until you can actually hit them or secure a minion with it. E you can use either to follow up after you’ve hit them with Q or to help you finish off a low-health minion if you’re struggling to last hit.

Remember, “last hitting” is where you only hit the minions for the killing blow – that’s the only time you actually get the gold. So let your minions do most of the damage, then you step in and land the final hit. That’s how you build up enough gold for your items.

With your Ultimate – your R – that’s the big one. It freezes them and binds them to the ground, so save it for when they’re in a bad position. You wanna use it when they’ve pushed up too far or when you’ve got backup with you, so you can actually kill them while they’re stuck. Don’t waste it on someone who can just walk away after it ends. And if things look bad, just run away rather than forcing a fight.
