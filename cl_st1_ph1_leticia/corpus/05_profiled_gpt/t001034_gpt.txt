They really did just work seven days a week in Northern Ireland, and I remember thinking they clearly didn’t come over here to have a holiday. It always felt like the plan was: slog away, make as much money as humanly possible, and then trot back to Poland rich. I can see the logic, but I just can’t buy into it myself. I imagine doing that sort of thing for ten solid years, absolutely killing yourself with work, and then you just drop dead and think, well, what was the point of all that, why did I even bother?
