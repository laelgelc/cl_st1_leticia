I asked who’d thrown the letter and someone said it was her, so I picked it up and had a look. At first I thought, oh, this’ll be something saying she’s got the job and she’s leaving, you know, something like that. But when I opened it, it was actually a resignation letter. She’d written that she’d been unhappy at the company ever since the takeover, that she was handing in her notice, and that her last day would be the twenty‑sixth of June. That was it, nothing else.
