oh now honestly it was completely his fault. He’d tied this great lump of a dog to one of those silly little outdoor tables, on a windy day of all days. Anyway, a chair went over, scared the life out of him and he just bolted. Off he went, dragging the table behind him, straight through this tiny gap in the seating area. I don’t know how he managed it without taking half the café with him. Then he shot across the high street, table still rattling along, and by some miracle he didn’t hit anyone. He finally stopped when the table went smack into the Body Care shop window and smashed it.
