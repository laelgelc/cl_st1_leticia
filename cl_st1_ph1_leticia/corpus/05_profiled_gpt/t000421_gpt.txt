I just think it's amazing that so many people who went through those wars are still alive. When you read about what they experienced, it’s incredible that some of them then went on to have really happy, fulfilled lives afterwards. They managed to build something good for themselves even after all those horrific things.

But it was very different for a lot of the men in the First World War. Many of them never talked about what they’d seen, because they just couldn’t cope with it, and the people around them didn’t really understand what they were going through. No one recognised things like post-traumatic stress back then.

So instead of getting proper help, a lot of them ended up in hospitals where they weren’t really treated in the right way at all. I think that’s just very, very sad.
