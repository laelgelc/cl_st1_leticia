It’s such a tricky situation, because if you think about it from a landlord’s point of view, a lot of them just aren’t going to want to rent out a nice two-bedroom flat to someone who’s getting their housing for free. They’ll assume that person’s unemployed, that they’ve probably got various problems, and that the place is likely to get trashed. That really puts them off offering decent properties, and instead it kind of pushes things towards these rogue landlords, who are happy to let out substandard accommodation because they figure it’ll get damaged anyway.
