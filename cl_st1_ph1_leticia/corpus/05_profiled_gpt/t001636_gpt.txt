I wish I could remember the details of that documentary, but what really stuck with me was just how shocking it all was, what they’re actually allowing to go on. It sounded like they’re basically selling off bits of the NHS as if it’s just a business, and that really worries me. Mental health especially – the way they showed it, the cuts are so deep that loads of people just aren’t getting treated at all. The resources are so limited, it’s really, really bad. It’s like a whole section of people just falling through the cracks. But then, on the other hand, when you look at the benefits side of things, there are problems there as well.
