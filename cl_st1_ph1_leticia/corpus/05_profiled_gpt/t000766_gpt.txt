Yeah, you basically stick with the same group of kids all the time, like that’s your bunk, but the afternoons are different. In the mornings you’re with your bunk doing whatever’s been assigned, all together as a group. Then in the afternoon they get this free-choice period where they can pick what they want to do themselves.
