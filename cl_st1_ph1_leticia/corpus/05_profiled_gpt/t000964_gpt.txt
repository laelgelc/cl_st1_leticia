I met his former owner the other day, actually, when he was delivering a mower back to the big house opposite. I asked him straight out if he recognised the dog, and of course he did. I explained how we’d ended up with him – they’d been struggling with young children and just couldn’t really cope with having the dog as well, so she’d given him to us.

He made a comment about the dog’s eyes, said they still hadn’t improved. When the dog is tired his eyes don’t look straight at all, they sort of wander. You can see it quite clearly then, they’re not quite where they ought to be.
