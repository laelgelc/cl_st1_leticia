He was telling us about this incident he’d had, where he was demonstrating this water thing in front of a couple of blokes who were very sceptical. They were standing there watching, not really believing any of it, and then one of them said he’d have a go himself. So this chap let him try it, and after he’d done it, the chap looked him over and said, “You’ve got an allergy, haven’t you?” The fellow was a bit taken aback, but he said yes. Then the chap added, quite specifically, “It’s tree pollen you’re allergic to.” And the bloke with the allergy confirmed, “Yes, you’re right.”
