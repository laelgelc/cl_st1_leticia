When I was a teenager we had this proper routine on Sundays. We’d all meet up on this field near where I lived – me, my mates, and some lads – and at first it was all really innocent, just playing football together every week. As we got a bit older it turned into more hanging about, taking some alcohol down there, just drinking and chatting and that.

The place itself was kind of tucked away. On one side of the field there was a primary school, and on the other side another field with a fence between. There were trees around, and behind it all there was a golf course stretching out. Near the field there was a pub as well, and a bit of empty ground that was sort of like another field.

Right at the bottom of the field there was this old fence you had to climb or jump over to get onto it properly, because technically it belonged to the primary school – it was their field really. But we always used to go there anyway, just making it our spot every Sunday, playing, then later on drinking and socialising in that same area.
