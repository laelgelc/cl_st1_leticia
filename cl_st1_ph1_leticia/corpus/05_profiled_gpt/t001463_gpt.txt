I reckon those glasses were probably beyond repair, to be honest. They were more badly damaged than any pair I’ve had in nineteen years, so I don’t think there was much hope for them anyway. But it actually worked out really well for me, because they snapped while the optician was trying to fix them. He apologised profusely and then offered me a free eye test and said they’d pay for a new pair of glasses up to the value of a hundred and twenty pounds.
