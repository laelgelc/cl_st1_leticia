They make themselves inaccessible before the students even walk into the lecture theatre, and that just kills it. You can see the students switch off. They might be absolutely brilliant, but if they’re not actually reaching anyone, what’s the point? That’s when you see them all on Facebook, because nothing’s really getting through. But the thing is, the moment someone stands up and talks in a genuinely interesting way, you literally see the phones and laptops go away. They do want to listen. They don’t come to university thinking, “Oh good, three years of boredom.” They arrive expecting to be excited and engaged, and when they are, they respond straightaway.
