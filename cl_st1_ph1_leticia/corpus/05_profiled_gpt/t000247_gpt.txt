Couple of weeks ago, we were coming back from somebody’s mum’s house in a taxi, there was a few of us in the back. We pulled up outside ours and these two Somalian fellas walked past. One of them comes over to the taxi, so the driver just winds the window down a bit. Then this fella says he’s going to rob the taxi. The driver just sort of brushes it off, like, he’s got customers in the back, you know, like he wasn’t taking him that serious. It was just such a mad thing to happen right outside our house.
