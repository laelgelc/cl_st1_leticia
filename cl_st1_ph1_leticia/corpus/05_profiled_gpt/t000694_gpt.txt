there's this one roundabout I go through that’s really awkward, it’s like three roads meeting and one of them goes straight onto the motorway so it’s always totally jammed up. You’re meant to give way to the right, obviously, but the problem is the car on your right often just can’t move at all because the traffic’s backed right up, there’s literally nowhere for them to go. So you’re sitting there thinking, well, you can’t really give way if they physically can’t go anywhere. That’s when it gets a bit odd, because everyone’s waiting for everyone else and occasionally it just ends up with all of us stuck there, then suddenly everyone decides to move at exactly the same time.
