well yeah but I sort of think if you don’t get it, that’s the very reason to try and get it, you know? Like, read about it or watch things about it till it starts to make some sort of sense. But my memory’s terrible – I just don’t retain the details. I’ll come away thinking, right, I’ve finally got a handle on it, and then a few weeks later it’s all gone fuzzy again and I can’t remember names or dates or what triggered what.
