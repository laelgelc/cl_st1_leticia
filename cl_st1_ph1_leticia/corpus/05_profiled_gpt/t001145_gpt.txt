In Peru I didn’t have to pay rent during my internship, which was a massive help, and they covered my health insurance as well. But when you work it out, I was basically earning about a pound an hour. I was doing ten-hour days, five days a week, and for all of that I only got the equivalent of about two hundred pounds, paid in dollars. So even though accommodation and insurance were included, the actual pay for the hours I was putting in was really tiny.
