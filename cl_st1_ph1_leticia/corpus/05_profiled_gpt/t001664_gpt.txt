I was just thinking about this boy I’d had this on‑off crush on since about year eight. All my friends back then were really slim and athletic, and I always felt a bit less attractive next to them. Then during Trident, you know, that work experience thing in year nine or ten, I suddenly decided to crash‑diet and go swimming. I was swimming regularly and basically living on salads, and over about two and a half months I lost around two stone. When I came back to school, he suddenly really fancied me. It honestly felt like one of those teen films – him and his best mate both fighting over me.
