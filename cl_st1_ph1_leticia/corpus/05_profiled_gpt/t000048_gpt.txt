So we’re sat there and this communion service is going on, and in the end I just thought, well, I’ll take communion then. Everyone’s looking at me, nudging and saying, “Go on, you’re the religious one, you go up,” so off I went. I actually ended up being the very last person to receive communion, right at lunchtime on the Tuesday, which felt a bit funny. It was a woman serving as well, which I noticed. Then when it was all finished I just came back, sat down again, and joined in with the final prayer with everybody else.
