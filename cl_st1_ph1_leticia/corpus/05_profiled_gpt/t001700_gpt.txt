I just think you’ve always got people on each side who genuinely believe they’re justified, and they’re looking at it from their own position, their own history. Working out what’s actually just isn’t simple at all; it’s tangled up with decades, sometimes centuries, of events and consequences, and you have to try and take in that bigger, global picture to even start making sense of it. That doesn’t mean I agree with what they do – I don’t, personally – but I can’t pretend it’s all black and white. And I really don’t like the way the European, English media frame it, because it’s often skewed and doesn’t reflect that complexity fairly.
