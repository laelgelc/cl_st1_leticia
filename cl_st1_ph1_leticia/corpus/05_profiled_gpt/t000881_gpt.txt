I stand next to this expert ringer, which is a real help. She can actually stop and point out exactly where I am in the music, and tell me when I should be using the flats. If I lose my place, she helps me get back on track. She more or less knows when I’m going to go wrong and can anticipate it, which is quite something. I describe her as bossy, but she’s very good with it, really. It makes the whole business of ringing much more manageable for me.
