I actually tried a different place first, before I took it to the proper shop. I went to that little jeweller next to the YMCA, you know, by Greggs. I only chose it because the sign mentioned watchsmiths, so I thought, fair enough, they should know what they’re doing. But when I handed the watch over and watched him looking at it, I just had this feeling he didn’t really know what he was doing with it. Then he said it would only be twenty pounds, which struck me as far too low for what needed doing. So I just said I’d take it somewhere else instead.
