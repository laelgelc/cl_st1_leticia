So when I went to see this new physiotherapist, she actually said my stiffness is quite acute, which was oddly reassuring. I’m so used to feeling like I’m making a fuss when I complain, like people think I’m exaggerating, so having her confirm it felt like I wasn’t just imagining things. She’s given me exercises, but I’ve got to be really careful with them because if I push it wrong I can set my back off, and then that’s me done for days. 

What she reckons is that if we get the right exercise plan going, we might be able to stop the knee pain altogether. My kneecaps have shifted slightly outwards and there’s a muscle imbalance, so things aren’t supported properly. But she thinks if we correct that, we can reduce the stress on the joint, especially as the arthritis is only mild.
