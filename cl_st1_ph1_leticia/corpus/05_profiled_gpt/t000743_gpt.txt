I went to this café on Saturday with my friend, after we’d been looking at that bras and pants exhibition, and I had this gluten‑free cake. Almost straightaway I started to feel really ill – my heart was racing and I just felt horrible. It didn’t really settle until I’d eaten some proper, normal food, and it’s made me think that sugar really affects me badly now. 

It’s such a contrast with the other weekend when I was feeling sad and missing you lot. Normally I’d have stuffed myself with sugary rubbish, but instead I decided to be good. I had treats like dates and yogurt, then I slept and woke up feeling brilliant. I got up early, did loads of work, went to the gym and for a run. So I’m convinced now: I really can’t have sugar anymore, it just makes me ill.
