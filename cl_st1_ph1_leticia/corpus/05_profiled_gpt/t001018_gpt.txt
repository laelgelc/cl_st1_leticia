I sprang awake with this horrible burning sensation and, for a second, I couldn’t work out what was going on. Then I realised it was my hair that was actually on fire. Instead of anyone helping, they were all just laughing at me, like it was some big joke, which made it even more frightening. I completely panicked and bolted out into the street, trying desperately to put the flames out as I went, not really thinking straight at all. I just kept running, heading towards home, half trying to beat the fire and half trying to get somewhere safe. In absolute hysterics, I rang my mum, convinced that someone had deliberately set me alight, and she just came back at me, totally baffled, swearing and not understanding what on earth I was talking about.
