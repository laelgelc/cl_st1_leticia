the biggest thing that really worries me about having kids is that whole moment when they’re four and you’re basically forced to hand them over to the state school system. from then on it feels like the government’s got them, you know? they control what they learn, where they are all day, who they’re with, right up until they’re eighteen. it’s like your own child is being shaped more by that system than by you, and I hate the idea of that getting in between us and damaging the relationship I’d have with them. I know there are alternatives, like private schools or teaching them myself, but realistically we just couldn’t afford that, so it doesn’t feel like a real option.
