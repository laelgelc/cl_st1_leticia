when you put individual words together into real speech it suddenly becomes much harder to understand, even if each word on its own is quite simple. what I do is use an activity based on a sentence that was actually misheard in a meeting. I put up what someone thought they’d heard, then I show a series of different possible “misheard” versions in sequence and get them to guess what the original sentence really was. they see how plausible the wrong versions can be, and only at the end do I reveal the correct final version that was actually said.
