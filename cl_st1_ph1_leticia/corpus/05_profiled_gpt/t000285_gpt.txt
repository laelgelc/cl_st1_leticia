I was thinking about how these diets can go too far. I’ve got a friend down in Suffolk who cut out pretty much all the carbohydrates, and he’s gone really thin with it. Reminds me of that programme on the telly, You Are What You Eat, where there was that woman who did the same sort of thing, no carbs at all, and she ended up absolutely exhausted and sleepy all the time. It just shows you, you’ve got to have a bit of balance with what you eat, you can’t give up everything. Mind you, I was knocking back a fair bit of alcohol last night, which probably hasn’t helped my situation either.
