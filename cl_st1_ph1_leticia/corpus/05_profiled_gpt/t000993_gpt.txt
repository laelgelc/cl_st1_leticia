I don't actually think people are going to live as long as they're predicting, but the problem is what it's going to look like in about fifteen years’ time. By then, most of the people in the country will be old rather than young. That means your lot, the younger generation, are going to end up paying twice over really – paying into your own pensions and also paying for ours. You’ll have a relatively small number of young people trying to support this massive ageing population.
