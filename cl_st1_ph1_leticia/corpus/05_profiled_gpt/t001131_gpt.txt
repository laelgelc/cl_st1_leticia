I think some of those different levels in the system really need to be taken out. When I was working for the NHS for years, especially when I was at the hospice, I was forever being dragged into these strategic health authority meetings. You had to justify everything you were doing using this traffic light system. So if you’d got things in place with your trust, you’d either be on a red light, an amber light or a green light. It was all about service improvement and proving what you were doing.
