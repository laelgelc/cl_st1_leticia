From about four years old, a child has to go into the state school system, and that really troubles me. I don’t agree with the idea that from that age the state effectively takes control of what the child learns and who they spend their time with, day in, day out, right up until they’re sixteen or eighteen. It feels like handing over an enormous part of their life and development to a system I don’t really believe in. That’s not the kind of environment I’d want for my child. I’d want something where we have much more say in what they’re exposed to, how they learn, and the sort of people they’re surrounded by, rather than just accepting the standard route.
