Well, something funny happened on Sunday, first time I’ve ever been left on my own as warden. I had to get there early and do the lot – take all the covers off, light the candles, sort the microphones out, put the Bible in place, all of that. I was a bit on edge about it, to be honest, because I’d never done the whole thing by myself before. The bit that really worried me was turning the alarm off. So I got there nice and early, stood in front of the church door thinking, right, got to be brave now. I kept checking and rechecking the alarm code in my head, then used my big key to open the door, checked the number again, just to be sure.
