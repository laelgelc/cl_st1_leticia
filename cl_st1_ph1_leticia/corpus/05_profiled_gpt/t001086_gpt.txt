I still really like listening to albums all the way through in the order they're meant to be. You can tell artists actually think about which tracks go where, so the moods kind of rise and fall properly. It feels like a complete thing then, rather than just a bunch of separate songs. When you just shuffle individual tracks you lose that sense of it being a unified whole. And with records, because it’s such a faff to skip a track, you just let it play straight through, which really encourages that way of listening.
