I did actually get a bit of help building the statue on the Minecraft server. It ended up being quite a complex three‑dimensional design, because I was trying to merge different views of Super Mario. I took the pixel art of Mario from the side and from the front and combined them, so it wasn’t just a flat image. Then I altered Mario into this gnome‑like Statue of Liberty figure, giving him a pointy beard, a pointy hat, a torch in one hand and a sort of tablet like the Declaration of Independence in the other.
