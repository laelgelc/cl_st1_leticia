I was in the car and Ed Sheeran came on in the Live Lounge, and I got really excited and thought, “Oh my God, I wonder if he’s touring again.” So I went online to check for tickets and everything was sold out. Then I started thinking about how much he absolutely loves Ben Howard, so I thought I’d have a look for Ben Howard gigs instead. I searched around online and pretty much everything for Ben Howard was sold out as well. Then I spotted that he was actually playing at this festival called The Citadel, which I’d never really heard of before.
