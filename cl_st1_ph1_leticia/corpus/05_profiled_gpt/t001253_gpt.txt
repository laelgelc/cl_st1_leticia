I did actually find some of his ideas really interesting, and I’m thinking I might try to bring a couple of them into the summer school. One of the main things he talked about was having certain elements that are always the same in every lesson. So, for example, every teacher would write a list of ten vocabulary words in the same place on the board. And then you’d always have three lesson aims written at the top of the board at the beginning. He also suggested using the same colours for the same things, to keep everything uniform.
