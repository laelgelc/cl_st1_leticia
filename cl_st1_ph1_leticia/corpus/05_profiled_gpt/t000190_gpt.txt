I think with a lot of stuff that gets published there’s a bit of a trade-off between how well it’s written and what it’s actually saying. If the content’s strong enough, or really controversial, it’ll still get through even if the writing’s pretty shoddy. You can see that loads in the tabloids – they’ll print anything if it’s got a big, punchy angle, even when the sentences are all over the place.

Now that I read a lot more, and a much wider range of things, I notice it more. I can tell when something’s badly written or just oddly put together. And I do feel like all that reading has actually made my own writing better, even if I’m still not that confident about it.
