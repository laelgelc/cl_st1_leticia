So I ended up going home early, and then I had to walk about five miles into town to get to the police station and ask about my missing bag. I went in and spoke to this policewoman at the desk, and while I was explaining, you could hear all this laughter going on in the office behind her. Then she looks at me and goes, basically, “Oh, we know who you are, you’re the ones who were skinny-dipping on the beach and got all your clothes stolen.” So clearly the whole thing had done the rounds already, and the story had properly spread everywhere, way beyond just the people who were actually there.
