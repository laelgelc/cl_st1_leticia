It was all made more complicated because I’d been having assisted conception. They did this procedure where they inject a coloured fluid into your womb and then track it on an ultrasound to see if there are any blockages in your fallopian tubes. The idea was to check whether that was why I wasn’t conceiving. Later on, they found cancer cells up my fallopian tubes, in one of my ovaries, and around the outside of my uterus. Looking back, I now think that flushing process may well have pushed or spread those cells out into my abdominal cavity.
