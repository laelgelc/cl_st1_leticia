I've realised I just haven't been able to commit to anything in July or August, even when quite nice things have come up. I keep getting these offers, like house-sitting somewhere else, and on paper they sound lovely, but I have this really strong sense that by then I’ll want to be by the sea. It’s like this pull, and I can really feel it, so every time something away from the coast is suggested I end up saying no. I just can't bring myself to tie those months down.
