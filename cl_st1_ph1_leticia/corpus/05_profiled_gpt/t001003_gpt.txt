I think, for me, a parent should be properly educated so they can teach their child all the important simple things in life. They should know how to cook quite well, be able to manage money very well, and be social enough to help the child navigate the world. And then there are loads of other attributes on top of that. The thing is, most people only really develop a lot of these qualities by actually having children, so they tend to get better from their first child to their second.
