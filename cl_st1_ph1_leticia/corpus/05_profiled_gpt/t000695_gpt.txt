we were in this really narrow cycle lane and the woman in front of me suddenly slammed her brakes on because her bike lock had fallen off. There were parked cars down one side and traffic on the other, so there was literally nowhere for me to go – I couldn’t swerve out, and I just didn’t have time to stop. I went straight into the back of her bike and the impact snapped off part of my front mudguard on my old bike, which really annoyed me. I felt it was a bit daft of her to just stop dead like that in such a tight space. She did turn round and say sorry, and she picked up the bit of my mudguard and handed it back to me, but that was it really – just a brief awkward exchange and then we both carried on.
