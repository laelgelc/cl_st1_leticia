We were driving through that old area, you know, where some of it’s been done up and some of it’s still being knocked about, houses rebuilt here and there. It’s funny, because it’s near where your grandma grew up, so bits of it looked familiar but also a bit strange now. Anyway, I turned into what I thought was the right street, only to realise it wasn’t. I’d gone the wrong way down a one-way, hadn’t I? A lorry coming towards us flashed its lights, and that’s when it clicked I’d turned into the wrong street altogether.
