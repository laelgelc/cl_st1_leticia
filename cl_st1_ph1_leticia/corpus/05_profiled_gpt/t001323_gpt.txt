right so it’s a word game, you split into teams and you’ve got twenty‑five noun cards laid out in a five by five grid. Some of those cards belong to the red team, some to the blue team, some are just neutral, and there’s at least one that can end the game straight away if someone picks it. One person on each team is the clue‑giver. They can see which words belong to their team on a separate grid, and their job is to come up with a single word that links several of their team’s words at once. So if my team’s words were “tennis”, “horse” and “bath”, I’d try to think of one clue that connects them, and my teammates then have to guess those target words from the grid.
