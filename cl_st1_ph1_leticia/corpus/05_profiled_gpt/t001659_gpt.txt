I remember sitting in A&E years ago, after I’d had a miscarriage, and overhearing this young doctor talking about women coming in “bleeding all over the place” and how, when you actually looked, it was often just a tiny bit. And I did think, well, that’s true in a way – even a very small amount of blood can look like an awful lot when it’s spread out. But that really stuck with me. So later, when I was having all this bleeding, it made me wonder if I was just overreacting, if actually it was within normal limits.
