I showed her this handwritten letter from that elderly scientist, you know, the one who keeps writing to Dad. In it he was going on about how he still wants to measure him, and especially his children, listing all these different things he wants to know about us. I asked her if she’d actually done any of these measurements he kept banging on about. She said she had, over the years, done some of them. And then she suddenly came out with “droopy eyelids” as one of the things she’d measured, which really stuck with me.
