I always say I’m a pretty bad cook, really, but for a while I felt like some kind of master chef. A mate of mine had just come over from South Africa, and over there everything had always been done for him, so he’d never actually had to cook anything himself. When he arrived here he was completely clueless in the kitchen. I started showing him really basic things – how to do simple stuff with a pan, a few ingredients, that sort of thing – and he was absolutely amazed by it. To me it was nothing special at all, but to him it was like I was performing culinary miracles.
