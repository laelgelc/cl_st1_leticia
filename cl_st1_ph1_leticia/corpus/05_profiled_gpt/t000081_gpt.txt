Just before I left, I actually found out what had been going on with that maternity-leave teacher. She’d put these absolutely appalling comments about the young girl who was covering her on Facebook, out there in the public domain for everyone to see. I went straight to the head and said this was completely unacceptable, that she shouldn’t just be allowed to stroll back in as if nothing had happened. The head said they’d have a word with her, but in reality nothing meaningful was done. In a business environment, that sort of behaviour would lead to proper disciplinary action, quite possibly dismissal.
