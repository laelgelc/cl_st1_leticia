we'd got off the plane and because it was this really poor, unfamiliar place we honestly had no clue how to get to where we were staying. we just sort of milled around the airport for about an hour trying to work it out, then finally managed to get a train, and after that we had to get a taxi because it was way too far to walk. when we came out into the city it was just mental – chaotic, smelly, felt properly dangerous, nothing like england. everything here’s so ordered and purposeful, and there it was just this mad, busy thai city that felt completely different.
