I just don't have other adults around me, really, and that’s a big part of why I feel the way I do. It might sound completely crazy, but I honestly think everyone should be sterilised by default, and then you’d have to apply and meet certain criteria before being allowed to have children. 

Look at how we treat other responsibilities: you have to go through all sorts of tests and checks to drive a car, or to adopt a child. There’s this whole formal process to prove you’re capable and safe. Yet with having your own child, there’s nothing like that at all. No evaluation, no requirements. 

To me that’s absurd, and it only exists because having a child is just so physically easy.
