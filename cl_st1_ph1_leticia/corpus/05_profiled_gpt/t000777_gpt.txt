It went on for hours, that general assembly, and I only managed to sit through a bit of it before I escaped. One of my colleagues had decided he was going to make a great stand about the organisation taking money from the tobacco companies – four, four and a half million or something of that order. He was planning this big protest, denouncing it all because of smoking and health and so on. I couldn’t quite face it, so before I left I scribbled a couple of extremely sarcastic little messages for them to read out in my absence, thanking him profusely for pointing out that smoking is unhealthy, as if none of us had ever heard that before.
