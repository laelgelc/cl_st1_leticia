I think it really comes down to people’s different needs and personalities, doesn’t it? Some people absolutely want to be right by an airport or a main station, and they’ll deliberately place themselves there because they really value that easy access and convenience. For others, though, it just isn’t such a big priority. Even though transport links do have an impact on where you might live, some people are quite happy not to organise their whole lives around them, especially if they’re not travelling all the time.
