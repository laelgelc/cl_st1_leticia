They’d all decided to go up the ski lift, even though they didn’t really know how to get on or off it properly. When they tried to get off halfway up, they just fell in a heap, totally ungraceful. Then they looked down and realised the slope was actually really quite steep and scary from there. At first, the woman set off brilliantly and everyone thought she was doing amazingly well. But the truth was she didn’t actually know how to ski properly, so after a bit she completely lost control and ended up falling, which rather proved the point.
