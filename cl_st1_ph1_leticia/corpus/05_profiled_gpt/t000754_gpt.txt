I felt really naughty, honestly. There was this absolutely massive queue for the Metro after the Great North Run, like thousands of people, and it was barely moving. I just looked at it and thought, I am not doing this. So instead of joining the back, I wandered right up to the front near the station entrance, spotted a Caffè Nero and ducked inside like I was just popping in for food. I bought a sandwich to make it look legit, then came out and just slipped straight into the queue from there. I kept thinking, this is so cheeky, but I’m not standing in that bollocks for hours. No one said anything, no one challenged me at all, which made me feel even naughtier.
