So I carried on and then I saw these women with horses, so I stopped and asked them for directions. I still thought it was a car crash at that point, just because of all the smoke and the roads being shut. They told me it wasn’t a car crash at all, it was a military jet that had gone down, and that’s why the whole area was blocked off everywhere. One of the women then gave me really clear directions, told me exactly how to get through to the road that would lead me back onto the bypass.
