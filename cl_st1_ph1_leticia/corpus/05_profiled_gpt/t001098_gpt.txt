I just think there’s a really big difference between what older people and younger people actually need when they’re looking for work. If you’re over fifty, you’ve probably got loads of experience already, but the problem is more that you’re older, and you haven’t had to look for a job in years. Everything’s changed since they first started working – how you write a CV, how you apply, even where you go to find vacancies. So for them, proper help with CVs, applications and where to look would make more sense. But for younger people who’ve never had a job at all, what they’re really missing is experience. So I think work placements are much more useful for them than just sitting in some generic job-hunting session.
