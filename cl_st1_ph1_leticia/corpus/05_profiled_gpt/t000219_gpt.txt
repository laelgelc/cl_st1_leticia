Yeah, what struck me about the new album is how Christian it sounds, especially given Cohen’s Jewish. There are loads of biblical references in there, and quite a few of them feel very New Testament-y in tone. It makes the whole thing feel oddly churchy in places. He’s such an interesting bloke, though – you still get this sense that he’s a bit depressed, or at least pretty weighed down. There’s that line about asking an ex-lover who hates him to “hate me less,” which is just so bleak and raw.
