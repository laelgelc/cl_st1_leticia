I was literally stood in this auction, going through somebody’s dad’s belongings, when my phone kept going off with these massive long texts from him. He was getting really emotional, saying things like the Pope was lucky because he had someone who truly loved him at his deathbed, and asking who would be there for him when he dies. Mixed in with all that, he was calling me naïve and having a go at my faith and my religion and everything I believe in.
