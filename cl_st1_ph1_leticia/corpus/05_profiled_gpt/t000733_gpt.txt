In my job it really winds me up how clients just ask for random stuff purely because they can. They’ll throw out these totally arbitrary requests, no real thought behind them, just to get something moving and keep people busy. It’s like they’re using the fact they’ve got the power and the budget to make someone jump, rather than actually knowing what they want. Then we’re left scrambling around trying to make sense of it. I just wish they’d sit down for five minutes, make an actual decision, and then come to us, instead of treating it like a game.
