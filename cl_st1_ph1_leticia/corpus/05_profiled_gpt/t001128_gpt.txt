So like that woman the other day – she’d been in before, hadn’t ordered anything, then came back saying she wanted to order this wallpaper. I sat there writing all the details out for her, she’s copying them down as I’m doing it, and then she goes, “I’ll phone the order through.” Never did. She’d borrowed the book, we’d spent a couple of hours with her, helping her find exactly what she wanted. Later on I looked online and saw the same wallpaper was about twenty‑five percent cheaper than even the twenty percent discount we’d offered her. So you just know she’s gone off and ordered it there instead, just to save a few extra quid, and all that time we spent helping her meant nothing to her at all.
