The whole mess really came from this state-of-the-art new building they put up at one of the satellite centres. It was supposed to be marvellous, all singing all dancing, but it went massively over budget. I’m sure it must have ended up costing at least twice what they’d originally planned, and of course it didn’t stop at the building itself – there was all the furnishing and the equipment and everything needed to get it up and running. In the end the college was ten million in debt, which meant Ofsted had to come in straightaway.
