It was really weird, you know. This was a girls’ school, so you’d expect the gents’ to be a tiny little afterthought, couple of cubicles and that. Instead, you’d walk in and there were urinals as far as the eye could see. You could’ve fitted three classrooms in there, I’m not joking. Row after row of the things, like they were expecting coachloads of blokes to turn up every break time. 

I still can’t get my head round it. In a place where there are hardly any men, they’d built this enormous gents’. It just didn’t make any sense to me at all. You look at it and you think, who on earth planned this, and what were they expecting? I’ve never really worked out the logic of it.
