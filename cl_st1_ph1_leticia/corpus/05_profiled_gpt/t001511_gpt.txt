I think that criticism of Andy Burnham, that he didn’t really stand for anything during the leadership campaign, was pretty unfair. In the leadership debate I thought he actually cut through a lot of the usual political rhetoric and just answered the questions directly, which felt really refreshing compared with the others. It seemed like people were oddly against him, or at least not giving him a fair hearing, but I really liked him then and I still do now. I honestly think he’s a good politician.
