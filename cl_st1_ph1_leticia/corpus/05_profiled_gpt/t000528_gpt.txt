About eight or nine years before, she actually drove past the turning to where my mum lived and she knew that her half-sister was there. She mentioned it when she was going to a university interview, so she was clearly aware of it, but she still chose not to stop. They’d never met and everything around it was a bit strained, so I can understand why she didn’t, but it does feel like such a shame. My granny absolutely loved talking about her, so it would have meant a lot if they’d met.
