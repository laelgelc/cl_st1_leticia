We went over there one Monday evening for one of his tennis coaching courses and honestly, we were both really impressed. The whole place was floodlit, and there were games going on everywhere – rugby on one pitch, tennis courts full, hockey over one side, football on another. He was absolutely blown away by it all, just seeing that level of sport happening all at once. I said to him, you know, you *can* go to university, this is what it’s like. And he just wished he could go and do that all the time, just play sport, without having to study.
