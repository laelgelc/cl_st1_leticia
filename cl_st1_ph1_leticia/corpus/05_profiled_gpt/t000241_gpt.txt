What I’d really like us to work towards is having a business that’s set up so we’re not tied to any one place. I’m imagining creating a remote team, where everyone works from wherever they happen to be, and we do the same. That way, if we want to go and spend six months with family, or decide we’d like to base ourselves in a different country for a while, we can just go and do that without having to stop the work.

The idea is that the structure of the business, and the way the team works, is all designed around that flexibility from the start. So we’re not building something that needs an office or us physically being there all the time. Instead, it’s more like we’ve got a group of people we trust, all working remotely, and we can move around and live where we want while the business keeps running in the background.
