My relation, he’s a retired fireman, he was married to our kid, my late sister, and he’s married again now. Anyway, he bought one of those made‑to‑measure narrow boats, had it done up here in Liverpool. I went with them for a week on the canals, stayed on the boat with them. It was really quiet, you know, very soothing, peaceful, just pleasant all round. People stand on the banks waving as you go past, dead friendly like. And the ducks, they swim along in front of the boat, leading the way. I really enjoyed it, a lovely, calm week that was.
