Do you find it’s the same with television drama? I notice it particularly if I watch something like Morse or Lewis or Frost again. The first time round you’re caught up in the story, wanting to know what happens next. But when you already know how it all turns out, you can almost set the plot aside and just watch the people. I find myself paying attention to the acting, how the characters are played and how they interact with each other. It becomes quite fascinating, really, just observing those performances and the way they relate, because the suspense of the narrative isn’t there anymore.
