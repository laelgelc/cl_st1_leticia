It’s funny, isn’t it, how with family history there are these great big gaps, and you end up having to imagine what might have happened because nothing was ever properly written down. My uncle once asked how my great-grandmother actually met the man she went off with, and we simply don’t know. Perhaps my great-grandfather even introduced them, or they met when he was there on training. There’s this story about my great-grandfather chasing them through a station, stopping them leaving, but no one knows if it’s true or where it came from.
