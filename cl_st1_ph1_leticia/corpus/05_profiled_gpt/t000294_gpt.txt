The train was absolutely heaving, really busy, and then when we got to the shops it was just as bad. We tried to get into Café Rouge, but they said all the tables that could take a pram were reserved because of half term. The way the girl said it, though, made it sound like there were no tables at all, so a couple of other people started to walk out thinking they couldn’t be seated. The owner came rushing after them, saying there were plenty of tables, just not ones that would fit a pram. He was really keen to bring them back in and said he’d have carried the pram upstairs if there’d been a lift.
