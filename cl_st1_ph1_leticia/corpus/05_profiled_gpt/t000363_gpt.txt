From the first time I met him I was quite alarmed by how extremely he was dieting, and how much he seemed to enjoy getting thinner. The way he talked about it reminded me so much of people with eating disorders that I actually said that to him. I told him it sounded like he might be verging on having a problem. At the time I just assumed he wouldn’t really take my comments in, but later I realised he really had taken it to heart. He said he knew it sounded wrong and he was trying. He also talked about his kids and the idea of us meeting up with them, but I’m honestly relieved I’m not ready to meet his children yet.
