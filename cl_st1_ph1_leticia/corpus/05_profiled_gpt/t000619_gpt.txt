I saw him at work, in the office opposite, and he told me he was buying her out of the house. He said she was still living there, even after a year, and that she could leave any time. According to him, the arrangement was that he’d sort the money, but she was saying something quite different. From her side, she said he was being difficult about actually releasing the money until she’d found somewhere to buy. He apparently wanted it all done in a way where the money went straight into the purchase of another house, rather than going directly into her bank account. So I just got these two slightly different versions from each of them about what was happening with the house and the money.
