he was really upset and he just wouldn’t let me go on my own, he said he didn’t want me driving back on my own in the dark, so in the end he came with us. we went over to the village hall together, just to show our faces really. we literally just went in, found them, said “have a lovely day” and all that, and I explained about my dad, that he wasn’t there because he was ill, so they knew why he hadn’t come.

we didn’t hang about, though. a couple of people there said to me, “look, just go home, don’t worry about it, don’t stay, you’re not going to eat anything, don’t be daft paying for food you’re not going to touch,” that sort of thing. so we took their advice and left.

on the way back I stopped off and picked up a Chinese for the boys so they’d at least have something nice to eat, but I didn’t have anything myself. I just couldn’t face eating, so I made sure they were sorted and left it at that.
