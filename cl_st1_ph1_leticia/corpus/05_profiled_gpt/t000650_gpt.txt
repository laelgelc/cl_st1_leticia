and the thing with creepers is they don’t burn in the sunlight like the other monsters, and they’ll just keep following you forever if you let them. So someone decided to build this sanctuary tower next to the dome, so if a creeper was chasing you, you could just leg it up there and be safe. Inside the dome people really went to town on decorating it, putting lights all over the place so you could actually see what was going on. They added these little lit rest areas and caves around the edges where you could duck in and recover your health. By the end of the server’s life there were just holes everywhere around that arena, which really showed how much people had been using it.
