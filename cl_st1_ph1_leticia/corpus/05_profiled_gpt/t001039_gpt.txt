I used to absolutely love Lidl when we were in Northern Ireland, you know. It was brilliant there – all the new stuff in the middle was such good quality, and they had loads of organic veg and things like that. I’d actually look forward to going in and seeing what they had. 

Here though, I just don’t bother so much. The Lidl here, the quality’s just not the same at all. Most of the clothes and bits seem to be polyester, and I hate that – you get all static, little electric shocks and everything, it’s horrible. So I hardly ever buy anything from that middle section now, even if it looks like a bargain.

Biedronka, on the other hand, I think of as the Polish Lidl, and it’s brilliant. Their stuff’s really good quality, and loads of it is actually made in Poland, so I really prefer going there.
