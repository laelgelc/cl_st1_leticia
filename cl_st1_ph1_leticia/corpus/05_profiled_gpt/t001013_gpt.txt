We won that goldfish at a fair, you know, one of those where they hand it over in a little plastic bag with water. We brought it home and put it in a bowl on the sideboard, and it lived there for a while. When we moved, we decided we didn’t really want to take the goldfish with us, so we gave it to my uncle, who had a proper garden fish pond. The cat used to act like it wasn’t interested at all, then when no one was looking it would have a go at trying to catch the fish, but it never managed it. In the end the pond was the best place for it; keeping a goldfish in a little round bowl is really cruel.
