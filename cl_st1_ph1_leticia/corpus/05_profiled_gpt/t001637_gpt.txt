my grandad was really, really anti‑Thatcher, properly old‑school socialist and very principled about it all. There was this time when he was ill and needed an operation, and he absolutely could’ve afforded to go private. But he just refused point blank, because he didn’t want to jump ahead of other people who’d been waiting longer on the NHS list. That was his whole thing – why should he push in front just because he had the money? And the funny thing is, if it had been for someone else, he’d have paid for private treatment without a second thought.
