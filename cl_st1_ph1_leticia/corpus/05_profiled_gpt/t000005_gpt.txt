They also arranged for me to have a biopsy at the dental hospital, for something they’d found. If I’d had to go through an ordinary dentist, it would have cost an absolute fortune, so I was very grateful it was done that way. The result, fortunately, was fine, nothing sinister at all, but the whole business left me in a bit of a state. One cheek swelled up so much after the biopsy that my face was completely uneven. At the time I was already completely bald, so I felt terribly conspicuous. A bald head is one thing, you can just about face the high street like that, but bald with a lopsided, swollen face as well felt like one step too far, and I really didn’t feel I could go out looking like that.
