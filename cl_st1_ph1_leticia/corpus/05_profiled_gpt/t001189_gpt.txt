I mean, he’s got really good stamina, so once he’d picked himself up he did start to gradually overtake a few of them, especially on the hilly bits of the course, but he was still very much near the back. I’d gone and stood down by the finishing stretch, waiting for him to come through, and I’m watching these lads come in. Two boys suddenly sprinted past, they must’ve been about tenth or fifteenth, going hell for leather. Then there’s this other boy behind them, still running but actually being sick as he ran. I just found it completely baffling, because he clearly wasn’t going to win.
