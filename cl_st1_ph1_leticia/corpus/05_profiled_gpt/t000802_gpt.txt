Yes, there are loads of unemployed people in Britain, but that doesn’t automatically mean they’re suitable. We advertised for a receptionist in our building recently, and I went through the CVs for the admin role. You could often tell from the covering emails alone that some of them were basically illiterate. And my administrator absolutely has to be able to write properly. I mean, really basic things – like using a capital “I” when you’re referring to yourself. If they can’t manage that in an application, it’s not a great sign.
