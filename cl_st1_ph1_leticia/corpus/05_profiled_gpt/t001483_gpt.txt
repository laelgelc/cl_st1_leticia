Loads of people have golden retrievers in houses way smaller than ours, though. Nan and grandad’s dog is massive and they manage fine, so I don’t really think space would be the issue. If you really want a Sussex spaniel, I’m not against it at all, I’d just worry about how boisterous it might be. I’d hate it if it was too rough when playing with an older, smaller dog, especially once a golden retriever got bigger and maybe made the older one a bit grumpy.
