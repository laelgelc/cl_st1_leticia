She’d actually been to a couple of the earlier lectures in the day, which I missed completely because I was off playing hockey. One of the talks she told me about was by this woman called Helen – I can’t remember her surname at all – who’d done a show at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival about space. Apparently it was absolutely brilliant. She was incredibly enthusiastic and went through the whole history of NASA, how it all got going. And the really striking bit was her explaining that rocket technology actually started off with the rockets the Nazis developed, the rocket missiles, and that was where everything began.
