My daughter used to work in this pub, you see, and they told her her hours were seven till eleven. But it never really worked out like that. She had to stay on after eleven, clearing up all the mess after the customers, and the bar itself didn’t shut when it was supposed to, it just carried on until it had to close. It was unfair really, the way they did it, saying one thing about the hours and then keeping her late. But she liked the job, so she stuck with it.
