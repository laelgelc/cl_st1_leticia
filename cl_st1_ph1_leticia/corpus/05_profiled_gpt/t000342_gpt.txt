I had a really similar thing when I was working in a mainstream school with a low‑ability group. We had two girls, one from Thailand and one from Poland, and neither of them had much English at all. At first I was really worried about how on earth I was going to manage, because they couldn’t really follow what was going on and I wasn’t sure how to support them properly. But they ended up sitting together, and it was lovely watching them start trying to communicate and help each other, despite the language barrier. I thought that was absolutely brilliant.
