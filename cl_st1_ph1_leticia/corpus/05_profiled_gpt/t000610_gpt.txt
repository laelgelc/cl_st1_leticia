I suppose what I’m trying to say, even if I’m not putting it brilliantly, is that when we try to blend too many different objectives into one thing – like a holiday that’s meant to tick every box at once: proper break, quality time together, fun activities, maybe even ticking off some “shoulds” – it actually creates pressure. Instead of feeling relaxed, it can feel like this big, overloaded project that has to satisfy everything. It might be easier if we allowed ourselves to separate things out a bit: have some time that’s just for rest, some that’s just for doing things together, some for particular activities. Trying to cram it all into one package can just get overwhelming.
