I actually asked her outright one day, very carefully, if we’d somehow upset them, because we’d never had any acknowledgement for anything we’d sent. I said it as gently as I could, but it had really been bothering me. There was this horrible silence afterwards, and for a moment I thought, oh dear, I’ve really put my foot in it now. Then it all came out that they were absolutely horrified. Each of them had assumed the other one had written or said thank you, so nothing had ever been done. Their lives are so chaotic and disorganised that it just slipped through the net, and that’s how the misunderstanding happened.
