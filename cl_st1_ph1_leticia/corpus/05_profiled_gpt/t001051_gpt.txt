I think the reason we end up using medication, even if we say the core issue is nurture, is because the symptoms can get so bad that you just can’t function day to day. When you’re in that state, even basic things – getting out of bed, going to work, not snapping at people – become incredibly hard. So medication’s like a short-term bridge, just to get you to a place where you can cope enough to start actually working on getting better. But I don’t think you really get better from pills alone. That comes from improving your environment, actually wanting to change, and being around supportive people.
