I do really like watching MasterChef, it’s just nice, but I’m not keen on what they’ve done with the new series. I enjoy the cooking because it feels exciting and you don’t quite know what people are going to come up with, that sort of unpredictable element is fun. But now they’ve changed the format so that straight after the first “invention” test they send them off to work in some really high-end restaurant. That just feels like far too big a leap. One minute they’re doing something quite basic, like you could imagine them making beans on toast at home, and then suddenly they’re expected to turn out these incredibly complex dishes, like foie gras and all that. It’s that jump, from very simple food to very fancy restaurant cooking, that I find a bit ridiculous in the new version.
