It really made me think of that slogan on Facebook, “stop the glorification of busy.” I remember how much it resonated with me because I used to feel genuinely guilty about taking a lunch break. I was working on this small industrial estate, and there was a little business centre nearby. I’d go over there and have coffee with this bloke who worked in one of the small businesses, and we both had this warped idea that the really dedicated, hard-working people just didn’t stop for lunch. We honestly believed that pushing through without a break somehow proved our commitment.
