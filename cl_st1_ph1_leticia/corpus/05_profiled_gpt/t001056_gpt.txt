My drumming teacher plays there quite regularly now, you know – he’s got this monthly gig up at the Olympic Park. It’s funny, you think of it as just being for the Games, but it’s still very much a proper place people go. They’ve kept it going with all sorts of sports happening, and there are restaurants and pubs and different events on, so it’s actually a really lovely place to spend time, not just a big empty stadium.

Taking you two out to restaurants there or anywhere else is definitely easier now you’re older, but it can still be a bit stressful once you start getting bored. It reminds me of when I was little, having those big roast dinners at Granny’s house. I really didn’t like it because we had to sit there for ages at the table, and it just felt endless. Children generally don’t want to sit still in restaurants, do they? It’s much the same thing: grown-ups enjoying the meal and the chat, and kids just wanting to get up and move about.
