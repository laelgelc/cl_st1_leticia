So originally there were five of us in the group, including me. There was this Irish lad, a sound guy, a 3D games bloke and a graphics guy as well. On paper it looked like a really good mix, you know, everything covered. But the sound guy dropped out pretty much straightaway – I never even met him, he was just gone. The 3D games guy turned out to be really flaky, hardly ever there, not very reliable at all. And the graphics guy, well, I only saw him once and then he either left the course or just stopped turning up, I don’t really know. So in the end it was just the three of us left trying to keep it all going.
