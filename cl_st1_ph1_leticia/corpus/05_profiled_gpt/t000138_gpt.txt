The problem is really how they sell the houses. They release a whole batch of plots, but they don’t all get sold at the same time or at the same stage. So you end up with some houses, like ours, that get bought later on, even though they’re meant to be built alongside others. That means the ones that should be going up together are actually all at different points because people have bought them at different times. Then parts of the build just get held up while they’re waiting for customers to choose things, or they deliberately delay bits. It all just knocks everything back and slows the whole process down.
