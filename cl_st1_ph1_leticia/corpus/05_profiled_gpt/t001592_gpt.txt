I remember being at me grandma and grandad’s, trying to stay in bed during the air raids. You’d just be getting settled, then they’d be calling you to get up and go into the air raid shelter. Up and down, up and down, it went on and on. In the end I said I’d had enough and went over to me mum’s. The houses round there were terrible, absolutely crawling with black cockroaches, infested, everywhere you looked.
