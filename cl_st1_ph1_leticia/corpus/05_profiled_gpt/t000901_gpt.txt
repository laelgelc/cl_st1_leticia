Well the kids had done a few lessons on the dry ski slope beforehand, just to get them used to it, and when we went back out to Andorra as a family we all had a lesson together as well. But I was still absolutely terrified. In the end we arranged for me to have a one-to-one lesson with an instructor, just to try and get me over it. I remember being on the chairlift with him and just crying, and he kept asking me, what are you actually scared of? Are you scared of falling off the lift? Are you scared of falling when you’re skiing? And I was trying to explain, no, it wasn’t that, I just felt so scared when I looked down the mountain. It was just this horrible fear, even though I couldn’t quite pin down exactly what it was.
