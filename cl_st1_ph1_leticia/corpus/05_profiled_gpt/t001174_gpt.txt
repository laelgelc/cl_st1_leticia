I do think the two things are really bound up together – the modules you choose and what you end up wanting to write the thesis on. If you just start working and reading now, the decisions will come out of that, and the ideas will begin to form quite naturally as you go along. From my own experience, I have to say I think a philosophical subject lends itself much better to a long thesis than a purely literary one. I honestly couldn’t have written a big thesis on a straight literary topic; I’d have found it very hard going, whereas philosophy gave me more to get my teeth into.
