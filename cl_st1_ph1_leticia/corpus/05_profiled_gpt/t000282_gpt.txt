And then when the dictatorship finished and they could pick any name they wanted, all these new foreign products started coming in. Because a lot of people didn’t really understand English, they just grabbed names straight off the packaging. So you ended up with kids called Coca-Cola, or Usmail because they saw “US Mail” on a label, or Nasa, or even Madeinusa from “Made in USA”. They just thought, that looks cool, that must be a name, let’s call our kid that.
