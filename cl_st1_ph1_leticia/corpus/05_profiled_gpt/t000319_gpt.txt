I feel like we’ve had this global system for the last thirty years or so that’s been absolutely obsessed with economic outcomes, and it’s been at the expense of the environment and society. That’s been the dominant pattern, really, and when it finally collapses it’s going to be momentous. 

At the same time, I honestly believe that as a species we’re becoming more loving and more open. You can almost see it happening. The whole refugee situation, for example, is forcing people to really look at their own prejudices. Instead of automatically seeing others through the lens of religion, they’re having to see them as humans first, which I think is incredibly important.
