It was actually good fun in the evening, and I thought it was quite worthwhile as a sort of reconnaissance, really. The place has such a wide range of things on offer, but the information’s all a bit scattered about, so it was useful just to get the lie of the land. The staff were very helpful – they even took a board off the wall for us so we could see it properly, and they shifted a table and chair out of the way so we could get to it. There weren’t many people there at all, which was quite nice in a way. And we had a fire, though it wasn’t an open one.
