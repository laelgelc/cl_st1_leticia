there's loads of really disturbing Roald Dahl stories, they all sort of blur into one in my head, but there’s this one that really stuck with me. Everything seems a bit off, and then he eventually finds out she’s been killing her previous lodgers and preserving them. She’s also got these cats in the living room that he thinks are alive at first, but then he realises they’re actually preserved, not mummified or anything.
