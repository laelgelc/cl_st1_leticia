I was trying to find a navy bag to go with my dress. I hadn’t seen anything in Dorothy Perkins that I liked, so I’d more or less written it off. Then we were walking past and you said, “Oh that bag’s nice in the window,” and you thought it was in Evans. Turned out it was actually Dorothy Perkins. You said that was the one you liked, so I said I’d go back in and have another look. I went back into the shop but still couldn’t see it anywhere inside, so in the end I asked one of the assistants. She checked and said it was the only one they had.
