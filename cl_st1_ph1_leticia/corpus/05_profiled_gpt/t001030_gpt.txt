I’ve been absolutely binging on “Sounds of the Seventies” on BBC iPlayer lately. I suddenly realised I’d somehow missed three weeks of it, so I’ve been catching up properly. I had it on while I was tidying up and cooking the other day and it was just perfect, really enjoyable. There was a Bowie special in there as well, which I loved.

Music just moves me so much, especially all that seventies stuff – the rock, the disco, the ska, the reggae. It does something to me physically, I can feel it. If I’ve got that kind of music on while I’m walking for exercise, it almost doesn’t feel like effort at all, it’s like my body just goes with it and the time disappears.

What’s funny is, I’ve noticed that when I put my iPod on random, I hardly ever get anything from the eighties. It’s all seventies, then a little bit of nineties, loads of noughties, and a bit of sixties. Somehow the eighties have almost vanished from it, which is strange because I do actually like eighties music. I keep thinking, where’s it all gone then? It’s as if my iPod’s decided I’m a seventies person and that’s that.
