It’s this comedy night where people are meant to try new material, and almost every time someone gets up there without having written anything, it just completely tanks. They’ll go on with this idea of, “Oh I’ll just wing it,” and you can almost feel the room tighten because hardly anyone actually has the eloquence or the quickness to pull that off. You need such a massive backlog of comedy in your head to be able to improvise properly. Some of them are genuinely funny and can write and deliver really good jokes, but that’s a totally different skill. Being able to improvise onstage like that usually only comes after years and years of doing it.
