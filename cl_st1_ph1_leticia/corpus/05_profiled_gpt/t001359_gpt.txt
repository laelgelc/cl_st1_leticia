I was talking to this girl a couple of weeks ago, and she grew up bilingual, with two different languages at home – say, German and Italian, just as an example. Then on top of that she learned English, or at least really improved her English later on, and she’d also done French at school. She said sometimes, when she’s trying to find a word, all these languages sort of jumble up in her head, and her brain just throws out whatever comes first, from whichever language. And then there’s this other person I know, whose English is now really quite advanced, and he also speaks both Catalan and Castilian Spanish.
