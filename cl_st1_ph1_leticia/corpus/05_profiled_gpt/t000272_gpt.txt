I was working with these really disturbed children at this school, and they kept telling me I was doing really well, which was nice, you know. Then we had this absolutely terrible winter. I didn’t have a car at the time and I was living in Nottingham. My tutor, who the pupils couldn’t stand, decided to send me for my main secondary teaching practice to Derby. So I had to get a bus into Derby and then another bus out to Mickleover. Meanwhile, all the others who had cars were allowed to teach locally, which did my head in.
