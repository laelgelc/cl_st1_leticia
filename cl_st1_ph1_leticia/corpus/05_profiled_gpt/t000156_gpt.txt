When you’re watching it you’re constantly putting yourself in their position, even though you know it’s all fantasy. You can’t help thinking, “What would I do if I was there?” and that makes it really gut wrenching. And it’s such a strong dig at the way we’re all obsessed with reality TV. Everyone in the film is glued to these massive screens, compulsively watching these kids kill each other. You get the sense that if people just stopped watching, the whole thing would collapse.
