I remember when we used to go to Norwich, there was only the one Match of the Day camera there, and that was it. There weren’t any proper commentary-box facilities like you get now. We’d watch the game, then rush home afterwards to put the telly on and see who’d actually won, because you couldn’t always tell properly at the ground. The whole thing was done in this sort of tent-like setup, very makeshift. If it was windy, the camera would sway about and the picture on the screen would go from side to side, so it looked as if you were out at sea watching the football.
