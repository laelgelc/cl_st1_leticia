Just think of it like one of those essays we had to do in primary or early high school. You know, where the teacher would say “go away and find out about this topic,” and you’d just look stuff up, see what the pros and cons are, and then write what you think based on what you’ve found. It doesn’t have to be some massive, scary academic thing. If you break it down like that, it’s totally manageable and you’ll be able to get through it without overthinking everything.
