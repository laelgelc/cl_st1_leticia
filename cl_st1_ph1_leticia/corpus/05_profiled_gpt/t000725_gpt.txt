I think it was because the others were still there, it was the people really. There were four of us who were very close, and in the space of about two years we’d all gone. One of them, the one I’d known the longest, got cancer not long after she left and she died in her early forties. I went to see her shortly before she died. That hit me very hard, much more than leaving the job itself.
