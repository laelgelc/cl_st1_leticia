I asked him, “Do people actually come back to see the body then?” because I honestly wasn’t sure if that really happened. And he said, “Oh yes, they do,” and explained that that’s why the body has to be kept presentable all the time. He said you never know when a family member might suddenly arrive, even from far away, and only have a very short window to say goodbye. That idea, that someone might rush in like that, really surprised me; I hadn’t realised they had to think like that.
