I really, really wanted a Fiesta, just because they’re such nice cars, and I’d had my heart set on one of the newer-shaped ones. But when it actually came down to it, they were just too expensive for what I could realistically manage. Even after I’d scraped every bit of money together and extended the loan as far as I sensibly could, it still didn’t quite stretch to a Fiesta. So in the end I had to be a bit more practical. I went for another car instead that was basically the same sort of age, with similar mileage and pretty much the same kind of spec, but it made a lot more financial sense. It just worked out to be the more sensible option for my budget.
