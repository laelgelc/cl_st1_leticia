I used to have this job where students would come to me with all sorts of questions about essays and referencing, and I’d end up giving slightly different answers to each of them because everyone’s situation was a bit different. Later on, I moved into working more directly with academics, trying to get this idea of “academic literacy” properly embedded into their courses. It wasn’t about transforming everything overnight, more about gently upskilling them, helping them tweak what they were already doing so that those skills were actually built into the teaching rather than bolted on separately.
