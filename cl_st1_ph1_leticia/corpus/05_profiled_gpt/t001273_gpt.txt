My cleaner texted me on Saturday night and told me to watch the X-Factor because there was someone on it who looked like me. So I switched it on, quite curious, and there was this twenty‑three‑year‑old model with a very, very posh accent. I just sat there thinking, this is absolutely nothing like me at all. But clearly my cleaner must think I’m quite posh if that’s who she thought resembled me, which I found rather amusing and a bit surprising.
