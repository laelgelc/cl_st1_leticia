We were driving through this really hilly area, all these old stone houses from the mill days, and they were filthy, really grubby looking. We came up this really steep terrace and I didn’t even want to park there, it felt that awkward. Then I suddenly spotted this bright yellow child’s bike actually mounted up on a wall. It looked so odd I couldn’t stop staring at it. A bit further along there was another bright yellow bike, this time stuck up on a post outside a little bike repair shop.
