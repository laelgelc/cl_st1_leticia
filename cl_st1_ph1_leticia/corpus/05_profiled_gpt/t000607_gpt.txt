If I’m honest, the one big catch with living forever is if it was just me and nobody else. That’d be brutal, watching everyone you love grow old and die over and over. It’s hard enough losing people once. Some of the science fiction tackles that, actually. You get these characters who’ve been around so long they’re just completely jaded with it all. They end up opting out in different ways – going into these virtual worlds where they can escape reality, or choosing to go into hibernation and saying, “Wake me up in a few thousand years when something interesting is happening again.”
