Some of the student loans that were taken out about ten years ago have actually been sold off to private companies now, so instead of dealing with the government loan body people are suddenly having to deal with these companies instead. The problem with that is that the companies can change the rules about how you repay, and that’s what really worries me. If my loan got sold on like that, I’m scared they’d just turn round and say I had to repay it all immediately. I don’t think that should be allowed at all. At eighteen you just sign these agreements without really understanding them, and there isn’t any real choice anyway.
