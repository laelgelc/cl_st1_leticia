When you’re putting a story together, you usually start by mapping time against the acts or the escalation – how things build, where the key turning points sit, that sort of thing. Then you decide whether you’re really going to use the protagonist’s character arc properly. To do that you have to pin down the core of the character, the one underlying reason and common thread that actually drives him along his trajectory. Once you’ve got that, the whole structure makes sense and you can see what belongs and what doesn’t.
