I absolutely hated this thing they give you after meals in India, it’s like a seed-and-sugar digestif. It tastes so strongly of aniseed, and it’s just everywhere, like you get it nationwide. They serve it in this sort of plant-like container at the end of the meal, and it’s really unlike anything I’ve ever seen outside India. The worst bit is the sugar – it often has ants in it, so they put some in your hand and you’ve got to blow the ants out before you eat it. It just completely put me off, I couldn’t stand it at all.
