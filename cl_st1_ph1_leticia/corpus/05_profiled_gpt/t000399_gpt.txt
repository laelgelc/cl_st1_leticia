when I was going to work I’d have to be out the house for about five to seven in the morning, in the pitch dark, snow on the ground, absolutely freezing. I’d rush to catch the seven o’clock bus, then I’d be stood there for about half an hour in minus eight degrees waiting for the next one to turn up. And once I finally got off that second bus, I still had a ten, sometimes fifteen minute walk, all uphill, from the bus stop to get into work. It was just a horrible start to the day, every day, in that kind of weather.
