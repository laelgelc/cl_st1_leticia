I’d texted her to say I’d give her a ring the next day, planning to call about half two. Then, about half an hour before that, she rang me instead. She said she’d literally just got home and had to go straight back out again, so she wouldn’t be in at half past two after all. She asked if I was in that night, and I said no, I had Avon or something on, so we’d have to speak tomorrow instead. That’s when she said, “I’ve got loads to tell you.”
