So because we’d said we’d just do presents when we were both in Germany, I honestly wasn’t expecting anything for my birthday while I was in Peru. I’d sort of written it off and was fine with that. Then on the morning of my birthday the doorman stopped me and handed me this little freeze-dried rose and a card from him. It was really sweet, and in the card he’d written that he didn’t want to leave me with nothing on my birthday, and to have a really good day at work. I thought that was it. But when I got back later and went into my room, there was another present and a card on my bed that he’d arranged with the maid to put there specially for my birthday.
