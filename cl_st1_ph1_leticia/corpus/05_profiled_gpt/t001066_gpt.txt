Well, what really tickled me was that when he was talking about “your mother” he clearly meant my mum, and never once mentioned the woman he was actually married to at the time. I just found that so funny, that in his muddled state he still associated everything with my mother. And then, in the same visit, his own younger brother came in and he didn’t recognise him at all, just called him this kind man who brought him water. When I explained who everyone was, I had the distinct feeling they suspected I was only there after his money.
