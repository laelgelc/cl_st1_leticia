I just don’t really get why they latch onto these really specific numbers and act like they’re magic. It’s the same with those recommended weekly alcohol units – they pick a figure and suddenly that’s the line between “healthy” and “unhealthy”. It all feels a bit arbitrary. And they’ve done it before with salt, haven’t they? That whole phase of “don’t eat too much salt” everywhere. I still remember that advert with Jenny Eclair going on about not eating more than a bit of salt – it stuck in my head, but it always felt a bit over‑simplified.
