I’d honestly never really thought about all the different types of education before, not properly anyway, until I was helping her pick a school for her kid. It was only then that it really hit me how early it all starts. Her little girl’s six, and they had this fancy dress day at school. They sent home a list of things you could go as – like “doctor” and so on – and next to each one it actually said stuff like how much you earn and that you care for people. So you’re basically being taught to think in those terms from that age.
