I keep thinking I really ought to walk that parcel down to the post office instead of getting someone to run it down in the car, but I can just see how it’ll go. I’ll come home, have some lunch, and then I’ll end up saying to myself, “Oh, I’ll do it tomorrow.” And then tomorrow comes and it’s the same thing again. It’s like this endless cycle of putting things off, and it feels almost like a sort of depression, the way I just can’t seem to get myself moving to do these simple little jobs.
