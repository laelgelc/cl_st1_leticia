What I hadn’t realised at first was that there was this other car tucked in behind us, clearly assuming we knew exactly where we were going. Eventually I just said, this can’t be right, and we stopped and turned round. When we came back past him, I had to say, I’ve absolutely no idea where I’m going, I’m just guessing like you are.
