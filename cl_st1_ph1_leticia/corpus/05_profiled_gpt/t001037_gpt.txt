I mean for me, in a relationship the motivation has to be that you genuinely want to see the other person. Not because you’re bored, or stuck, or don’t know what to do, but because you actually want to be with them. And at the same time, it can’t be this dependent, clingy thing. I really believe each person should be a complete individual in their own right, with their own life and interests, and then you come together and make something more. Together you become a greater whole, something beautiful and beneficial and expansive, and yes, challenging for both of you.
