I've never had to deal with this illness while working before, so I’ve got no idea what’s normal or what I should be doing. When I went to the hospital, the specialists were actually really surprised that I’d had no help at all – nothing from my GP or anyone else. They said they’d have to look at my whole situation properly, but only once I’d finished this three-appointment programme I’m on at the moment.

What makes it more confusing is that most of the other people at the clinic aren’t working at all. A lot of them can barely get around, and some are basically bed bound. Just going to see the doctor wipes them out completely. One appointment is enough to totally floor them, they’re that severely affected. It’s hard to know where I fit in that, or what I should be expecting of myself.
