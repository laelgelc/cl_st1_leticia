We made ours kind of in the middle of the two driveways, between ours and next door’s. It ended up in this slightly awkward spot where, if you didn’t steer carefully, it could have been in the way for parking. I actually texted our neighbour’s daughter to say, you know, “If it’s in your way you can just kick it over,” and she replied saying she couldn’t do that, which was quite sweet really. 

Over the next day it just sort of slowly gave up on life. First it started leaning, then the eyes went, then the nose, and every time I looked out the window it was a bit more tragic. By the end it was just this small, depressing little heap where our amazing snowman had been.
