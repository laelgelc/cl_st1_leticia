what I was thinking was something a bit like hangman for the memory verse. So you’d have all the blanks written out on a sheet that only the leaders can see. Then the teams have to go off and do some sort of task or challenge first – once they’ve done that, they can come up to a table and ask for a letter. A leader would then fill that letter into the right spaces on the separate sheet. After that they’d have to go back to their home base before they can do the next task and come back for another letter, and so on, until they’ve worked out the whole verse. We’d have to make sure the teams can’t see each other’s answers though, so they’re not just copying.
