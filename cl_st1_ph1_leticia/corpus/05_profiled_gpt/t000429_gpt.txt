I reckon a lot of it’s down to the way we live now, you know, everything’s so disinfected and scrubbed that people don’t build up any tolerance. I’ve got a really bad dust allergy myself – used to be constantly sneezing, eyes streaming, absolutely miserable. I was quite sceptical about antihistamines at first, thought they wouldn’t do much, but they’ve made a massive difference, as long as I remember to take them. Now it seems loads of people go to pieces at the slightest trigger – all these peanut allergies you never used to hear about. And they even say male sperm counts are dropping globally because of oestrogen from the pill ending up in the water supply.
