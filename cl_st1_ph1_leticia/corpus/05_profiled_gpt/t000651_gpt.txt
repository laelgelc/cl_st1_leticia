So after all that, I ended up coming up with a third idea, really as a reaction to the vandalism and the stealing that had gone on with the previous map. The basic thought was that if people weren’t so worried about losing things, they might not be so inclined to nick or wreck stuff in the first place. So I suggested that most of the everyday resources – things like dirt, wood, sand, meat, all the stuff you’re constantly using – should be held communally. Anyone could take what they needed from the shared stores. Then only the really valuable things, the rare resources, would be kept private and hidden away. The hope was that that balance would cut down on resentment and encourage more of a community spirit.
