I had such a good reputation in that place that some of the classes were actually terrified of me. There was this one time, I remember it vividly, I decided to have a bit of fun with them. I got into the classroom before the bell went and hid myself just behind the door, out of sight. Then the bell went and they all came piling in, shouting and messing about, thinking there was no teacher there. I just stood and watched them for a moment, taking it all in. Then I stepped out from behind the door, gave them a big smile, and the whole room just went absolutely silent. They all sat down straight away, and I just said, “hello.”
