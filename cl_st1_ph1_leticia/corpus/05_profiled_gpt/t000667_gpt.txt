When I left the hospital I had this really strong feeling that I wouldn’t be going back. It was like something came over me, so when I said goodbye to him, I did it very deliberately, almost as if I knew it was the last time. He looked really, really ill, worse than before, and that really stuck with me. He still wanted to take the children in to see him, but I said maybe they should have the choice, rather than us deciding for them. I just felt it was important that they were given that option, especially seeing how bad he looked.
