I told the masseur my mum was dying, and she immediately asked if Mum was a worrier and whether it was something to do with the spleen. I said she did worry a lot, and that it was pancreatic cancer. The masseur started talking about how, in her understanding, anger gets stored in the spleen. I was really emotional before the massage, so I questioned her on it, and she explained more about this idea that anger is associated with the spleen. It was a bit strange, but it stuck with me, that link she made between my mum’s constant worrying, stored anger, and the particular area of her body where the cancer was.
