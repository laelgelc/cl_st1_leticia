I quite like watching what my hair’s doing as it goes grey, actually. There’s this one grey streak that just keeps getting longer as more of the hairs decide to lose their pigment and join in. I used to keep the really odd ones, like the hairs that were half white and half brown, in what I called my “hair museum”. I don’t bother now, though, because I get those strange half-and-half hairs so often it wouldn’t be anything special to save them anymore.
