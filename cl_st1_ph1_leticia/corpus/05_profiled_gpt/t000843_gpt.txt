Well, somebody has now invented exactly the sort of thing I was talking about – proper prefabricated shower cubicles. You can basically get this whole unit, all in one, that you just put in, and it does away with most of the faffing about with tiles and grout and all the rest of it. And they’ve also brought out these big waterproof sheets – “wedi board” – that you just glue straight onto the wall, so instead of lots of tiles and joints you’ve just got these large panels. The only seams are really in the corners, which might still be a bit iffy leak‑wise. It does make you wonder how people come up with these ideas, and why it took so long.
