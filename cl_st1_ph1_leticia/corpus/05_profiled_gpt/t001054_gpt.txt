A Facebook memory popped up from four years ago, showing when you were just over two and we went to watch the diving at the Olympic Stadium. It reminded me how we had to be absolutely silent in there, because the divers on the ten-metre board needed to concentrate and any sudden noise could really put them off. You absolutely hated having to be quiet. The building where the diving was held was incredibly warm as well, and you were really uncomfortable in the heat, so you were very cross about the whole thing and not enjoying it at all.
