I use this little row-counting app on my phone for my knitting now, instead of faffing about with bits of paper. I’d got myself up to forty-four rows, feeling quite pleased, and then I just left the phone sitting on the arm of the sofa while I carried on. The cat suddenly decides she’s going to get involved, jumps up onto the arm of the sofa and just sits there, staring at him like she’s expecting something. She’s completely fixated on him, and I’m just thinking, don’t you dare touch that phone, I’ve only just got to forty-four.
