What I do now is sleep with just one of those inner-ear headphones in, and the other ear goes on the pillow, and it helps a lot with the noise. Honestly, those in-ear headphones have been a godsend for me. I used to be such a light sleeper, every little thing would wake me up. In India it was awful – there were constant beeping sounds, and people knocking and trying the door handles at night, which was actually quite scary when you’re trying to sleep.

The headphones don’t block everything out completely, so you can still hear things if you need to, but they make all the noise feel much further away. It’s like it’s happening at a distance instead of right next to you, which makes it so much easier to get to sleep.
