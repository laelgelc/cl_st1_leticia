In interviews when they ask why you want to work for them, you just sort of come out with the usual stuff, don’t you. I normally say I’ve researched the company, and then pick something off their website that sounds vaguely worthy or interesting. Quite often it’ll be something like their employee volunteering scheme, or some initiative they’ve got going on, and I’ll act like that’s really what drew me in. It’s all a bit generic and made up on the day, really, just to have an answer.
