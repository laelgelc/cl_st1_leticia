I remember coming out of the pub and just thinking, “Where’s the car gone then?” I was that shocked I even started looking over the fence, as if it might’ve somehow ended up down there. You do daft things when you’re in a bit of a panic. I’d promised my mate a lift home as well, so I thought at least he’d stick around while I tried to sort myself out. But no, he just said he was off to get the bus and disappeared, left me to it.
