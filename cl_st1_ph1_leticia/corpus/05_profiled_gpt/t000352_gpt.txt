I was watching this dot-com presentation the other day that just went on and on, far too long really. One of the slides they put up was a photograph of this woman’s daughter and her friend in what was clearly a North American kitchen. The two girls were standing there, each of them hunched over their mobile phones, texting away. What struck me was that they had their backs to each other, completely absorbed in their screens. There was no interaction between them at all. They might just as well have been on another planet, given how cut off from each other they seemed.
