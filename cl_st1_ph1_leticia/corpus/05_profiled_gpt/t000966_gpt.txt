I was there for what felt like ages, trying to coax these kittens out, calling them and offering them milk and bits to eat, and I was just about ready to give up. Then suddenly, I felt this tiny little kitten come right into my hand. We ended up keeping that one as our own cat. After that, the others started to follow, and we managed to get them all out and into a basket. The rest of the kittens all went on to really good homes.
