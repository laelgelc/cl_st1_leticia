My next-door neighbour was vegetarian for, what, ten, fifteen years, and then one barbecue she just suddenly said she really fancied a sausage. So she had one, just like that, and after that she just carried on eating meat again. Nobody said anything or made a fuss about it, it was just accepted, and that was that. I remember thinking it was a bit strange how quickly she switched back, after being veggie for so long, but at the end of the day it’s up to her. Starting or stopping being vegetarian is each person’s own choice.
