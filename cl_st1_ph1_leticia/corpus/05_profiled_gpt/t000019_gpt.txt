If I had, say, an hour for lunch, by the time I’d actually got myself home there was hardly any time left. If I got the bus, or even if I walked, I’d end up with maybe ten minutes, twenty minutes at the most, sometimes half an hour if I was really lucky, actually in the house before I had to turn round and go straight back again. It seems completely mad to me now that I ever bothered doing that, all that faff just for such a tiny little window at home, but I did sometimes, and at the time it somehow felt worth it.
