I would quite happily skip mashed potatoes, I just don’t like them at all. I do sometimes wonder if that goes back to junior school, actually. We didn’t queue up at a hatch with dinner ladies serving, like lots of people did. We had these big tables, and the oldest kid on each table had to dish out the food to everyone else. So you just got what you were given, really, rather than choosing. I sometimes think that whole setup might be why I’ve never taken to mash.
