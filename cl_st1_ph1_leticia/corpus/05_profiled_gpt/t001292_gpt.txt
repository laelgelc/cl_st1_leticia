Acting out of fear feels like such a poor motivation to me, and that’s where I get really conflicted with people begging for money. I don’t usually give money, but I don’t want to ignore them either. So what I tend to do is more of a spiritual or energetic thing. Silently, I “word” through the person, a bit like a Namaste, acknowledging their presence and the equality between us. I imagine projecting this pink, loving energy and intend the highest and best outcome for them. Maybe, in their string of incarnations, they’ve chosen to experience lack this time.
