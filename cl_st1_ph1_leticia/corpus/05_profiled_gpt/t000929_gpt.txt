If you've got very little or no work experience, this whole framework becomes your evidence base. Instead of just saying you’re good at collaborating, you can actually point to the work you’ve done with other people inside the system. It’s like what photographers do with a portfolio, or what filmmakers do with a showreel – you show, you don’t just tell. 

That’s why I really like the idea of turning this into a product for young people: something that helps them sell themselves without relying on the stereotypical CV, which is so hard to make genuinely appealing.
