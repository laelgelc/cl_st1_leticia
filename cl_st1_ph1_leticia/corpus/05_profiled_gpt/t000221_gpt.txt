Campo Santa Margherita’s meant to be really cheerful and picturesque, isn’t it? It says here it’s full of different architectural styles, and it’s dedicated to Saint Margaret of Antioch – she’s the one with the dragon emblem. Apparently kids love it there, they’re always whizzing round on bikes and skates. The guidebook says it’s popular like that day and night. And the reason it’s such a big square is because in the nineteenth century they enlarged it by filling in some of the canals at the southern end.
