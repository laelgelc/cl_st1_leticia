So the idea is: we start by promoting some kind of free mini product, yeah? Something small but useful that people can get just by signing up on the website. That’s the first hook. They land on the site, stick in their details, and they get access to that free thing straightaway, which starts building a bit of interest and trust.

Then, once that’s running, we put out a couple of YouTube videos that are all linked back to the website. Every video points people back to the site, where that free mini product is waiting. So you’ve got this loop going: people see the video, get curious, click through, visit the site, and sign up.
