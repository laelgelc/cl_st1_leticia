I just let them all go off and do their thing and I stayed put on the baby slope, still trying to get the hang of it. After a bit I thought, right, I’ll have a break, so I went and sat in this little café at the bottom. I’m sitting there with a drink, looking out, and I suddenly spot her up on this snowy bank at the side, literally climbing down it. She kept chucking her skis down in front of her, clambering after them, throwing them again, all the way down. When she finally got to the bottom she just said, “I’m never skiing again.”
