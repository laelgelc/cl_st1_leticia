I think this idea could work really well because it changes the whole dynamic of what people bring. Instead of just turning up with problems they want fixed, they’d be coming with experiences they’re keen to develop by sharing their stories. That feels much more positive and generative. Once those stories are out there, other people—because we’re social creatures—would naturally want to get involved, respond, and add their own layers. It becomes a kind of collaborative storytelling, where development happens organically through that shared process.
