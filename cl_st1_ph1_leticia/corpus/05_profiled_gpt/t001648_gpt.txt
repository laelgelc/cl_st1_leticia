I remember once having this awful, really painful infected cyst on my vulva. I just booked in with whoever was free next, and it turned out to be this young Asian woman doctor. As soon as I told her what the problem was, I could see this look of distaste, almost repulsion, go straight across her face. Then, when she examined me, it was there again, very clear, before she pulled herself together after a second or two and just got on with it. But I’d seen it, absolutely plainly. I was mortified. And I thought, this is exactly what people are frightened of – that someone’s going to think “oh, that’s disgusting”. It would be very easy, after that, to decide you’re not going back to the doctor again.
