I had this friend who was a civil servant, working in one of the government departments, and she was always the first to know what was going on. It was purely because she smoked. Ministers would head off to the smoking room for a cigarette, and that included the secretary of state and all sorts of very senior people. She’d be in there with them, chatting away, and she’d pick up information that was way, way above her pay grade. So in that sense, it was definitely sometimes worth being a smoker.
