On my eleventh or twelfth birthday, I had two school friends over, and they were both really into EastEnders. There was this big special episode on where Sonia gives birth, and they absolutely insisted we had to watch it. So we all ended up sitting there – me, my two friends, and my mum – watching this half-hour of childbirth. I’d never seen EastEnders before, so it was just incredibly distressing and made no sense to me at all. When it finished, my mum just went, “That’s EastEnders, see what you’ve been missing?” which was very much a joke. I now describe it, very ironically, as an excellent eleventh birthday.
