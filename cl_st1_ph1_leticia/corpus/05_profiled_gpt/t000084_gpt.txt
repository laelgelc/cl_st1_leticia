They see the kids going home at three or four and think we all just swan off then, but they’ve no idea what it’s actually like. Classroom teaching is really stressful and really hard work, and it’s not just the planning and marking, it’s the behaviour. When you’ve got disruptive pupils you’re not allowed to exclude, and there isn’t the proper support in place to deal with them, it wears you down. People on the outside just don’t appreciate that side of it at all.
