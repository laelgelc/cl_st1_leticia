I remember one night driving home in really heavy snow, the roads were all slushy and covered in salt. Everything was in such a state that you couldn’t see much on the cars, the muck was everywhere, all up the back. I got pulled over and the copper comes up and says he can’t see my number plates, and he’s going to give me a ticket for it. I said to him, “Well, have a look at yours,” because his plates were just as bad, completely covered in the same slush and salt. He went back and had a look at his own car then, realised the state of it. In the end, even though he started off trying to ticket me, he let me off.
