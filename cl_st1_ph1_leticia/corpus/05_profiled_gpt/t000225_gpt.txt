I've been thinking a lot about Mum and Dad recently, and about the grounds for divorce back then. I’ve started to believe that Mum tried to convince us all that Dad was an alcoholic, just so she could use that as the only legal ground for divorce that really applied. I can clearly remember Dad crying and saying she was lying, and at the time I just didn’t understand what was going on. It’s only really clicked with me now, looking back on it, how that might have been what she was doing.
