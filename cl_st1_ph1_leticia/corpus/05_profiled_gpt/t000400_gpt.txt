It really did suck, that old commute, it was such hard work. I’d finish a shift at eight o’clock at the care home, already knackered, and then I’d have that long trek ahead of me. I had to walk about three miles from the care home just to get to the bus stop, and then stand there for around twenty minutes, just hoping the bus actually turned up on time. After that, there was still the ride home, so the whole journey in the evening took about an hour.
