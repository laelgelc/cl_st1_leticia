It’s amazing, I honestly thought it was completely dead and it’s just suddenly come back to life. The whole thing looked beyond saving, and then it’s done exactly what hers did. When she first had it, she more or less neglected the main plant as well – the big stem went all brown and dead-looking, and she just sort of gave up on it. Then she noticed these little new shoots coming off what looked like a dead main stem, and she picked those off and potted them up into smaller pots. So now I’m thinking that might just be what these plants do, that they sort of revive themselves like that even when you’re convinced they’re finished.
