When I was helping the German teacher at my previous school, we tried a different approach. These were students who’d actually chosen to do German, but they were still really reluctant. So I used to take them out of the classroom one by one and sit with them in a separate room, just going through the exam questions. At first, every single one of them would say, “I don’t know anything, I can’t do it.” But once we started working through the questions together, they suddenly realised they did actually know the material.
