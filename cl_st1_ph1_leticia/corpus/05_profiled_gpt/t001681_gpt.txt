well when he first stopped doing bits of project work and was suddenly at home more, it all got a bit muddled really. Now he does most of it, and he’s actually a very good cook, so he mostly does the cooking too.

But I feel quite conflicted about it. On the one hand I’m glad he’s taken things on, on the other I can get quite resentful that I’m not the one doing the shopping or choosing the food, which sounds ridiculous when I say it out loud. I don’t like that side of myself, but it’s there – I miss having that control over what we eat and how things are done.

And I do worry that when I retire there’ll be another fight about it all. At the moment he’s got his way of doing the kitchen and the house, and I know I’m going to want to reclaim some of that. I can already feel myself bristling at the idea of just fitting in around his routines, and I really don’t like that feeling.
