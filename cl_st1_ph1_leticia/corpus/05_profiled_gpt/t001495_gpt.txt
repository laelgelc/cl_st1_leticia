well it depended loads on the level really, like the top classes sometimes were tiny, only three or four of us, but the lower level ones could be massive, like thirty in a class. and then the show rehearsals were a bit different again – they were about a fiver an hour, and we’d be doing three hours a night, four or five nights a week. there were around a hundred people at those, so that’s quite a lot of money coming in. and on top of that we all had to buy our own costumes. plus we got a day off school every time we did a show.
