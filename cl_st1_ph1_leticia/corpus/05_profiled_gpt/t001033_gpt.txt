I just really hope they know what it’s like starting out with a place like this. Usually you lose money the first year, maybe just about break even in the second, and only start making a proper profit in the third. It’s a long haul, isn’t it. It reminds me of that Polish couple I knew in that remote village in Northern Ireland, running their restaurant. They were open seven days a week, absolutely relentless, but everyone always knew they’d be open, so people would plan to go there. That kind of consistency really worked for them.
