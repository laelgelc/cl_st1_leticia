I got talked into moving my pension into this new superannuation scheme that, on paper, looked so much better than the one I was in. At the time it sounded like a really good idea, so I went along with it. What I didn’t properly take on board was that it needed to be actively managed, which just isn’t something I’m on top of. Once I realised that, I tried to move it back again, but I couldn’t. So now I’m stuck with two frozen pension pots. One of them is projected to pay me about twelve pounds a year, which is nothing at all really, and the other one is only going to pay about a hundred and twenty pounds a year. In the end I’ve had to start my superannuation again completely from scratch.
