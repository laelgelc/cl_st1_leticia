I don’t think there’s actually a shortage of houses, really. For me it’s more of an ethical thing: I don’t think people should be allowed to own more than two houses. You’ve got your main home, and if you’re lucky maybe a little summer place or something, and that should be it. If ownership was limited like that, fewer people would end up having to rent because there wouldn’t be so many properties tied up. I know most people don’t own more than that, but there are some who do.
