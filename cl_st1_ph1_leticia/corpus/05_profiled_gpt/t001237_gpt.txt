It was actually a really good night, loads of people turned up and the atmosphere in the pub was lovely. There were quite a few of the Shakespeare lot there as well, so when he finally arrived, he went and sat miles away from me, chatting to somebody else. When he did come over, he gave me a hug, then asked if I was going to the bar. I said yes, so he came up with me. I offered to get him a drink, and while we were waiting he was saying he wanted all the Shakespeare audition stuff from my perspective, that he trusted me. After we’d both got our drinks, I assumed he’d go back to where he’d been sitting and I’d go back to mine, but instead he just stopped at another table and sort of hovered there.
