we had this sort of rota going on really, different ones of us in and out of the room with him, or just leaving him to sleep while the nurses and Macmillan people came and went through the day. it wasn’t all hushed and miserable, we were just… being us. we’d sit there laughing and chatting, doing crosswords round the bed, and it could get really silly at times. we’d end up laughing so much the nurses would pop their heads round the door to see what on earth was going on because we were making such a racket.
