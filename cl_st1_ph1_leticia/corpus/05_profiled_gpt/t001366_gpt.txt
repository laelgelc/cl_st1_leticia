I remember when I was little, just sort of picking things out on the piano by ear, and they just didn’t notice, really. They were knackered by then, I suppose, and it just got overlooked. Later on, when I finally did get lessons as a teenager, I completely lost the motivation. There were too many other distractions, and it was such hard work once you start doing it properly. I didn’t stick with it, and now I feel this massive regret about it. It feels almost criminal not to be using that bit of my brain, you know? Like being illiterate, but in a musical sense. I lie awake sometimes thinking I should have done music with my life.
