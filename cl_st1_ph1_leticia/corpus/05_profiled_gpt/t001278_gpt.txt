I find the language they use on the BBC about war really grates on me, you know, all these headlines about “remembering the fallen.” It just feels like such a ridiculous euphemism for people who actually died in the war. It sanitises it in a way that I find quite irritating. When I was away on my French weekend, I met this woman who’s done loads of conflict resolution and peace‑building work in proper war‑torn areas, and that really stayed with me, hearing about what she’s been involved in.
