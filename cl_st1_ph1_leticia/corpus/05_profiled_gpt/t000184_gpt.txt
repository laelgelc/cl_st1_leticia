well it was actually quite funny in a really frustrating sort of way because I just could not find this People at Work exhibition at all inside the museum. I went in thinking it would be obvious, you know, some big sign somewhere, but there was nothing that really pointed you to it. So I asked one member of staff, and they told me it was on the second floor, quite confidently, so up I went.

When I got to the second floor, though, nobody up there seemed to have the faintest idea what I was talking about. I asked a couple of different people and got a lot of puzzled looks and vague gestures, but no one could actually direct me to it. It was as if the exhibition didn’t exist, which was rather odd considering it was supposed to be on before going on a nationwide tour.

Eventually someone else told me that no, it wasn’t on the second floor at all, it was actually on the first floor. So back down I went again. Even then it wasn’t straightforward, because it still wasn’t terribly well labelled and I had trouble finding it on the first floor as well. It felt like I was going round in circles for ages before I finally stumbled across it. No wonder there was hardly anybody in the exhibition if it was that difficult to track down.
