I’m not saying every single nun hates it or anything, but I just don’t think all of them actually enjoy having to follow loads of strict rules every single day. Some of them might like it, but others might just be putting up with a really hard, sort of punishing life because they think it’ll get them a really good spot in heaven. It’s like choosing to be really strict and deny yourself stuff on purpose. And if it turns out there is no heaven after all, then it feels like that whole life of hardship wouldn’t really have been a life well spent.
