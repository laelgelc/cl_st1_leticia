and the second bit of advice was about when the girls all fall out at school. You know, they’d come home absolutely devastated because so-and-so had said or done something dreadful, and you’d be in bits about it yourself, stewing all evening and half the next day. And then, of course, they’d trot back into school and by the following afternoon they were the best of friends again, as if nothing had ever happened. Meanwhile you’ve spent the whole day upset on their behalf. So the advice was very clear: don’t ring up the other mother and have a go, however much you feel like it, because the girls will sort it out themselves.
