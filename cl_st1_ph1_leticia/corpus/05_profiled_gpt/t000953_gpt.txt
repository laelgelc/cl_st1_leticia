We were in that new Costa in Brixton the other day, and this woman came over, clearly on a mission about reusable nappies. She gave us this leaflet about some cinema launch thing – they’re doing a parent‑and‑baby afternoon, plus a free demo about the nappies. All fairly standard until I actually looked at the form. One of the lines was “ethnicity”. It really struck me as odd in that setting. I mean, it’s just a small local event about nappies and a film screening, and yet they still feel the need to ask for your ethnicity on the sign‑up form.
