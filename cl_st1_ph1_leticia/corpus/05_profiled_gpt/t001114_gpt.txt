there’s this book I read that really stuck with me, it’s a young adult novel about this girl, she’s absolutely gorgeous but you don’t really like her as a character, she’s quite unsympathetic. she goes to this party and ends up being gang raped by a group of local footballers, and then they share the pictures of her online afterwards. the whole story is basically about what happens next – all the fallout around consent and how people react when things get spread on social media, and how she’s treated because of how she looks and behaves, even though what happened to her is obviously rape. it really digs into those grey areas people pretend are grey, when they’re actually not, and how social media just amplifies everything and makes it harder for her. I honestly think books like that should be recommended to young people, and especially to young men, because it really forces you to think about consent and consequences.
