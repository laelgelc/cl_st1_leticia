Back where we were, it was just hectic all the time – noisy, on busy streets, constant traffic and people. It always felt a bit full‑on. Out here it’s completely different; it’s calm and relaxed, and you can actually switch off when you’re at home. And even though we’re further out, getting into town’s dead easy. A taxi in of an evening’s about twelve quid, which really isn’t anything to worry about – it’s the price of a few pints, so it doesn’t feel like a big deal at all.
