We’d been to the Priory, you know, the manned site, probably last year, and in the little shop there they had all these guidebooks. I suddenly thought, that’s what we should have done for the other place – the free site we’d already been to. The guidebooks tell you far more than you ever get from the odd notice stuck up on the site itself. So we decided we really ought to go back and get one, but of course we kept putting it off and putting it off. Then we had this absolutely beautiful day, so we finally went, bought the guidebook, and went round the site again. It’s very near that fruit farm, so it made sense to pop there on the way back.
