Well, from what I know – which isn’t much, to be fair – daffodils and tulips are really hardy. In my family we’re generally awful with plants, but those always seemed to survive. We used to dig the bulbs up and chuck them out every year, then somehow they’d still come back. My dad would go at the garden before winter, digging everything up, and my mum would be telling him not to, but he’d do it anyway. Even with all that, the daffodils and tulips just kept on surviving.
