I did a proper classic dad thing the other day. I nipped to the shop without my glasses, bought a paper, then went and sat on a bench and could only read the big headlines. So I cycled back home, went to get my glasses out of the bag and thought, “Oh God, I’ve lost them.” I was hunting round, getting in a state, and of course I’d never taken them with me in the first place. It’s not like me at all, I’m usually really good at finding things.
