And I could hear water somewhere, proper running water, so I thought, well, we must be near it now. We followed the sound and there it was, the very place we'd been looking for all along. 

There was a chap there, so I got talking to him, and he says it used to be a mill. Then he tells me all the stuff off it, the bits and pieces, had been sold on to some mill down south for restoration. And he was dead against it, said it shouldn't have gone, like it was wrong really, taking it away from there just to smarten up another mill miles away.
