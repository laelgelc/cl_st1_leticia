Paul Merson was actually a really big-name player, and he ended up going bankrupt because he blew so much money on betting. He was saying once that his first professional pay cheque was only about a hundred quid, and he went straight down to Ladbrokes and lost the lot. He was that gutted he ripped his shirt up and then went home and told his mum he’d been mugged, just to cover up the fact he’d gambled all his wages away.
