There was that little car accident she had, nothing major, but people were shouting at her, telling her she was too young to be driving, as if she were some child who’d pinched the keys. They simply didn’t believe she could possibly be old enough because she’s so small and looks so very young.

And he’s exactly the same. Because he looks so young, and he’s not big either, he’s had trouble getting into places when he’s supposed to be working, going in to DJ. If the bouncers or the security don’t know him, they just see this baby-faced lad and say no, you’re not coming in without proper ID. He’s actually had to go all the way back home to get his passport, or a driving licence, just to prove he’s old enough to be there, even though he’s meant to be playing. It’s ridiculous really, but I keep saying to him, it’ll be an advantage one day. A few years down the line, when everyone else is starting to look their age, he’ll still be getting taken for much younger, and he’ll be glad of it then.
