We were in Australia, in that flat that had the little back gate out into the alley. He must’ve wondered where it went, so he opened it to have a look, and of course the dog shot straight out. Then he tore off after the dog and lost him completely. I had to wake your dad up – he was absolutely jetlagged – so I could take the car and drive round looking for them both. In the end I found him walking down the street, in tears, saying he couldn’t get the dog back.
