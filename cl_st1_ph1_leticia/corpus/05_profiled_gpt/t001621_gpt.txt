What really puts me off is when someone gets too insistent. There was this guy I was chatting to who, on paper, seemed great – really nice, fun, attractive, well-qualified, good job, and he’s got accommodation right in the centre. He comes back here quite a lot to play rugby, so he said he’d be around at the weekend and asked if I wanted to meet up. I was even wondering if he’d be staying in halls they put them up in when they play rugby. But that kind of pushiness just makes me lose interest.
