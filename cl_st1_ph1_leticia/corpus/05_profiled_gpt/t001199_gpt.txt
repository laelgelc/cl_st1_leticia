We started off down at about two thousand metres and ended up going all the way up to around four thousand three hundred in one day, even though you’re not really meant to sleep above about three and a half thousand first night. None of us were used to that kind of altitude at all, and I already had a bad stomach before we even got going. At first we followed the highway, then we decided to cut off and go another way. I wanted to go up along the ridge of the mountain, but instead we took this route along a bog, and it just got really, really boggy and really high.
