I do actually like yoga, but I use this P90X yoga video that goes on for about an hour and a half, and it just gets a bit boring and slow. It’s definitely harder than a standard yoga video, but I still find the pace drags. So what I do is play it at one-and-a-half speed, which brings it down to about an hour. That way each pose is quicker and I’m only holding them for about twenty to thirty seconds instead of longer, which suits me much better.
