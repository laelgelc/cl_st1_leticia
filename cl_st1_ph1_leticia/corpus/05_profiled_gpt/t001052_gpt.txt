I do see where you’re coming from about language, and I’m not saying it’s completely irrelevant, but I think we’re tinkering at the edges. For me, the real issue is the stigma around depression itself and whether people can actually access treatments that work. Changing “patient” to “client” or similar feels quite cosmetic when the underlying concepts and words like “depression” have been around for so long. I’d rather energy and resources went into reducing stigma and improving effective treatment than into these politically correct tweaks.
