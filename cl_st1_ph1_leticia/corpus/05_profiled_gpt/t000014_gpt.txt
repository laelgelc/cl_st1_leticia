Well, it’s very much a recreational course rather than a formal, traditional one. We’re running it here at the Creative Innovation Centre, and the emphasis is on creativity and innovation rather than a standard “beginners’ painting” sort of class. Most of the people who come are mature students, and quite a lot of them have already done a fair bit of art education in the past, though it’s open to anyone who wants to join. We’ve got roughly twenty people doing painting and drawing, and another twenty in the life drawing sessions. In spirit, it’s probably closest to a foundation-type art school course.
