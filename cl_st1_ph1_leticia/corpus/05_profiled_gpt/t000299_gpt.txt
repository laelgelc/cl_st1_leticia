feminism started off opposing gender roles, yeah, but obviously it was mostly women in it at the beginning because they were the ones being oppressed by those roles. That doesn’t make it weird or dodgy that it was mainly women, it just makes sense. It’s like with the civil rights movement – it was mainly minority ethnic groups leading that, because they were the ones actually being discriminated against. You don’t look at that and say, “oh, there aren’t enough white people so the movement’s invalid.” Same thing here: having mostly women in feminism doesn’t undermine what they’re fighting for at all. So I don’t get what your point is.
