I said to him, even if I had a gun in my hand, I’d still run away rather than shoot someone, because I’m not Rambo, am I? It’s just not me. And I kept saying, his whole scenario was ridiculous anyway – like, where are the police, where’s anyone in authority? It’s not real life, it’s like some action film in his head. I tried to explain that I’m a pacifist, and I just don’t believe in killing people, however bad they are, but I also admitted that part of that is because of my own experience. I’ve never had my sister hurt, my mum’s never been raped, I don’t know anyone who’s been killed. So I don’t have that kind of anger or personal drive for revenge that some people might have if they’d actually lived through something like that.
