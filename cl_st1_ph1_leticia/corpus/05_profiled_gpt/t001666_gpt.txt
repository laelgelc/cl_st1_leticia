I kept saying to him, “Look, I’ve given you every possible excuse not to pay, you really don’t have to,” but he just won’t have it. Even when I try to insist, he’ll sneak money into my bag so he’s still the one paying. I’ve told him he’s got to get over this, because he already pays for the petrol and for dinners when he comes down here. I never have that expense because I don’t go up to see him, so I don’t pay out in the same way. The least he can do, when he visits, is let me buy him dinner to say thank you for coming.
