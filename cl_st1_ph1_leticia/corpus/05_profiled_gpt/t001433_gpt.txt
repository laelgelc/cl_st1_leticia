It was funny, actually – it was only yesterday. This bloke came past and I stopped him and said something to him, and he just went, “Yeah, yeah,” agreed straightaway. As I was taking his address down, I said, “Oh, I know that area,” and he said, “Do you?” So I told him I grew up round there, on [that particular road], from when I was about five until I moved away in my twenties. Then we got talking about schools and I said I’d gone out of catchment for secondary, and he knew exactly what I meant. It turned out we had all these little links – same sort of local area, same schools – just one of those odd small‑world moments.
