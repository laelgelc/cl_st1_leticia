Years ago I was on a train, going past these blocks of flats around teatime, and I could see into all the kitchen windows. I found myself really wondering what people were cooking in there, in quite a lot of detail. Had they planned it, or decided on the way home? Had they taken the meat out this morning, or bought everything on the way back from work? I wouldn’t actually go and knock on the doors to find out, of course, but that sort of curiosity about what and why people are cooking is just how my mind works.
