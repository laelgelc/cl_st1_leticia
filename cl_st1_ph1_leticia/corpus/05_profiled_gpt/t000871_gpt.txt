Running the gallery really was the best thing I ever did, you know. I always brighten up when I talk about it. Value for money was absolutely crucial to me. I was very careful to keep the artists’ prices realistic, so that people who weren’t famous still had a proper chance to sell their work. At the same time, it meant that ordinary customers could actually afford to buy original art, not just prints or reproductions. I think that’s a wonderful thing, really – making it possible for both sides, the artists and the buyers, to connect in that way.
