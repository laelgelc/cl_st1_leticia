Samuel Pepys left his library to one of his family members, and then that person gave it to Magdalene College in Cambridge. In his diary he wrote in a special code, and somebody became fascinated by it and decided to try and work it out. The diary was very honest, but not very nice about some people, and if others had known what he really thought at the time, it could have caused a lot of trouble. So this man made it his lifetime’s work to crack Pepys’s code.
