In Kenya I was working in signals, codes and ciphers. I’d been sent out as the vanguard WRAF signals and ciphers officer, really to set up a section there. Before that I’d been very briefly in Egypt, and then in Cyprus during the terrorism. Kenya was meant to be rather similar in that sense: I was sent in anticipation that we might have to open up operations during terrorism. In fact, that never actually came to pass, so the plan we’d all rather expected simply didn’t happen.
