Well, exactly – a lot of people only get online at first just to keep in touch with their mates, don’t they, and that’s as far as their thinking goes. But for a lot of them, there’s a much bigger opportunity they’re not seeing. Take something simple like that pickup coach or bus that runs people home after they’ve been out drinking. If they had even a basic app, suddenly the whole thing changes.

People could just open their phone, see where the bus is, when it’s due, and choose for themselves when to leave the pub. They’re not hanging around wondering, they’re not missing it because no one told them. And from the operator’s side, that convenience means more people using it, more regularly, because it’s easy. So the tech actually grows the business, not just keeps people chatting.
