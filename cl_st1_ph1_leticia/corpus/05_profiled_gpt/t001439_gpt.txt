When I’m at the hospital, I find that visitors often come and talk to me, especially women. One lady came over whose husband had died on the first of May, and this was only the second week in May. She was full of praise for the staff, saying how wonderful they’d been with him. She told me she was organising a fundraising event in their garden. The idea was to throw paintballs at a big white sheet, then people would write messages on it, cut it up and auction off the pieces to raise money.
