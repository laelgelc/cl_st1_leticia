We were decorating and painting this house, and the owner starts telling us about this ridiculous business with his gas. He’d been trying for years to pay his gas bill, and the company just wouldn’t have it. He took me to show me the meter – perfectly clear, right there, a proper gas meter – and yet they kept insisting it didn’t exist. Every time he rang them, they told him there was no gas meter in the house. He’d say, “But I’ve got one, I can give you the reading now,” and they’d still come back with, “No, you haven’t got any gas.” He was honestly exasperated; he simply couldn’t get them to accept he had a meter.
