For a couple of months we had absolutely nothing coming to the feeder, and I was really disappointed. I did wonder if it was because there are so many cats passing through the garden. Then this great tit turned up that obviously had babies. It started bringing them to the feeder, which was lovely. The first day it brought two fledglings, the next day there were three, and then the day after that there were four. It was feeding them directly from the feeder, so they were clearly just fledging.
