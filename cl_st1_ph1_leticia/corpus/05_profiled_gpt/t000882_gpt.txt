I picked up this marvellous stack of ten CDs the other day, they were only 25p each. I absolutely love them all. They’re full of worship songs, hymns, carols, and some really top-quality British music – proper stuff like Elgar. One of them is actually a set with four discs in it. When you add it all up I only paid a tenner, so it’s effectively about a pound each, which is ridiculous value really. I’ve had such a lot of pleasure out of them already. There’s one especially beautiful one, Winchester Carols from Winchester Cathedral, with this glorious trumpet part – it goes, “da-da-da…” [starts imitating the trumpet sound].
