I’ve been going to that Monday night swim for years, straight after the triathlon club, and for ages I never even saw him there. Now he’s started coming back, and in this letter or text he’s sent he’s saying I must be coming to see him, because I’m always waiting to get in when they finish. So I told him, no, I’ve swum on Mondays for years, this is just what I do. And then, because he said I could easily have come five minutes later if I wanted, I actually did go later the following week, deliberately, just to make the point.
