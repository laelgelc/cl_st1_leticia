I suppose it’s possible I’ve gone off quite a few foods just from being sick on them. One of the worst times was on that camel safari in the Indian desert when I was really ill. We were camped downwind of these incredibly flatulent camels, and absolutely everything tasted awful, even my own gas. All we had to eat was chapatti and a bit of dal, and it just made everything seem worse. We were sleeping out in the open, and there were dung beetles crawling and scuttling around us as well. It was a pretty grim experience overall.
