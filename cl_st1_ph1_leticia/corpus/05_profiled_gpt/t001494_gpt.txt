there was this thing online the other day about supermarkets and people taking stuff back, and it absolutely blew up on social media. This bloke had gone to Aldi – or it might’ve been Lidl, I’m not entirely sure which – and bought himself a packet of chocolate biscuits. When he opened them, he discovered that one of the biscuits didn’t have any chocolate on it at all, and he was absolutely devastated. He put this post up, going on about how this one plain biscuit had completely ruined his Sunday morning.
