My last proper childhood nosebleed was actually when I was on this British Council trip to China, and it was all rather stressful. They’d managed to lose or at least misdirect our luggage in Hong Kong, and we only had a day there before I was due to move on to the next place, so I was worrying about whether I’d ever see my suitcase again. I didn’t have much money with me either, because I’d been a student for years, so that just added to the anxiety. When I finally got to Beijing it was the end of August, beginning of September, and it was absolutely boiling, which of course is dreadful for nosebleeds. I woke up the first morning in this basic student accommodation with blood everywhere, and there was no ice, nothing properly cold – just tepid water from the tap.
