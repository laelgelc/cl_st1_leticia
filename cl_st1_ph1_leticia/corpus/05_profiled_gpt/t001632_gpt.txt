We were actually a little bit late getting here because I’d been on the phone hearing all about her first day at the new job, which was apparently absolutely disastrous. Not the job itself, but everything getting there. The transport was a nightmare – everything that could go wrong did – and she was convinced she was going to make a terrible impression. After all that drama she ended up only five minutes late in the end, but of course by then she was in a complete state.

The job itself sounds really good, though. She’s the gym manager, and she’s also doing all the sports therapy treatments there. On top of that, she’ll probably be teaching Pilates as well, because she’s a trained Pilates teacher. So it’s quite a mix of responsibilities, but all the things she’s qualified for and enjoys.

It’s a very posh gym, attached to a private club, so quite a smart set‑up. The whole place sounds very upmarket, proper members’ club sort of thing, rather than your average leisure centre.
