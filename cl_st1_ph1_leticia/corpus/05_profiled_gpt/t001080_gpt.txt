I saw this interview with Jason Robinson the other day, and the interviewer asked him this really stupid question about that World Cup match where Jonah Lomu scored. He goes, “Do you ever think about that tackle you missed on Jonah Lomu?” as if he’d remember one specific missed tackle from ages ago. Robinson was really polite and said, “Yeah, sometimes I do,” but I was just sitting there thinking, why would he? It was such a daft question, and I couldn’t believe that was what they chose to ask him.
