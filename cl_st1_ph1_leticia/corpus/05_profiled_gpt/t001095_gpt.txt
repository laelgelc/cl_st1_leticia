I was woken up at about five o’clock by this persistent beeping alarm, really quite annoying. Lying there, I realised something odd: when I changed the side of my head on the pillow, it sounded noticeably louder in one ear than the other. It just kept going, so I half slept through it, drifting in and out, but every time I woke up it was still beeping away. I assumed it must be an alarm buried in someone’s suitcase or bag, maybe tucked away under something, because it just carried on regardless while I was dozing and then waking up again.
