One of our absolutely key marketing messages was always, “You have to start early.” We kept stressing that employers’ deadlines come round far sooner than students realise, and we were gradually getting better at shifting them to think ahead each year. The difficulty, from a marketing point of view, was getting across that we weren’t just a vague “advice shop” – we actually had structured ways of helping them work through really knotty career decisions. And of course, many of them stayed away precisely because they didn’t yet know what they wanted, when in fact that was the best time to come.
