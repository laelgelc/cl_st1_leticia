So we were out to dinner with three friends, just chatting about those mattress sellers, and one of my friends suddenly went, “Hang on, I think I know who you mean.” She reckoned it was this mother-and-daughter pair she knew. We went through where they were based, and it all lined up, so she got her phone out to double-check. She had a photo of the daughter on there, and it turned out the daughter had actually been her bridesmaid. It really showed what a small world it is, that they were the same people.
