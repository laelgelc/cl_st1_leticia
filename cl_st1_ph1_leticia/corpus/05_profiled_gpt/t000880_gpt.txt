Because I was the only one working, I just couldn’t cover everything on my own, so putting the second bedroom on Airbnb really kept us afloat. It started very simply, just renting out that room, but then we talked it through and realised it could actually be scaled up into a proper business. The idea was to buy places, renovate them, and then rent them out through Airbnb. That way you’ve got an income stream, and you’re not completely stuck. And one of the real advantages with Airbnb is you do have a certain amount of control over who comes in and how it all runs.
