For my responsible event management module we had both a formative and a summative assessment. The formative didn’t count for marks, it was just to get feedback, but the summative essay was basically the same task, just bigger. My formative had gone really well, so when it came to the summative I just reused it as a base. I copied and pasted what I’d already written, then added more content and developed it. I actually checked with the staff first and they said it was fine, because the formative was only emailed to tutors and never put through Turnitin, so it wouldn’t show up as self‑plagiarism.
