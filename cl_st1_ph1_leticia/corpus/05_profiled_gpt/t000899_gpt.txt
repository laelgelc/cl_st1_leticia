We’d looked at the map and just kind of assumed the way down from the top would be fairly straightforward, nothing too dramatic. Then, of course, the weather just suddenly turned on us. All this fog and cloud started rolling in and we literally couldn’t see where we were going. He just sort of set off, skiing ahead quite confidently, like he knew exactly what he was doing. I was the total opposite, edging my way across the slope really gingerly, bit by bit, not wanting to commit to anything because I just couldn’t see what was in front of me.
