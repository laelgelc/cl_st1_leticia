well for me the whole point of teaching is that you put the work in beforehand, you know, you really think through your lesson and choose proper, solid materials. If you’ve laid those foundations well, then once you’re in the classroom you’re not faffing about with whether the worksheet makes sense or if the task is clear. You can actually concentrate on guiding them through the work, helping them understand or complete the tasks, and in a way it frees you up to do the real teaching.
