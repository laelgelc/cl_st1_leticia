Yeah, they are really nice like this, all salty, aren’t they? If you ever want to do jacket potatoes quickly but still get the skin crispy, there’s a trick I use. Do them in the microwave first to save time, just until the inside’s cooked. Then coat the potato in olive oil and salt, wrap it in a paper towel, and pop it in the oven for about five minutes. That way the skin crisps up instead of staying all soft. It’s a really good tip.
