I just wouldn’t have any nonsense in the classroom, you know. There was this one lad, big brute of a kid, ended up playing for Birmingham actually. He started acting up, so I made him bend over and I gave him a whack. I know, strictly speaking, it was probably illegal even then, but I wasn’t having him running the show. I wasn’t generally violent, not going round belting kids for the sake of it or anything like that. Another teacher said to me, “You can’t do that, it’s illegal,” and I just said, “Well, go on then, report me.”
