I just find the whole situation absolutely staggering, you know. She used to come in to me every single day, moaning on about how much she did, how little she was appreciated, how badly she was paid, and how everything was so much better over at this other school down the road. In the end I just said to her, well, if you think it’s so marvellous over there, go and work there then. And I meant it. People later said I was a bit harsh, but I stood by it. I explained to them that I’ve worked in different places, I’ve seen what it can be like elsewhere, and for all its frustrations this place is actually pretty good. It’s not perfect, of course, but compared to some of the environments I’ve known, we’re quite fortunate, and I wasn’t going to sit and listen to her running it down day after day.
