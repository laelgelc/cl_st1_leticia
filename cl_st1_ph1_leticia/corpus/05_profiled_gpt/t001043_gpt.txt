There’s this brewer I know who really plays around with his beers. He’ll chuck a whole basket of orange peel into a brew just to give it a bit of a tang, that sort of thing. He’s always deciding what a particular brew needs, trying something different each time. Once he’s done messing about with it, he leaves it for a couple of days to settle before he barrels it up. And he never does massive batches either, usually just the two barrels of each variation.
