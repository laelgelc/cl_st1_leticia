well the rapids are the bit before the waterfall, where the water’s already rushing really fast. By the time you actually go over the edge, you’re being pushed along and you can’t really control where you’re going. People can get really badly hurt or even killed, mostly because it’s such a long drop and you can hit jagged rocks at the bottom, or just because the force of the water is so strong. Even high diving into water can break bones if you hit it badly, so a waterfall’s much worse.
