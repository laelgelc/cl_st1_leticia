But it turned out he was ringing about a hospital appointment, and that was really the first inkling we had that anything serious was going on.

It all happened incredibly quickly. They’d been on holiday and he’d just had what seemed like a sore throat, nothing that would make you think of anything more sinister. And then from that point it just galloped. Within about three months he’d gone from that sore throat to being desperately ill and then dying.

By the end he was absolutely skeletal, because he simply couldn’t swallow. It was that rapid, horrible decline where you can almost see someone disappearing in front of you.
