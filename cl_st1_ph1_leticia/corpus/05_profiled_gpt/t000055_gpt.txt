We were in the car park and I had him on that extendable lead, which I really don’t like. I hadn’t managed to clip it in time and he suddenly spotted three dogs that were off the lead. The second he saw them he just bolted. Because it was that horrible extendable thing, I had no proper control, and he yanked me straight off my feet. I went headfirst into the side of a car, literally slammed into it, and then he carried on running. By the time I’d hit the car he was already off, still charging towards the other dogs, and I was just left there in a heap.
