It really depresses me when I read these stories about young people and sex and pregnancy. So often the girls don’t actually seem to enjoy sex; they’re doing it because the boys want it, not because they genuinely want to themselves. And both boys and girls are learning about sex from the internet and pornography, which gives this completely unrealistic picture of what sex is like. That whole portrayal is just so unhelpful for girls and for their chances of ever having a healthy sex life. You get the sense that some of these girls are having sex under a kind of duress, going along with it, and not really enjoying it at all.
