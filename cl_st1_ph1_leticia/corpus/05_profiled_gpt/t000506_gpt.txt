But passports are actually really important for proving who you are, for jobs and things. They’re even more important than a birth certificate or a driving licence, because they’re much harder to get and they show that you live here properly, permanently. When I was trying to get teaching work it was really difficult to prove who I was without a passport, and that’s why I ended up renewing it.
