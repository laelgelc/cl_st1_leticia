I remember walking up those stairs absolutely terrified, just bracing myself for this massive jock to come charging after me and punch me, because I’d literally just threatened him. I was kind of waiting for the impact with every step, convinced I’d pushed it way too far. Then I got to the top, turned around, and he just wasn’t there. Nothing happened at all. Later, outside, everything had gone a bit feral – my mate was screaming at this hot dog vendor and we just went on this ridiculous little rampage.
