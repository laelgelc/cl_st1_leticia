so they basically completely redid the whole car parking scheme at work because they'd sold off some land and a couple of buildings, and with that went some of the parking as well. now it's all based on how far away you live. if you live within three miles you can't even apply for a permit, you're just expected to use alternative transport, and if you live further away you can apply. 

at the start they were really strict, only giving permits to people who fitted these really tight criteria, and then they realised loads of spaces were sitting empty every day. obviously that wound people up, seeing all these unused bays when they were struggling to park. so at Christmas they reviewed it all and decided to issue more permits. they’ve sort of accepted that now and then someone with a permit might still not get a space, because some permit holders only use the car park occasionally, but they’d rather that than have empty spaces.
