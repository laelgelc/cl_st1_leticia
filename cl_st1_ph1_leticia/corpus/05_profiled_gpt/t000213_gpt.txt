In a conversation, people forget the finer details and just remember how they felt, often becoming defensive rather than really thinking things through. With an email, everything you’ve said is there in black and white, so she can go back, reread it, and properly chew it over in her own time. That’s why it’s so important to get the wording right, to be very careful about how you phrase things, so the message comes across clearly and thoughtfully and gives her space to reflect.
