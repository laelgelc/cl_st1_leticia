It was such a good sleep-over, like properly classic. We were doing all the usual stuff, drawing on each other’s faces and everything, and then we had this big “midnight feast” that was basically just loads of sweets we’d got from the Spa. My mum was not impressed that we were eating loads of sweets after we’d already brushed our teeth, she kept telling us off for that. My mum and dad were asleep upstairs but every few hours they’d come down and be like “can you be quieter?” and I’d be all “sorry, sorry”.
