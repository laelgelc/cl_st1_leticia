I always say I haven’t many real friends, largely because my career took me into rather odd, specialist jobs, always moving on. I was never really involved with the WRAF as such, and I preferred it that way, as I was not at all keen on the welfare side of things. The people I knew best were four very experienced flight lieutenants, flight commanders, when I suddenly found myself in charge of WRAF officer training. I was really a complete beginner there, coming from intelligence, and they kept me firmly on the rails and we had a tremendous amount of fun together.
