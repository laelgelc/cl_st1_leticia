I actually went to see a life coach recently, because I'm trying to get a new career going and felt a bit stuck. She said that when people think about careers, they usually narrow themselves down far too quickly, and that really limits what they can imagine doing. Her suggestion was to do something completely different: instead of being “sensible”, write a list of absolute dream jobs, even if they’re wildly unrealistic. The point isn’t that you’ll actually do them, but that it starts you thinking more creatively. I remember saying something like being George Clooney’s personal secretary, which is obviously ridiculous, but that was exactly her point. It’s just a way to begin that process of thinking out of the box.
