yesterday at school I had this really stark example of what I was talking about. My class had been properly misbehaving, and I ended up having to keep them in over the time when I was meant to be on break duty. I couldn’t be in two places at once, so I went and asked one of the Year 6 teachers if he’d cover my duty for me.

He just said yes straightaway, no complaint, no sighing, nothing. Just, “yeah, sure,” and off he went, out into the cold, while I stayed in with my lot.

And I was standing there thinking, if it had been the other way round, I know exactly what I’d have been like. I’d have been all, “fine, okay, I’ll do it, fine, fine, yes, fine, yeah, fine, no worries,” but inside I’d be really hacked off about missing my coffee and having to go out in the freezing cold. It really highlighted how instinctively nice he was, and how grudging I probably would’ve been.
