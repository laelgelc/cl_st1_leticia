No, fifth of January, not December. I just saw part of the garage door open, so I called out, “Is that your garage?” and this chap appeared from round the back. He was up there fixing his roof, where the storms had damaged it. I asked him if it was his garage and if he had any jump leads. He said they were actually closed and he didn’t have any leads, but he’d got this battery pack. So he brought that over for me and jump-started the car. It absolutely saved me, really, because I’d have been completely stuck otherwise.
