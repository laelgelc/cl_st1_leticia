I used to collect plants quite seriously, you know, and press them and then try to identify them properly. I’d go out on fieldwork with someone along the little streams, looking for snails and things, and while we were doing that the local people would often stop and talk to us, very friendly, just curious about what we were up to. It all felt quite safe then. But later on, some of the Africans I knew – our driver and my lab assistant – told me I really mustn’t walk about on my own anymore, that it simply wasn’t safe.
