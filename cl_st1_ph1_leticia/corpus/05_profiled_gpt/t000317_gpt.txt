I think, for me, the really fundamental point in all of this is that people are just people. Wherever you go in the world, most human beings basically want the same things for themselves and their families: safety, warmth, comfort, a roof over their heads, enough food, some sort of community, relationships, and that feeling of belonging to something bigger than just themselves. 

And yet there are so many things set up to divide us – religion, nationality, gender, all these labels. It feels very much like a “divide and conquer” strategy, doesn’t it? While poor people are busy fighting each other over these divisions, they’re not looking at the really important issues.
