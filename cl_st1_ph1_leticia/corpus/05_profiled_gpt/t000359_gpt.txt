I was saying that the whole point of intelligence agencies is to gather information and stop things happening before they do, not to go round killing people. I’d had this big argument with someone who insisted that agents should just go abroad and shoot anyone they thought was a threat. I told him I couldn’t back that at all, because I’m a pacifist and I don’t believe anyone deserves to be killed. He then pushed this hypothetical at me – like, what if someone was in the street just shooting people, what would I do? And I said I’d run away. I wouldn’t fight them, I wouldn’t shoot them, because I’m just not that kind of person who could do that.
