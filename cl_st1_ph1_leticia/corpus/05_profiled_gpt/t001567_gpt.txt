We were walking along just after Christmas, you see, and he turns to me and says, “You look a bit down.” So I told him straight, it’s all these jobs round the house, half-finished or not even started, that are getting to me. Everywhere I look there’s something needing doing, and it just weighs on your mind, doesn’t it. We had a bit of a talk about it, and in the end we said, right, we’ll make a proper list. We’ll go through and mark which ones I can still manage and which I can’t. Then for the ones I can’t do, we’ll get somebody in and pay to have them done. We decided we’d start with the toilet, and get a plumber to come in the morning to have a look at it.
