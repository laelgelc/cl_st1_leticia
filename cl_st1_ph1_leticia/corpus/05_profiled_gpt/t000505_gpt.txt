I’d actually heard about those public beheadings before, from one of my patients’ daughters. She was a young air hostess, and she’d gone out there on a trip. She told me she’d gone down to the market to buy some gold, as you do, and suddenly there was all this commotion. Then, right in front of her, a woman was brought out and beheaded there in the marketplace. It absolutely horrified her, really shook her up, and she said afterwards it had unsettled her so much she never wanted to go back there again.
