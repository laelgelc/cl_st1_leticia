He goes through it all really carefully, step by step, which I like. For example, he had this car with a grinding noise coming from the back axle. He started by unbolting the axle and taking it apart so you could see exactly what was going on. Then he showed you how to spot the chipped gears that were causing the trouble. After that he took a circlip off so he could remove the damaged parts properly. Once he’d got the replacement bit, he put it all back together, fitted the new part and the circlip again.
