I remember this David Attenborough programme where he went into this cave absolutely full of guano, like mountains of the stuff, and it was just heaving with bats. He was standing there explaining how their sonar works, saying how they can fly incredibly close to your head and never actually hit you because they’re so accurate. And as he’s saying all this, you can see all these bats whizzing past his face – and then one of them just goes straight into his face anyway, completely ruining the point.
