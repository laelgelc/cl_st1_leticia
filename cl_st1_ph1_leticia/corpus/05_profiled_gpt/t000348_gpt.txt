They really are difficult, broccoli and cauliflower, so we’re hoping the greenhouse will help us manage them better. What I’ve found is that going round garden centres and especially proper kitchen gardens gives you such good ideas. You see how people actually use their space, like planting rows of beetroot and then putting something else, like carrots, in between to save room. It’s all those little tricks. And the Women’s Institute gardens are brilliant too, for seeing what you can grow and how they’ve laid everything out.
