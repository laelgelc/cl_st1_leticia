He was really, really restless near the end, and we honestly thought he was going to go. Mum and I just kept singing to him, over and over, trying to soothe him. I rang my sisters and said, “I think this is it, you need to come,” because it really felt like he was about to die. We just carried on singing, almost willing him to hang on until they got there. It was all the same songs in a loop, just to keep him going, keep him with us, until everyone could be there.
