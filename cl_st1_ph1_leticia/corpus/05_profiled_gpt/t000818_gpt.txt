I suppose part of it is just that the West had more habitable terrain, you know, places where it was easier to settle and grow things, so things evolved more quickly there. But then once we’d evolved and got all this power and technology, we went off to other countries and enslaved people and imposed our religion and culture on them.

Like with the missionaries in New Zealand – they went to the Maoris and taught them about the seventh day, saying you must rest, lay down your weapons, don’t fight on that day. And then, once they knew the Maoris would be unarmed and resting, they used that to massacre them and take over their land. That’s how they went there and took it, using religion as a kind of cover.

I think they probably did something similar in America, though I can’t quite remember the details, but I know that’s definitely what happened in New Zealand. So they’re there preaching the word of God, but really they’re acting on behalf of the invading, pillaging armies.
