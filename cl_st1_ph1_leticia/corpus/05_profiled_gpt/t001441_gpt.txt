Years ago I lost my phone when me and Zara were both working in the same shopping centre. We’d synced our breaks so we could meet up, and the whole plan depended on us being able to text each other. Then my phone went missing, and of course that was the day she decided to change her break time. Because I didn’t have a phone anymore, and she’d shifted her break, we just completely missed each other. We were in the same place, working in the same centre, and still didn’t manage to meet up for forty-eight hours.
