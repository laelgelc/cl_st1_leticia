I used to absolutely love going to car boot sales, but they can be a bit heartbreaking as well. You end up putting out all these things that once meant loads to you, stuff that was really expensive at the time, or that you’d saved up for or begged for at Christmas. Then people come along and haggle you right down, and you let them go for hardly anything. I even sold my two china dolls there, which I’d really treasured, and it felt a bit sad.
