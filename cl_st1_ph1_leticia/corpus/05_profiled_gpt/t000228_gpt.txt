Living abroad’s really changed how I look at friendships. It feels a bit like plate spinning now – I’ve got all these different friends and family members, and I have to consciously keep each plate going by getting in touch, otherwise it just wobbles and falls. It’s odd though, because even here in England it can be the same. You can live really close to someone and still not see them. Like Mum, she lives just round the corner from someone she hasn’t actually visited since Christmas.
