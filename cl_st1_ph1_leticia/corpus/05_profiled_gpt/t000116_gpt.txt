it’s actually an Indonesian rendang, that’s probably my favourite curry ever. I do it with about a kilo of beef steak, and the first thing I do is really tenderise the meat and cook it off on quite a high heat, then take it out. The sauce is really easy, it’s a bit like doing a green curry. I use onions, red chillies, and then ginger or galangal, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, and tamarind. It ends up a bit sweeter than some curries, and I put in loads of cinnamon, plus cardamom to give it that lemony flavour, and quite a bit of clove.
