I really didn’t fancy going on that trip to Wales at first – four days just sounded a bit too long for me, so I did try to wriggle out of it. But I’d enjoyed helping out before, and in the end it turned out I was the only one with a vehicle that could take four people and four bikes. So we worked out a way to do it: all four bikes went upright on the roof, properly fixed on, looking a bit like one of those professional race support cars.
