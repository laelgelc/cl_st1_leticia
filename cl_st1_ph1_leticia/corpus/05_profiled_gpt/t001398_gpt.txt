I’d just got back from a month travelling round New Zealand, and it was literally right after Mum and Dad had separated. I’d been travelling for about thirty hours with no sleep, so by the time I walked through the door I was absolutely shattered, completely done in. 

Dad had been on his own and I think he was really lonely, so as soon as I got home he just launched into everything that had been going on. He was talking a mile a minute, really full-on, like he’d been saving it all up for when I arrived. 

I just couldn’t cope with it at that point. I was so tired I felt like I couldn’t actually speak, like my brain had just shut down. It was all a bit overwhelming, trying to take in what had happened between them while I was that exhausted.
