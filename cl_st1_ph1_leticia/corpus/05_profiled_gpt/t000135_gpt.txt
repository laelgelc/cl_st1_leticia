So I get there and it’s a smoking hotel, like properly reeks of smoke, my room just stank. I’m thinking I need some fresh air and something to eat, so I head out, and it’s just this empty industrial estate. No life, no people, nothing.

I’m walking and walking, and there’s literally nowhere to go. I pass this petrol station and for a second I’m like, do I just grab some crisps and chocolate and call that dinner? But I really wanted a proper meal, so I kept going.

By this point I’ve walked like two, three miles, still through all this residential and commercial stuff, and there’s just no proper shops anywhere. No high street, nothing that looks like somewhere you’d sit down and eat.

Then finally, out of nowhere, there’s this one Italian restaurant, just sitting there on its own. No other restaurants, no cafés, just this lone Italian place in the middle of everything. It felt so random after all that walking, but it was literally the only option I’d found.
