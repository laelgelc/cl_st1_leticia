On my very first Avon round I decided I’d do it properly, so with every book I dropped through a door I tucked in a little note. I explained I was just starting out, told them exactly which day I’d be back to collect the book and when the next one would be delivered. I said if they didn’t want to be disturbed, just leave the book outside. When I went back, some had left the books out with notes saying “no more, thank you,” and others asked me to keep leaving them, even though they didn’t want anything that time.
