In Norway there isn’t really much incentive to work super hard or be especially innovative, because there’s no real sense of meritocracy or extra reward if you do. It’s much more about everyone fitting into and serving the system than standing out or pushing ahead. That whole mindset is so strong that someone I know actually chose to leave Norway to study in America and England, at least partly for that reason. They wanted an environment where creativity and entrepreneurial behaviour would be recognised and properly rewarded.
