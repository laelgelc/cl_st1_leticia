yeah it was actually a bit dangerous, to be honest, and I was already really wound up from that first typhoon. Then the following week, my first proper day at the new school, another one hits – Typhoon Tembin this time. I remember just thinking, this has got to be some kind of joke.

I tried to get a taxi because the weather was just insane again, but there were none at all, nothing running. So I had to walk it, about half an hour in that really heavy wind and rain, just getting absolutely battered the whole way. I didn’t even properly know where I was going, so I kept having to stop random people in the street, asking for directions, shouting over the wind.

By the time I finally got to the school I was completely drenched, like soaked right through everything. I was kind of hoping they’d take one look at me and be like, “Right, let’s get you some dry clothes or something,” you know, just a bit of help after that walk.
