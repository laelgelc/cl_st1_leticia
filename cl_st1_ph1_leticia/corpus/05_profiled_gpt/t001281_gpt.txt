I really don’t think this is about your personality, or that you’re somehow to blame because you don’t have some kind of spiritual outlook or whatever. People love to make it sound like it’s all inside you, but there’s loads of stuff outside you that piles on and makes you feel like this. It’s not as simple as “this is just who I am now”.  

And I know it feels like this is permanent and you’re going to feel like this forever, and that really sucks, but it isn’t actually permanent, even if it feels that way right now.  

When you were back home over summer, did it feel any easier because you didn’t have all these responsibilities hanging over you? Or was it just stressful in a different way because of your family, like them putting pressure on you or making you feel bad?
