Well there’s this Glaswegian woman, see, and she goes to the doctor and says, “Doctor, every time my husband comes home drunk he hits me. What can I do to stop it?” And the doctor says, “Right, when you hear him coming up the path, you go to the sink, fill your mouth with water, and just keep swishing it round and round. Don’t swallow it, just keep swilling it.” So that’s what she does, every time he comes in drunk she’s there, mouth full of water, swishing away.
