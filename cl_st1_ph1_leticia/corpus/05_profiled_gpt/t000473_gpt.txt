I was sat in this navy office, like I was saying earlier, and I’m always the only girl in there, so I ended up just staring out the window at everyone walking past in Peterborough. And honestly, they’re rough as fuck. The women especially – tottering along in these stupidly high heels, hardly any clothes on, like what they’d wear for work is basically nothing, and their hair’s all bleached and wrecked. I was just thinking, God, that’s vile. 

But then I was like, that’s what I grew up with, that was just normal where I’m from. It’s mad, because I’ve been living round here for three years now and it’s made me soft, like I’ve got used to a different standard of what people look like and how they behave. Really, Peterborough’s the rough standard I should still be used to. 

Where I am now, yeah, you get rough people, but you’d never see the really extreme stuff, not to that level. I actually feel quite safe here in comparison, which is weird when I think about where I started out.
