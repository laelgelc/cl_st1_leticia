There's this horrible bit just after the roundabout on my way to work where the road suddenly narrows down to basically the width of a single car, like a proper funnel. Then just after a pedestrian crossing it opens out again and there's loads of space. What usually happens is most drivers will just hang back a bit and let you go through that narrow section on the bike, and then once you're past the crossing and the road widens, they'll overtake you there where it's much safer and less stressful.
