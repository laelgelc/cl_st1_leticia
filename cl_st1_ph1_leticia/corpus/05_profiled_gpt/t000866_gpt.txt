it's a little village right on the eastern edge of Snowdonia, and the name is something like “St Michael’s in the valley” – that’s the sense of it anyway. I went back there quite recently, actually, to try and track down where the old farms had been, the places my lot would have worked. There’s a tiny church by the river, dates from the twelfth or thirteenth century, absolutely beautiful in a very simple way. Inside the church there’s this extraordinary statement, carved or written there, recording that my great-grandfather was born in 1821, which rather stopped me in my tracks when I saw it.
