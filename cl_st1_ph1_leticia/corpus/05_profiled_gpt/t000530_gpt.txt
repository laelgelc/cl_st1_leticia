I’ve got loads of old black‑and‑white photographs from my mum’s side, especially my grandmother’s family, and I’m absolutely fascinated by my grandmother’s father. He was this really dashing, adventurous figure, a ship’s engineering captain who worked on a big cruise liner, and he was involved during the First World War as well. It just feels like boats and cruising are in the blood, really, because it runs through my mum and my grandmother too. That whole side of the family has this strong connection with the sea and ships. I’m definitely going to go back and see her again before Christmas.
