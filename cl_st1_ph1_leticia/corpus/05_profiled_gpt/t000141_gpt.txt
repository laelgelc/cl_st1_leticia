I had a bit of a panic at the petrol station with my new car the other day. I was filling it up and it just kept going past fifty litres and the pump still wasn’t clicking off. With my old car, the tank was only forty-five litres and I usually only ever put about forty litres in, so I’m stood there thinking, this can’t be right. It felt really wrong, like something must be up with the pump or the car, so I bottled it and stopped it at around fifty-two litres. I was worried I was paying for loads of fuel I didn’t actually need.
