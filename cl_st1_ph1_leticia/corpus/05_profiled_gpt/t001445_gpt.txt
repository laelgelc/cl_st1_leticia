You can't just let things slip; that was one of the striking things in Blair’s first year. People like Blunkett genuinely looked exhausted, simply because so much work was being pushed through in such a short period of time. Whatever one thinks of it, you could see the effort. By contrast, the current lot are hyperactive but don’t seem to produce anything positive or properly thought through. In those early Blair years there really was an attempt to bring everyone in via this stakeholder approach, to involve all the relevant parties before decisions were actually made.
