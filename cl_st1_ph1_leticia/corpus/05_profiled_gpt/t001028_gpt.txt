I remember just after New Year, standing at the bus stop in about minus seventeen, absolutely freezing, thinking, “Oh, the bus will be here in five minutes.” I waited, and waited… more than half an hour went by and nothing turned up. Then it finally dawned on me: it was Saturday, and of course the timetable was completely different. I’d been shivering there for nothing. I just thought, never again. I really have to remember to check what day it is before I go out and stand at a bus stop in that kind of cold.
