In that tiny courtroom there were literally four of us, so it was completely obvious where everyone’s eyes were going. You couldn’t really pretend you hadn’t noticed, because there weren’t enough people for it to be ambiguous. That kind of blatant staring is the sort of thing you expect in pubs or clubs after a few drinks, not in more formal situations. In an office, or anywhere that’s meant to be professional, you just wouldn’t behave like that. And really, you’d assume a court would be the same, but apparently not in this case.
