So we roll up there with our two sports cars and the caravan, feeling rather pleased with ourselves, and then get this absolute bombshell. They want this huge deposit off us, basically in case we disappear with all the company’s property. We’re standing there thinking, “You what?” Then they tell us we’ve got seven days either to stump up the money or get properly registered. And just as we’re digesting that, we find out the law’s changed: instead of six months, we now have to stay a full year before we can sell the cars.
