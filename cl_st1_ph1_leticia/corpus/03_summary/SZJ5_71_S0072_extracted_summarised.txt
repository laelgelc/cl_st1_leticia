The speaker describes a July trip to Greece taken a couple of years earlier because Lou had a placement, saying the dry heat was still too hot and exhausting, recounts being advised by hotel and shop staff not to visit in August and to come before October because everything closes then, explains that businesses in Pefkohori shut at the beginning to middle of October as owners return to Chalkidiki, and fondly depicts Pefkohori as a small, beautiful coastal town with a lovely square, many mini supermarkets, and children happily carrying cold beer in free cool bags down to the beach.
