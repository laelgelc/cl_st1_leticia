The speaker says that if people do not value education they will not accept it, that education cannot be forced and lack of achievement is often due to individuals not taking the opportunity, giving examples of non-academic people who have achieved a lot and intelligent people who do not care, and concludes that while part of the situation is the government's fault, people growing up on a council estate with non-working parents will not achieve in life and that this is not the government's fault but the individuals'.
