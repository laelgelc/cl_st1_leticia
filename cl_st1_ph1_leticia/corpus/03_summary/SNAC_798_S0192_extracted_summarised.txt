The speaker describes a second, less eventful horse-riding experience where they mostly rode in a line while enjoying beautiful scenery with blue hyacinth macaw parrots and other wildlife, contrasting it with a first, amazing and sketchy ride in Ecuador that lasted four or five hours up and down a mountain to a waterfall, ending with all four riders cantering and then galloping through the streets on very well-trained horses in a way they feel wouldn’t be allowed elsewhere.
