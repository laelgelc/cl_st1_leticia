The speaker explains that a former schoolmate asked for help because her fiancé wants his mother in Essex to make several types of cakes (fruit, chocolate biscuit, and sponge), so the speaker proposed a solution where the mother provides the fruit cake early for icing, the other cakes are delivered the day before to be iced and used as cutting cakes with polystyrene tiers added on top, noting this is the only practical way to manage the timing and stacking, and recounts that the schoolmate responded by calling the speaker amazing and being relieved it was possible.
