The speaker explains that they won a goldfish at a fair, brought it home in a plastic bag and kept it in a bowl on the sideboard, later gave it to their uncle with a garden fish pond when they moved and did not want to take the goldfish, describes how the cat would pretend not to care and then try unsuccessfully to catch the fish when no one was looking, and concludes that the pond was the best place for the fish and that keeping a goldfish in a small round bowl is cruel.
