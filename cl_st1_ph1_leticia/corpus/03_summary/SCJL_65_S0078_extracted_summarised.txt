The speaker explains that the group televising MineCon in Las Vegas wore "occupy Minecraft" T-shirts and discussed bringing the Occupy movement into Minecraft, noting that the connected Minecraft community incorporates real-world events such as Occupy and Steve Jobs’ death—illustrated by a large in-game "RIP Steve Jobs" sign and iPod build—and concludes that news can enter the Minecraft world, which is one way they considered the concept of cyber provincialism, along with using it to get a community to look at different cultures.
