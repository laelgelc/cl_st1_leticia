The speaker describes being about twelve vehicles away from a devastating landslide in Nepal and reflects on how close in time they might have been to it, recounting the sight of a vanished mountainside with roofs sticking out and knowing people had died there, then goes on to describe another horrific experience in Nepal where they unknowingly travelled into a recently flooded area that had become a refugee camp with aid tents, dead buffalo in the water, and extremely difficult travel that took about twelve hours.
