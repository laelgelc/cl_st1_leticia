The speaker explains that their favourite curry is an Indonesian rendang made with about a kilo of beef steak that they tenderise and cook on high heat before removing, and then they make an easy sauce similar to a green curry using onions, red chillies, ginger or galangal, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, and tamarind, noting that it is a bit sweeter and includes a lot of cinnamon, cardamom for a lemony flavour, and quite a bit of clove.
