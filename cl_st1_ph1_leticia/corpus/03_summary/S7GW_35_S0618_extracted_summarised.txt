The speaker says that, for them, the fundamental point is that all people are simply people, that humans everywhere want the best for themselves and their families, primarily in the form of safety, warmth, comfort, shelter, food, community, relationships, and a sense of belonging to something larger, and they add that many things divide people and that, in their view, this division functions as "divide and conquer" while poor people fight each other instead of focusing on more important issues.
