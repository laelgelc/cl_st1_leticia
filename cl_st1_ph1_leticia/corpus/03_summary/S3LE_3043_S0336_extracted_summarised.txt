The speaker explains that due to land and building sales at their workplace, the car parking scheme was completely renegotiated so that people living within three miles cannot apply for a permit and must use alternative transport, while those living further away can apply; initially permits were given only to those meeting strict criteria, but after noticing many empty spaces and resulting frustration, management reviewed the situation at Christmas and decided to issue more permits, accepting that some people might occasionally not get a space because certain permit holders only use the car park infrequently.
