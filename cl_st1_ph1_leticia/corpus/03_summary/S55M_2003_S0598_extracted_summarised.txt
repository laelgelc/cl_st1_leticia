The speaker explains that for their responsible event management module they had both formative (ungraded, feedback-only) and summative assessments, and because the formative task was effectively half of the summative, they successfully reused and expanded their well-marked formative essay—copying, pasting, adding content, and confirming with staff that this was acceptable—since the formative had only been emailed to tutors and not uploaded to Turnitin, which otherwise would have flagged the summative as self-plagiarism.
