I like using my glasses as fashion statements as well as for seeing properly. I’ve got white ones in both strengths, and various other types in both strengths too. There’s this really large pair I ended up with after a bit of swapping at a dinner party, and I’ve also got a cheap market pair that turned out to be expensive Specsavers frames in my strength. Because of the nature of my work I’m forever scratching, damaging, breaking, or just losing them. That’s why I don’t mind picking up different styles and colours – they’re practical, but they’re also fun to wear and I treat them as part of what I’m wearing.
