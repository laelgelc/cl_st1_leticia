On my eleventh or twelfth birthday, I had two school friends over who were absolutely obsessed with EastEnders. There was this big special episode on where Sonia gives birth, and they were adamant that we had to watch it. So we all ended up in front of the TV – me, my two friends, and my mum – watching this half-hour of childbirth. I’d never seen EastEnders before, so it was just this really distressing, slightly grim blur that didn’t mean anything to me at all. When it finished, my mum just went, “That’s EastEnders, see what you’ve been missing?” in this joking way. I always describe it, very ironically, as an excellent eleventh birthday.
