At work I was asking people for ideas, and loads of them had done all these little trips. They were saying you can actually do weekends away really cheaply if you don’t go to the obvious big tourist places. Instead you pick somewhere a bit more obscure, and you can get flights for like forty, fifty quid. Then you just stay in a little B&B when you’re there. So it doesn’t have to be this massive expensive thing at all, you can just nip off for a couple of days.
