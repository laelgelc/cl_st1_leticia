Oh my God, that reminds me of when I lost my phone years ago, back when Zara and I were both working in the same shopping centre. We used to be so organised, like, “Right, our breaks are at the same time, we’ll just meet up then,” and it all depended on having our phones. Then I lost mine, and of course that was the day everything went wrong. Zara had changed her break time and I had no way of knowing, no way of contacting her at all. We completely missed each other and then somehow didn’t see each other for forty‑eight hours.
