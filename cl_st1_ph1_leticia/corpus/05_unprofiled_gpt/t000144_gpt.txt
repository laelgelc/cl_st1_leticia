so basically because they’ve sold off bits of land and a couple of buildings at work, they had to totally redo the parking scheme. they brought in this new rule that if you live within three miles of the place you’re not even allowed to apply for a permit, so you’re just expected to use public transport or whatever. only people who live further away are allowed to apply for a permit now.

when they first brought it in they were really strict about who actually got one, so only people who met these tight criteria were issued permits. but then they started noticing that loads of spaces were sitting empty, and obviously that wound people up because they were struggling while there were all these unused spots.

so over Christmas they had another look at the whole thing and decided to issue more permits. they’ve kind of accepted that it means occasionally someone might turn up and not get a space, because some of the people with permits don’t use the car park every day anyway.
