I just feel like, in comparison, beer and wine are actually more natural than all those really processed foods full of weird fats and palm oil. Those ingredients, and a lot of vegetable oils, just seem really inflammatory and kind of unnatural to me. When I first went on that psoriasis diet and cut all that stuff out, my symptoms basically disappeared. But then as soon as I started eating those gluten-free biscuits again, it all came back, even though they were gluten-free and dairy-free. So it’s not just about gluten or dairy, it’s all the processed rubbish in them.
