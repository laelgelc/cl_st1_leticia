there was this story about a university student who was working in the library, and this boy wanted to ask her out but it was a silent place, so he couldn’t really talk to her. instead, he did this really sweet thing: he drew a sort of WhatsApp-style conversation on paper, with little message bubbles and everything. in it, he basically gave her a choice: either go for a coffee with him, or decline with some kind of humorous excuse. he then gave her this hand-drawn “chat” as his way of asking her out, because they had to keep quiet in the library.
