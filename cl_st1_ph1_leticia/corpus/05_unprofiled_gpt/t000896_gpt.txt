They’d all decided to go up the ski lift, but none of them really knew how you were meant to get on or off it properly. They managed to get on, but when they tried to get off halfway up they just sort of fell in a heap. Then they realised how steep it actually was from there, and it looked really frightening. The woman set off and, to start with, she looked absolutely brilliant, like she knew exactly what she was doing. But in reality she couldn’t really ski at all, so after a bit she completely lost control and ended up falling.
