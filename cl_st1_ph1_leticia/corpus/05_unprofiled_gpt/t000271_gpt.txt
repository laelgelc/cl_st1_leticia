Ray was really the one who got me going with the bell-ringing, he was so helpful, but he didn’t have any close family round here. In the end he moved down to Winchester, because that’s where his nearest relatives are, and of course there’s the cathedral there as well. Another bloke who helped me was a fireman, but he’s got three jobs on the go, so he’s often busy and not always around. Then there was a doctor who helped too, and he knew this retired chap from our maths department. That retired colleague actually comes out walking with us now. I was saying to him I wanted to do something really different in retirement, not just sit in the pub or watch things.
