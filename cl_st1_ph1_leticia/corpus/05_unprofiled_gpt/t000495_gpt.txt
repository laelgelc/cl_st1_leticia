I remember when we used to go to Norwich, there was just the one Match of the Day camera there, and that was it. No commentary box or anything like that, just this basic setup. We’d rush back home afterwards to watch it on the telly and actually find out who had won, because you couldn’t really tell properly when you were there. The whole thing was filmed from what was basically a tent, a sort of makeshift shelter, and when the wind blew the picture would sway about. It was like being at sea, the whole image rocking from side to side.
