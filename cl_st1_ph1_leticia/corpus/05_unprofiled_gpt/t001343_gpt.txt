For a couple of months nothing came to the feeder at all, and I was really disappointed. I wondered if it was because there were so many cats going through the garden. Then a great tit turned up that had babies. It started bringing them to the feeder, which was lovely. At first it brought two fledglings, then the next day there were three, and after that four of them. It was feeding them directly from the feeder, and you could see they were just fledging.
