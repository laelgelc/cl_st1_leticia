Yesterday this chap came past and I stopped him and said, “Are you all right with that then?” and he said, “Yeah, that’s fine.” As I was taking his address he said, “You won’t know it, it’s in Fradley.” I said, “Well I lived in Fradley,” and he went, “Did you?” So I told him I grew up there, on [that particular road], from when I was about five, and stayed there until I moved away in my twenties. We ended up comparing notes about the area and the schools. I explained I’d actually gone out of catchment for secondary school, so our paths hadn’t crossed back then, but it was funny realising we had all those local connections in common.
