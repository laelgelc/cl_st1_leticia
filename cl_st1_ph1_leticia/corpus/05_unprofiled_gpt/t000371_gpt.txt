In interviews, when they ask, “Why do you want to work for this company?”, I never say what I’m actually thinking. I just come out with those standard, generic things. I’ll say I’ve researched the company, and I’m really impressed by whatever I happened to spot on their website that morning. Sometimes I’ll pick something like their employee volunteering scheme and act as if that’s a huge draw for me. It’s basically all made up on the day, tailored to whatever they’ve put online.
