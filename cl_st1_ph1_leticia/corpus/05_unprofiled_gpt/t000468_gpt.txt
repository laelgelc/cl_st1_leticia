My daughter used to work in this pub, right, and they’d tell her her hours were seven till eleven. But it never really was eleven, because once the customers were still in, the bar wouldn’t close till it absolutely had to. Then she had to stay on after that, clearing up, sorting everything out. So she was there later than she was meant to be, every time. It was unfair the way they did it, really. But she actually liked the job, so she’d just stick with it because of that.
