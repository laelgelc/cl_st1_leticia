I kept being woken up at about five o’clock by this persistent beeping alarm, just going on and on. Lying there, I noticed something odd: when I turned my head on the pillow, it was much louder in one ear than the other. It carried on for ages, so I just assumed it must be an alarm buried in someone’s suitcase or bag, and I sort of drifted in and out of sleep with it still going in the background. When I finally woke up properly, it was still beeping away, exactly the same relentless noise.
