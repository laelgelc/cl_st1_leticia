And what really tickled me was that, in all of this, he associated my mother in the conversation but never once mentioned the woman he was actually married to at the time. I just found that so funny. Then, while we were still there, this other man came in and my dad didn’t recognise him at all. It was his own brother, but he just referred to him as this really kind man who brought him water. I ended up having to explain who everyone was, and I got the sense they thought I was only visiting for money.
