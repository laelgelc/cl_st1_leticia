He does all sorts of clever little tweaks with his beers. For example, he’ll add a whole basket of orange peel into a brew just to give it that nice tang. He’s forever experimenting, really, deciding what a particular brew seems to need, then letting it sit for a couple of days before he barrels it up. And he only ever does small batches – usually just the two barrels of any one variation – so each one’s a bit of a limited edition, in a way.
