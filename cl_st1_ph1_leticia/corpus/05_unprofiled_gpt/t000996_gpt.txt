well yeah the way it works is they don’t just sit on your money, they take a cut first in admin fees, so every time you move it somewhere else you get hit again, which is why you don’t really want to keep changing providers. then with what’s left they put it on the stock exchange, spread out over lots of different things – some high-risk, some medium, some low-risk. some of those will lose money, some will gain, some will just sit there, and what you end up with is the average return across the lot.
