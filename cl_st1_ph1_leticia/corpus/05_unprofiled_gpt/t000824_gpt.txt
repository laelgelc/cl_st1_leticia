My view is that if you can’t present a business case in ten minutes, you haven’t really thought it through. Of course, there are situations that are more complex and genuinely need more time, but as an opener it should still be possible to get the core of it across in about ten minutes. To illustrate what I mean, I’ve got to speak about the village traffic problems and the new Tesco planning application. That sort of thing really forces you to boil it down to the essentials.
