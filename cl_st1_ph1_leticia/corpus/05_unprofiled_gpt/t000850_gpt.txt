They had this Colgate stand at the show, and they were handing out these cloth Colgate bags with bits in them—free toothpaste, a toothbrush, and some other things. I ended up giving mine to someone as a Christmas present. It actually gave her an idea: she suddenly thought she could use that sort of thing herself, you know, having a stand at agricultural or similar shows. It just hadn’t occurred to her before, but seeing what Colgate did really made her consider that approach.
