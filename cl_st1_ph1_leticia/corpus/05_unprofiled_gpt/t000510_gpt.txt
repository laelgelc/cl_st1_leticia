I’d texted her saying I’d give her a ring the next day, planning to call at about half past two. Then, half an hour before that, she rang me instead. She said, “I’ve just got home and I’ve got to go straight back out, I won’t be in at half past two.” Then she asked if I was in that night. I said no, I wasn’t – I mentioned something like Avon – and said we’d speak tomorrow instead. And she just went, “I’ve got loads to tell you.”
