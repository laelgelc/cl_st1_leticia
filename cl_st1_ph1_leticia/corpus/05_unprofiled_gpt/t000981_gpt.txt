two of the lads from last year actually came down to watch as well, which almost made it worse. we were stood there after just laughing about how bad the defence is now, like properly taking the mick out of how slow and clueless the back line is. I was asking them if they knew any defenders who might fancy coming in, because we’re that desperate – just anyone who can head a ball and not panic when it drops.

talking to them really hammered it home how different it feels this season. we lost games last year too, but it never felt this hopeless. the talent we had then was miles better, across the whole team. the big problem was the manager – he was rubbish, and you always felt like he was holding us back tactically or with his decisions.

now it’s the opposite: the manager’s not even the main issue, it’s that the squad just isn’t anywhere near the same level. it honestly feels like I’ve gone from playing for a decent club to some much worse side, even though it’s technically the same team.
