So my friend had to do this ridiculous thing for the Metro – they were doing a piece on people who eat roadkill, and she got roped into helping. She was absolutely hanging, like properly hungover and basically still drunk after leaving this party, and then had to get in the car and drive around the countryside looking for dead animals. She was hoping she’d just find something small and manageable, like a bird or a squirrel or something, that she could sort of tick the box with. But instead, after all this driving in her hungover state, she suddenly comes across this entire deer by the side of the road, like a full-on proper deer, which was definitely not what she’d been hoping for.
