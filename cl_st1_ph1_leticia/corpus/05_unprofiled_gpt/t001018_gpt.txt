I sprang awake with this horrible burning sensation and for a second I didn’t understand what was happening. Then I realised my hair was actually on fire. Instead of anyone helping, they were laughing, and that just made it worse, because it felt like they were enjoying it rather than trying to put it out. I completely panicked and bolted out into the street, just running and flailing, trying desperately to smother the flames as I went. All I could think was to get away and get home, so I headed in that direction, still half on fire and absolutely hysterical. I grabbed my phone and called my mum, sobbing and screaming that someone had set me on fire on purpose. She was just baffled and swearing down the line, not really understanding what on earth was going on.
