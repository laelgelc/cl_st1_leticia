Then on the Sunday you pull all of that together into a single summary video. In that video you spell out: here’s the problem we’ve been working on, here are the resources we’ve realised we need, here’s where we’ve got to so far. That becomes the entry point for anyone coming in later.

So new people don’t have to wade through hours of conversation. They can just watch that one Sunday video, see straightaway what’s needed, and then decide how they can plug in. They might think, “I can offer this bit,” or “I could co‑create that resource with them.”

By joining in like that, they’re not just helping solve the original problem, they’re also potentially growing their own business or services at the same time. It’s very much about co‑creation: people see a clear invitation to contribute, they match that with what they can offer, and it becomes this ongoing cycle of solving real problems and building up their own thing as they go.
