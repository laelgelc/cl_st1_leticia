oh yeah they are nice with all that salt, really good actually. If you ever want to do jacket potatoes quickly, there’s a trick I use. Do them in the microwave first to save time, just cook them through like normal so the inside’s done. Then coat the potato in olive oil and salt, wrap it in a paper towel, and pop it in the oven for about five minutes. That way the skin crisps up properly instead of staying all soft. It’s a good little tip.
