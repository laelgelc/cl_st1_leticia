We mostly get recorded spam calls now rather than people actually talking. There’s this one that’s really persistent: it always rings the fax line first, and then it switches to the main phone. Every time, it’s the same recorded voice going on about some government scheme for a new boiler. It just repeats the same message over and over. We’ve tried to do something about it, but you can’t trace it or block it properly because the number never shows up properly – it’s always either coming up as international or completely withheld.
