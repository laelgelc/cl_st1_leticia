I used to absolutely love going to car boot sales, but they’re kind of heartbreaking at the same time. You end up putting out all these things that once meant loads to you, that were really expensive at the time, or that you’d saved up for or begged for at Christmas. Then people come along and haggle you right down, and you let them go for next to nothing. I even ended up selling my two china dolls, which I’d really treasured, and that really stuck with me.
