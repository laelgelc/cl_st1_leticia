Yeah, I did, we went skiing in Bavaria in February. It was really fun but it was actually quite hard because I just kept falling over all the time. We were practising really slowly at first, and then I tried to be brave and actually let go on the slope. The problem was I had absolutely no idea how to slow down properly. So I came up with this ridiculous little strategy: I’d just sit down on my backside at the bottom of the slope to stop myself before I hit the queue of people waiting for the lift. I thought it was quite a good plan, so I kept doing it. Then there was this one time when a man giving a ski lesson with a group of kids started crossing the slope just as I was coming down and trying my “sit-down-to-stop” technique.
