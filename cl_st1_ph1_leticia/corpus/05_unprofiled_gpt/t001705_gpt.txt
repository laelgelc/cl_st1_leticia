So there was this one *Magnum* episode that was just incredibly dark and scary, completely unlike what it’s usually like. Magnum drops what seems to be the nice twin back at her house, and then it looks as though there’s someone else in the house, but you never quite see who it is. It’s all very unsettling and you’re not sure what’s going on.

Later, she rings Magnum in a panic and says she’s killed someone. He rushes over, but when he gets there there’s no body, no blood, no sign that anything’s actually happened. It’s really eerie, because you’ve got this feeling that something awful has gone on, but there’s literally no evidence.

There’s this really tense bit where Magnum’s in the house and there’s someone watching him from inside a cupboard. You know they’re there, he doesn’t, and it’s just horribly creepy, much more like a psychological thriller than a light detective show.

In the end it turns out that the whole business with the “other” twin isn’t what it seems at all. The twist is that the other twin actually died when they were both kidnapped as children. The one who survived has multiple personality disorder, so the “other” twin we think is out there causing trouble is actually just another personality inside her. So all the stuff about the horrible twin and the persecution and the attempted murders is actually her. It makes the whole story really horrible and quite dark, especially compared to what *Magnum* is normally like.
