well I only ended up going on my own because the girl I was travelling with went off to meet her friends to do this road trip, so I suddenly had this random free night in New York before my tour started. I really didn’t fancy just sitting in some hotel room in New York on a Friday night being bored, so I thought, sod it, I’ll go do something. I found this cheap ticket for a Yankees game and just bought it without really thinking it through.

it was only afterwards that it actually dawned on me what that meant – like, I was going to have to get the subway up to the Bronx on my own, at night, and then walk from this really rough station to the stadium by myself. at the time of booking it I just didn’t process any of that, I just thought, ooh baseball, cheap ticket.

on the way there this really salacious bloke stopped me and it was horrible, I felt really vulnerable and just wanted to get away. and then coming back afterwards I was properly scared, like what the hell am I doing here on my own? it was only then that I realised how dodgy the whole thing was and how stupid it was to put myself in that position just because I didn’t want to be bored for one night.
