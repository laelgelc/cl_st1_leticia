For three of those years they’ve been in completely different places – first Taiwan, then Leeds, then London – so it’s all been a bit scattered and long‑distance rather than settled.

He’s a brilliant bloke, genuinely. Very, very nice, and not at all pompous, even though he’s that whole hunting‑shooting‑fishing type and comes from quite a different background. He was privately educated and all that, and his family have this estate in Hampshire that he’s inherited. It sounds grand when you say “estate”, and it’s got a river running through it and fishing rights, but in reality there’s no money to keep it up, so it’s all rather crumbling around them.
