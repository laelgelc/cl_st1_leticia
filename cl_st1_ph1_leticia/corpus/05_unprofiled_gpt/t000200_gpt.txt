When we reference the textbook we have to do it in this really specific way. So it’ll be like, “according to 2015 [author]” and then you either put the quote or you paraphrase what they’ve said, and then after that you have to add “cited in 2014” with the page number. It’s a bit convoluted, because you’re basically saying you got it from a 2014 source that was talking about the 2015 author. The funny thing is, when you write it all out like that, it makes it look like you’ve read loads more than you actually have.
