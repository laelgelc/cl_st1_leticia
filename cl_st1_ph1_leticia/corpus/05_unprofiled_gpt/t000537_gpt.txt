for years I kept wondering about this children’s book I’d had, the one I literally couldn’t read past a certain page because it frightened me so much. I remembered the title, so eventually I tracked it down and ordered a copy from a bookshop. When it arrived it turned out to be this old wartime hardback, on that really poor, faded paper. I sat down and read it again, all the way through this time, and I just couldn’t work out which bit had scared me so badly back then. I couldn’t recognise the part that had once completely stopped me reading.
