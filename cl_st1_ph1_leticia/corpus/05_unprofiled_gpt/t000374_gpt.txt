So they’d obviously been a family who liked those huge wreaths with the name all over them, and we’d gone completely the other way and just had these small flowers that Mum actually liked. As we were coming in, their flowers were still being taken out, so there was this strange overlap. I went to move ours, just instinctively, and the funeral director stopped me and said, “No, no, don’t do that, that’s our job.” I remember thinking that was a bit odd, being told not to touch our own flowers.
