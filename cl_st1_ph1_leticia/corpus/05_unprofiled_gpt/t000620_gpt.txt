We went over there one Monday evening for one of his tennis coaching courses, and honestly, we were both really impressed. All the pitches were floodlit, and there were games going on everywhere you looked – rugby on one, tennis on another, hockey over there, football as well. He was just amazed by it all, absolutely blown away by the whole university set‑up. I said to him, you know, you could come here, you could go to university. And he just wished he could, if it meant he could just play sport all the time and not have to bother with any of the studying.
