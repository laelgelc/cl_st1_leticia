So then I came across these women with horses and I thought, right, I’ll ask them. I still thought it was a car crash at that point, just because of all the smoke and everything. I pulled over and asked what was going on, and they said no, it wasn’t a crash at all, it was a military jet that had gone down and the whole area was blocked off. One of the women then gave me really clear directions, telling me exactly how to get to the road that would lead me back onto the bypass.
