I really don’t like the word “god” because of that old patriarchal image of a man in the sky; it just doesn’t fit what I feel. For me it’s more like an all‑encompassing energy, source, something we’re all fragments of. So when I look at homeless people, I see them as other selves who’ve chosen an experience of lack to learn and grow from. That’s why just handing over money feels wrong to me; it creates an energetic imbalance unless there’s some exchange. I much prefer arrangements like doing a bit of gardening for help, like the example I mentioned earlier.
