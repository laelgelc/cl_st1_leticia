I just don’t get why they latch onto these really specific measures and act like they’re the magic answer. It’s like the recommended weekly units of alcohol – they pick a number and suddenly that’s the big guideline everyone’s meant to obsess over. Same with when they were going on about salt years ago. There was that whole thing about not eating too much salt, like it was the single big evil. I always remember that advert with Jenny Eclair, her going on about “don’t eat more than a bit of salt” and all that.
