Running the gallery really was the best thing I ever did; I can feel myself brighten up whenever I talk about it. What mattered most to me was making sure people got proper value for money. I was very careful to keep the artists’ prices realistic so that, even if they weren’t famous names, they still had a real chance of selling their work. At the same time, customers could afford to buy original art, not just prints or posters. Being able to make that possible, to connect ordinary buyers with genuine, original pieces, was a wonderful thing.
