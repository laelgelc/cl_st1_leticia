I just wouldn’t put up with any nonsense, you know. There was this one lad, big bloke, ended up playing for Birmingham later on. He was towering over me, giving it the hard man, and I just told him to bend over and I gave him a whack. I know, it was probably illegal even then, but I wasn’t having him run the show. I’m not some raging violent bloke, not really, but with those kids you had to be firm. Another teacher said, “You can’t do that, it’s illegal,” and I just said, “Well, go on then, grass me up.”
