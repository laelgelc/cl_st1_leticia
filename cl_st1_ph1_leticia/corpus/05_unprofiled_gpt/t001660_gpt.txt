there was a really interesting thing that came out of that Ovarian Cancer Action survey, and it was picked up on Woman’s Hour as well. They were talking about how men with prostate cancer are routinely asked about their erectile function, their continence, their libido, their sex lives – all of that is built into the way they’re treated and followed up. It’s just assumed that those things matter and will be discussed. But for women with gynaecological cancers, there’s nothing comparable. We don’t get that same kind of structured conversation or counselling about whether and how we might want to continue our sex lives, or how treatment is affecting us in that way.
