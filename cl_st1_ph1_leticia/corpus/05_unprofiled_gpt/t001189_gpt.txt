He has got really good stamina, so once he’d picked himself up he did start to overtake a few as they went round. It was a really hilly course, though, so he was still very much at the back, just not quite last anymore. I moved round to stand near the finishing stretch, thinking I’d at least see him come in. As I was waiting, these two boys suddenly came sprinting past, somewhere around tenth or fifteenth place. Then another boy came along behind them, actually running while he was being sick, and I just couldn’t understand it, because he clearly wasn’t going to win.
