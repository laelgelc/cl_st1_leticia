So on Saturday morning, before we actually met, I ended up speaking to him on the phone for a bit. It was just meant to be a nice, friendly thing – you know, my first time talking to my friend’s boyfriend. She’d told me he was shy and a bit nervous about meeting my parents and me, so I was really gentle and chatty, just trying to put him at ease. It wasn’t like some big gossip session or anything, just me knowing he was a bit anxious and trying to make it less awkward.

But afterwards, he completely flipped at her. He had a proper go, saying she shouldn’t have told anyone he was shy, like she’d been talking about him behind his back. He was acting like we were all laughing at him or something, and even said he didn’t know why he was bothering to come at all. It really upset her, because from her side it was just sharing something small with a close friend, not betraying him.
