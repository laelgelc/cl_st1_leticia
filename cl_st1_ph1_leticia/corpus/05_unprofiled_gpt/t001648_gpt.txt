I remember once having this really painful infected cyst on my vulva. It was absolutely excruciating, so I just rang up and took the next available appointment. I ended up seeing this young Asian woman doctor. When I told her what was wrong and she realised where it was, she just looked really distasteful, almost repulsed, before examining me. It was only a second or two before she caught herself and got on with it, but I very clearly saw that look go across her face. I was absolutely mortified. And I just thought, you know, that kind of reaction could so easily put somebody off ever going back to the doctor again, especially about anything in that area.
