After I moved in there, her husband developed Parkinson’s, so she had our shared boundary changed into a proper wall and had the whole back garden paved over to make it more manageable for them. That all led to this whole conversation between us about what she was doing and why. She was very much the sort of neighbour who would keep you talking for half an hour if you just popped out to hang your washing on the line. You’d step outside for a minute and suddenly find yourself in this long, winding chat over the garden, whether you’d meant to or not.
