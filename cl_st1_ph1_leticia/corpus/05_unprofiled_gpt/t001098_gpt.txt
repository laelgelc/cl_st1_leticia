I think with people who are over fifty, a lot of them have got loads of experience already, they’ve worked for years, but they’re struggling mainly because of their age and the fact they haven’t had to look for work in ages. For younger people who’ve never had a job, proper work placements make more sense, because they actually need that experience. But for the older ones, it’s more about giving them help with things like writing a CV, filling in applications, and just knowing where to look for jobs now. Job hunting’s completely changed since they first went into work decades ago, so it’s not that they can’t do the jobs, it’s that they’re out of touch with how you’re supposed to find them these days.
