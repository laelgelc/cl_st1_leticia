I remember when we were kids, if someone in the village had chicken pox or measles, our mums would practically march us round there so we’d catch it and get it over with. It was just what you did, everyone shared the germs. Now it feels like parents are so protective, keeping their children away from every little bug. The trouble is, you’re getting people who don’t catch things like chicken pox or measles until their twenties, thirties, even forties, and by then it can do them serious harm.
