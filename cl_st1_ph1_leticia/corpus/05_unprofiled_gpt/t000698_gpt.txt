I'm quite pleased that this time I'm not doing some fixed, time‑limited thing like Lent; I've just given it up indefinitely, and I don't feel hooked anymore. At school they told us Lent was supposed to be thirty days, but it actually goes on longer, so you can apparently have whatever you've given up on Sundays. I always thought that was a stupid idea – if you only give something up for six days at a time, it doesn't really count. I suspect some RE teachers liked that because they didn't want to give things up properly themselves. And of course, it is Lent now.
