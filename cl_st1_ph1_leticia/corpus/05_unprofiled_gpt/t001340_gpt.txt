I actually went out and bought myself a recorder because I’m hopeless at identifying birds just by ear. The idea was quite organised in my head: I’d go into the woods, record the birdsong, then come back home and play it alongside this DVD I’ve got, matching each call to the right bird. In practice, it hasn’t worked out so neatly. The moment I press the record button, the birds often just stop singing, as if they know I’m listening. And when they don’t, there are usually several of them going at once, all overlapping so I can’t separate which song belongs to which bird. To be honest, I’ve never actually sat down and listened back to any of the recordings I’ve made.
