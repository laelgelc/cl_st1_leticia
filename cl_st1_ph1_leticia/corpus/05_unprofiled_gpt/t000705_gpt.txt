I’d honestly never really thought about different types of education before, not properly anyway, until I was helping her choose a school for her kid. It was only then that it really hit me how different they can be. Her little girl’s six, and they had this fancy dress day at school. But it wasn’t just “come as whatever you like”; they actually gave them a list of things like “doctor.” And next to “doctor” it literally said stuff like “earns lots of money” and “cares for people.” So from that age they’re already being taught to think in those terms.
