I still really like listening to albums all the way through in the order they’re meant to be played. When artists put an album together, they actually think about which track should follow which, how the mood rises and falls, and how it all fits together as a single, unified thing. When you just shuffle individual songs, you lose that sense of a complete whole, it becomes more bitty. With records, because it’s such a faff to skip tracks, you naturally just let it play straight through.
