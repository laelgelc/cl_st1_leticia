what I really want us to set up is something that lets us live how we actually want to live, not just be stuck in one place because of work. So I’m imagining a business where the core team is remote, and we can be anywhere we like – house‑sitting here, or spending a few months with family, or choosing somewhere we just feel like living for a while – and the work still carries on.

The idea is that everyone we work with is also remote, so they can be wherever suits them, and the whole thing isn’t tied to a particular office or city. It’s more like a distributed team that we coordinate from wherever we happen to be.

That way, we’re not only earning, but we’ve got the flexibility to move around, to be near people we care about when we want to, and to choose our locations based on what we want from life at that point, rather than where a job happens to be.
