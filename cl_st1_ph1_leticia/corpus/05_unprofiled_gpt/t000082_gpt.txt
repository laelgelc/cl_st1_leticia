I just find the whole situation staggering, really. In the end I just said to her, well, if you think it’s so wonderful at that school, why don’t you go and work there instead? 

Some people thought I was a bit blunt, but I’ve worked in all sorts of different places, and I know this place is actually pretty good, even with all its frustrations. That’s exactly why I felt justified saying it. I wasn’t being nasty, just honest. From my own experience, it’s clear we’ve got it better here than a lot of people realise, and I wasn’t going to sit there and listen to her run it down day after day.
