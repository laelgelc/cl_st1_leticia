Well, a funny thing happened on Sunday. It was my first time on my own as warden, so I went down to the church early to get everything ready. I had to take all the covers off, light the candles, sort out the microphones, put the Bible in its place, all that. I was nervous doing it all by myself, especially the alarm. I’d never turned it off before, so I was really worried about setting it off by mistake. I stood there at the door, checking and rechecking the alarm code, making sure I had it fixed in my head, then used my key to open the door, still double-checking the number.
