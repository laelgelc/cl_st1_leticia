I was in such a state that morning, honestly. I’d been sick about four times just trying to get into work, and every time I thought, right, that must be it, I’d have to stop again. By the time I actually made it into the building I was barely functioning. Then I had this meeting I couldn’t get out of, and I just sat there nodding away, agreeing to everything because I couldn’t really take anything in. I’ve got no idea what I signed myself up for that day, I was just trying not to move too much in case it all started again.

When I finally staggered back to my desk, someone had very kindly left a can of Coke and a Mars bar there for me. Honestly, I looked at it and thought, that is black medicine, that is. It was like this little care package from heaven. I just sort of clung to it, thinking, if anything’s going to get me through this, it’s that. And it did, it actually got me through the rest of the day.
