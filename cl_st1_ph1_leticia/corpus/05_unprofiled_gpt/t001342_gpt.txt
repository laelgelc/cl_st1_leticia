I took it back the next day and said to the woman, “I’m really sorry, I can’t cope with this, it’s just too noisy.” She was very good about it and said, “Well, we’ll find the quietest one we’ve got.” So we stood there in the shop listening to all these alarm clocks ticking and I picked the one that sounded the quietest. Brought it home, put it in the bedroom, and it was still absolutely unbearable. I could even hear it out in the hall with the bedroom door open. I’ve decided it must depend what surface you stand it on.
