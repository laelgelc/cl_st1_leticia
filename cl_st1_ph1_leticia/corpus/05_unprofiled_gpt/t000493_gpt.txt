imagine you’re on your bike and you go up one hill, and then another hill, and both hills are exactly the same distance, but one is much steeper than the other. the actual riding up each hill, that’s the experiment – you’re doing the thing, carrying it out. then afterwards, when you’ve finished both rides, that’s when the evaluation starts. you sit back and ask yourself questions like, which hill was harder? which one left you more exhausted? which one felt easier to ride up even though the distance was the same? that bit – comparing them and answering those questions – that’s the evaluation, not the riding itself.
