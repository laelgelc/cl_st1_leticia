So my aunt who’s a lecturer in molecular biology actually offered to read my dissertation as well. I was really grateful, so I gave her the introduction to look at. Because mine was more of a literature‑based dissertation rather than an experimental one, the way she suggested changing it didn’t quite fit. She made it more like what she’s used to, and it ended up being less focused for what I needed. When I showed both versions to my lecturer, she actually preferred my original introduction. So in the end I stuck with what I’d written, even though my aunt had put effort into it.
