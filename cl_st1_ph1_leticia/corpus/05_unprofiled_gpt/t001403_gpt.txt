I was watching this programme about Britain in the 1940s, after the war, when there was this big shortage of workers, and it was really different with people on benefits. They had loads of people who were classed as disabled, like there was this bloke who’d been in a wheelchair from birth, and he actually wanted to work. Instead of just leaving them on benefits, they’d get assessed by doctors who would look at what they *could* do, not just what they couldn’t, and then they’d place them into jobs that suited whatever abilities they still had.
