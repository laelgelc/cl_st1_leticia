I’ve been on a bit of a binge with “Sounds of the Seventies” on BBC iPlayer lately. I suddenly realised I’d somehow missed three whole weeks of it, so I’ve been catching up and listening to loads of episodes back to back. It’s been perfect background while I’ve been tidying up and cooking; I had it on all afternoon the other day. There was a Bowie-themed episode as well, which I really enjoyed.

Music just really moves me, especially that 1970s stuff – rock, disco, ska, reggae, all of it. When I’ve got those tracks on, walking for exercise stops feeling like exercise at all; it’s like the music just carries me along and I don’t notice the effort.

What’s funny is that when I put my iPod on random the other day, it felt like there was absolutely no 1980s music coming up at all. It was all seventies, a bit of nineties, loads of noughties, and some sixties, but basically nothing from the eighties. It surprised me, because I do actually like eighties music. It’s not that I’ve gone off it or anything; it just seems to have vanished from my iPod somehow, even though that decade’s got so much I enjoy.
