well I mean when I went out there with them I didn’t just sit and do everything for them I just gave them ideas and let them get on with it you know we did things like potato printing and leaf printing and then I raided the kitchen drawers and said right what patterns can we make with these and they were off and with the sandpit I showed them how if the sand’s really damp you can build these sort of meeting tunnels and then I just left them to carry on and they loved it and afterwards apparently they told their mum that auntie and uncle were much better at playing than granny and grandad which rather proves the point I think if you’re constantly doing everything with children they never really develop their own imagination or independent thinking you give them a spark of an idea and then you stand back and let them work it out themselves that’s how they learn to play properly on their own
