I decided I was going to crack down and get something done, you know, be properly productive. So I went out into the garage and started working out there, cutting these great big boards for the big Norfolk. That took me a while, but it felt good to be doing something practical. Once I’d got that done, I thought, right, next thing is to make a banner. Then I went over to the church to pick up the church banner, but when I got there it was just too wet, conditions were no good for what I’d planned. Still, after all that, I felt absolutely fully alert.
