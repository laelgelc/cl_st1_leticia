I was absolutely furious, really, and deeply hurt that they wouldn’t marry us in church after everything I’d done and everything my family had given. It felt like the institution had completely forgotten any sense of Christian compassion, and it reminded me of this sort of blind, irrational bureaucracy you see everywhere now. 

It’s not just the church; it’s the same with the NHS, where rules seem to matter more than people. Even silly things, like at a garden centre restaurant where I only wanted a very simple meal, but because of their rigid pricing rules I had to pay more than made any sense. No flexibility, no common sense, just “that’s the rule.” 

All of it together made me think that bureaucracy has gone completely mad in some cases, and it really colours how I feel about institutions now, especially the church.
