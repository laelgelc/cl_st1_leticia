When you were talking about that black smoke and chains, it reminded me of something from when I was a teenager. Every Sunday we used to all meet up in this field, like my mates and a few lads, and we’d play football and then later on we’d just sit around drinking. The field was kind of boxed in. On one side there was a primary school, and on the other side there was another field with a fence. There were trees around, and behind that there was a golf course. Near it there was a pub and then some empty ground, sort of another field.

We always went to the same place, like a proper Sunday thing, and it was just normal – football, hanging out, having a drink. But the layout made it feel a bit cut off, you know? School on one side, then that fenced-off field, then the trees and the golf course behind, and the pub and that bit of empty land nearby. It all kind of added to the atmosphere of the place, like it was our little spot away from everything.
