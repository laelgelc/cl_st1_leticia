so we went down this lane like he’d said, and there were these old houses and a few outbuildings, and this dog started barking away at us. and I could hear water somewhere, you know, that sort of rushing sound, so I thought, well, we must be close. we followed the sound round and there it was, the very place we’d been looking for all along.

there was a chap there, so I got talking to him about it, and he told me the whole story. apparently it used to be a mill, proper old place, but they’d taken a lot of the original material off it. he said they’d actually sold those bits on to another mill down south that was being restored. and he was quite put out about it, said he didn’t think they should have done that at all, you know, reckoned it was wrong they’d let it go like that.
