Before, we were right in the middle of everything – hectic, noisy, on busy streets, all a bit full‑on. Here it’s just so much calmer and more relaxed, it feels completely different. And even though we’re further out, getting into town isn’t really a big deal. In the evening it’s, what, about twelve quid in a taxi? Which, you know, in the grand scheme of things is nothing – it’s like the equivalent of a few pints of beer, really, so it doesn’t bother me.
