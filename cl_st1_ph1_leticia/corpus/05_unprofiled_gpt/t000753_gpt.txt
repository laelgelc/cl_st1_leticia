Someone asked me the other day if I’d got my Garmin ready for the run, and I had to say I don’t even have one. I’m perfectly happy just knowing what time I finish in, that’s all I really want. It’s not like my friend, whose Garmin tells her her pace and distance every single kilometre. She gets all those stats popping up, but I honestly don’t care about that level of detail. I don’t feel like knowing my pace every kilometre would make any difference to my running.
