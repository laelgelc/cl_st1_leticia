I think with these family stories there are always these odd gaps, aren’t there, where you end up having to imagine the “facts” because nothing was ever properly written down. My uncle once asked how my great‑grandmother actually met the man she went off with, and nobody has any idea. It could’ve been that her husband introduced them, or that they met when he was there on military training. And then there’s this story about my great‑grandfather chasing them through a station, which nobody can verify or even remember where it came from.
