I was stood there yesterday, just opposite the X-ray department, and this bloke came past after having a scan. He had a shaved head and tattoos, and his wife and little boy were with him, the lad being pushed along. As they went by, I saw him look over and read the words on my booth. He didn’t look at me at all, not directly, but you could see something change in his face, you know, like it had suddenly hit him. Then I heard them mention that he was going for a CT scan next, and I just thought, he’s probably just been told he’s got cancer. You could almost feel it off him, just from that moment and the way he reacted.
