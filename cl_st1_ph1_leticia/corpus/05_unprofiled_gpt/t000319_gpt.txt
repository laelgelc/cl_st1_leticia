I do feel like there’s this whole global system that’s been built almost entirely around economic outcomes, and it’s been at the expense of both the environment and society. That’s been the dominant pattern for the last thirty years or so, this kind of neoliberal model, and when it finally collapses it’s going to be absolutely momentous. But I’m oddly optimistic about it. I genuinely think humanity is becoming more loving and more open. Even with the refugee situation, as awful as so much of it is, it’s forcing people to confront their own prejudices, to see others as humans first rather than just as “Muslims” or whatever religious label.
