When I travel I always use inner‑ear headphones now, instead of earplugs, to help me sleep in really noisy places. I started doing it after staying in this room above a club in Belize – it was so loud, but the headphones made it bearable. I’ve had some pretty frightening nights in India too, with people knocking on the door and actually trying the handle, and I was just lying there thinking, please, whoever this is, just go away. Using one inner‑ear headphone while the other ear rests on the pillow makes a huge difference. It doesn’t block all the sound, but it distances it so it feels far away. I strongly recommend it.
