I did actually get a bit of help building the statue on the Minecraft server. It ended up being quite a complicated three‑dimensional design, because I tried to merge the side‑on Super Mario 3 pixel art with the front‑facing one, so it wasn’t just a flat sprite. From there I modified Mario so he turned into this gnome‑like Statue of Liberty figure: I gave him a pointy beard, a big pointy hat, and put a torch in one hand and a sort of tablet in the other, like the Declaration of Independence.
