well anyway it was months after I’d first rung them about the bits of branch coming down that anybody actually turned up. someone finally came out with a camera to take pictures of the trees. it happened to be while I had this completely clear view, because they’d taken the old windows out and not put the new ones in yet, so I could see straight out to the trees and the road. literally just before he arrived I’d watched quite a sizeable branch come down, and then a group of schoolchildren walked past not long after, so it really brought home how risky it was. he said he’d call me back after he’d reported it, but of course nobody ever did. and the other thing I mentioned was how dense all the growth is up the trunks – it makes it really hard for drivers to see properly at the junctions. you have to pull right out into the road before you can see what’s coming.
