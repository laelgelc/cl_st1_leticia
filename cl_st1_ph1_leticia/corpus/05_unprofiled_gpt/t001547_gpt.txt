So in my head this housing system would work really differently to how it does now. You’d still effectively “own” your home in the sense that it’s yours, you live there, you can make it how you want, and you’re not under constant pressure to move or treat it like an investment. You could modify it while you’re living there, make it nice, make it suit you, but you don’t then sell it on for a big lump of money.

If you did decide you wanted to move – say you suddenly fancied living down in Devon – you wouldn’t go through this whole buying and selling circus. You’d go on a website, look for somebody in Devon who wants to leave there, and you’d just swap houses with them. So it’s more like a big, organised exchange system, where everyone’s housed and can move around when they need to, without all the financial madness attached.
