I was in this café with my friend after we’d been to that bras and pants exhibition, and I ordered this gluten‑free cake, thinking I was being really good. I ate it and almost immediately felt absolutely awful – my heart was racing and I just felt really ill. I ended up having to eat some normal food to calm everything down, and it slowly got better. So I’ve decided it must be the sugar that’s doing it to me now. 

And what’s interesting is, normally when I’m feeling sad and missing you lot I’d just eat loads of sugary rubbish. But that weekend I didn’t: I had dates and yogurt instead, went to sleep, and I actually woke up feeling brilliant, got loads of work done, went to the gym and for a run. So I really can’t have sugar any more, it just makes me ill.
