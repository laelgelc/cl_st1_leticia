I was coming home one night and it was absolutely throwing it down with snow, really heavy, and the roads were just thick with slush and salt. Everything was getting covered, cars were filthy. I got pulled over, and the copper comes up and says he can’t see my number plates, so he’s going to give me a ticket. I said to him, “Have you seen the state of the roads? Everything’s covered.” The slush had completely obscured the plates, not just on my car but on everyone’s. I pointed out that his own plates were just as bad, you could hardly read them either. In the end, even though he tried to ticket me at first, he backed down and let me off.
