I wouldn't turn round to someone and say they have no idea what it's like to travel or do a masters or PhD. If you know people who've done it, you've spoken to them, you've watched programmes about it, then of course you've got some inclination, some idea of what it's like, even if your perception might be a bit skewed. What frustrates me is that people with children often talk about it in this really black‑and‑white, dismissive way. It's that “you just don’t know” tone that I wouldn’t use myself.
