so the whole idea is really about making relationships visible and concrete, almost like mapping them out step by step. Instead of just saying “networking matters,” we’d actually demonstrate how a specific relationship is formed and used. For example, imagine a really ambitious seventeen- or eighteen-year-old who wants to meet Richard Branson. On the surface that sounds impossible. But if they realise that someone just one year above them already has a connection with Virgin, and Virgin is connected to Richard Branson, suddenly that goal looks much more achievable. By showing that chain explicitly, they can see how to make that meeting happen in a logical way, rather than it feeling like magic or luck. That’s the kind of breakdown we’re talking about: taking something that seems out of reach and showing, step by step, how existing relationships can bridge the gap.
