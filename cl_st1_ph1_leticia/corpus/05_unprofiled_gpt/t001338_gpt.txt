So because we’d already said we were going to do birthday presents when I got to Germany, I honestly wasn’t expecting anything while I was still in Peru. On my actual birthday I was just heading out, and the doorman stopped me and handed me this little freeze‑dried rose and a card from him. He’d written in it that he didn’t want to leave me with nothing on my birthday and to have a really good day at work. I thought that was it and it was really cute. Then I got home later, and there was another present and a card on my bed that he’d arranged in advance, given to the maid and asked her to put there on my birthday.
