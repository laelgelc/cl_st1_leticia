I actually went to see a life coach recently, because I'm trying to start a completely new career. What she said was that most of us really limit ourselves when we think about what we could do for work. We tend to stay in this tiny little box of “sensible” or “realistic” options, and that keeps us stuck. 

So her advice was to forget all that at first and just write down dream jobs, however unrealistic they might seem. The point is just to get the ideas flowing. I mean, she said it could be something as far-fetched as being George Clooney’s personal secretary. It’s not about actually doing that, it’s just to start thinking “out of the box.”
