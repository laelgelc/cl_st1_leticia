I was watching this thing the other day, like one of those BBC bits where the reporter’s walking along talking to the camera, then stopping and asking random people questions. They were going round talking to people about the EU, and what really struck me was that most of them basically admitted they didn’t really know that much about it. But then as soon as immigration was mentioned, everyone suddenly had really strong opinions. It was all different ages and both men and women as well. I actually thought it was really interesting seeing that contrast.
