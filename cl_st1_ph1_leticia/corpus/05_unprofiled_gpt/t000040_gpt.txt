I really, really wanted a Fiesta, because they’re such nice cars and I’d had my heart set on getting one of the new-shape ones. But when it came down to the actual prices, they were just too expensive for my budget. Even after I’d scraped together as much money as I could and extended the loan, it still wasn’t enough to make a Fiesta realistic. So in the end I had to be sensible about it. I went for another car instead, one that was basically the same age, with similar mileage and specifications, but that made a lot more financial sense for me. It wasn’t the dream choice, but it was the one I could actually afford.
