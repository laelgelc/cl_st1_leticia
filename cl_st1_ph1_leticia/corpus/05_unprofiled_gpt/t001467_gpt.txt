I was listening to this interview with Ed Byrne where they asked him if doing a hundred-and-fifty‑day tour was exhausting, and he said, well, yeah, it is a bit tiring, but it’s a pretty small price to pay for having such an awesome job. And he made the point that loads of people do much harder, more exhausting work day in, day out, so he really doesn’t feel he’s in a position to complain about having to work, essentially, two hours a night on stage.
