They rang me up first just to cover a theory class for one evening, so I said yes to that. Then, because of staff sickness, they asked if I could stretch it to two weeks, and I agreed. While I was there those two weeks, I got everything sorted – the register, all the paperwork, that kind of thing. After that, they came back to me again and said, actually, could I keep covering the class right through until Christmas, while they tried to recruit new staff. Then in October they told me they’d pencilled me in to stay on with that group permanently until Easter. I thought about it and said yes, I’d do it, so I ended up agreeing to carry on with them for the rest of the time until Easter.
