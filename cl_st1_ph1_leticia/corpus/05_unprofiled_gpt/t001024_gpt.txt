yeah it’s funny actually, I was listening to Johnnie Walker’s “Sounds of the Seventies” this morning and the first hour was all David Bowie. They had people talking about how he just sort of invented himself and became this character, and sometimes he even seemed a bit confused about whether he really was that character or not. 

But that’s the thing that really struck me – it shows you can just choose whatever role or character you want in life and play it. It doesn’t have to be false, it doesn’t mean you’re faking anything. You can just decide, “this is who I’m going to be now,” and do it, and that’s as real as anything else.
