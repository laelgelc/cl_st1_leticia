I just had this really strong feeling when I left the hospital that I wouldn’t be coming back. It was like something came over me, and I said a very deliberate goodbye to him. He looked so bad, really ill, and it struck me that this might be the last time. He’d said he wanted to take the children in to see him, but I felt uneasy about that. I told him we should give them the choice rather than deciding for them, especially with how poorly he looked. It didn’t feel right to just assume they should go in without asking how they felt.
