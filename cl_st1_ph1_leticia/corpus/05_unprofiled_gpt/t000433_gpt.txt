When we went out driving on the Sunday it was absolutely fine for us, the roads were clear enough to get around. But all around that loop road there was this really long line of cars just parked up on the verges. The main road had been cleared, but obviously people still couldn’t get their cars into the side roads, so they’d just left them there and walked home instead. I remember thinking it looked so odd. Do you remember that, all those cars lined up in the snow?
