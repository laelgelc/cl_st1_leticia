So there’s this bit in *Human Traffic* at the start, where one of the main guys is getting ready for this massive Friday‑night party. He’s in his room, putting his outfit together and everything, and he’s got a Bill Hicks DVD on in the background. And Hicks is doing this routine where he’s like, “Yeah, I take drugs, I drink, I smoke, but I don’t hurt anyone, I don’t go out and start fights.” The whole point is, if you’re just getting high on a bit of weed, you’re not exactly going to be smashing a beer bottle over someone’s head in a pub fight.
