There was this man who did this water thing, you know, some kind of technique, in front of these two blokes who were really sceptical. They were watching him and not believing it at all. Then one of them decided to have a go himself, just to see what would happen. After he’d tried it, the man went over to him and sort of examined him, did his thing, and then said, “You’ve got an allergy, haven’t you?” The bloke was a bit taken aback and said yes. Then the man added that it was an allergy to tree pollen. The sceptical bloke confirmed that was exactly right.
