Do you find that with television drama as well, when you go back and watch things again? I do that with series like *Morse*, *Lewis*, and *Frost*. The first time, I’m following the plot, but when I rewatch them I’m not really watching for the story anymore because I already know what happens. Instead I’m looking much more at the acting, the way the characters are played, how they interact with each other. It becomes fascinating just to observe the performances, the nuances in how they speak to each other and behave, rather than focusing on the narrative itself.
