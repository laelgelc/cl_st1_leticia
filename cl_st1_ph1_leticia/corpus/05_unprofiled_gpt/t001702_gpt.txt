We all went to spend Christmas with them, and Mum actually went out a few days earlier. At first she said it was all fine, but on the day this other person arrived, the woman just completely changed. She spent most of the time screaming and hiding away in her bedroom, and anything that happened she reacted badly to. Dad ended up being upstairs most of the time, trying to calm her down, and he was really torn, caught between the two sides and not knowing what to do.
