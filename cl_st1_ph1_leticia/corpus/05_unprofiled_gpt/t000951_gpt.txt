We were driving through this really hilly area, all these old stone houses from the mill days, looking really filthy and worn. The roads were so steep and terraced that I didn’t even want to park the car there. As we were going along, I suddenly spotted this bright yellow child’s bike actually mounted up on a wall, which looked so odd. Then a bit further on, there was another bright yellow bike, this time stuck up on a post outside a bike repair shop.
