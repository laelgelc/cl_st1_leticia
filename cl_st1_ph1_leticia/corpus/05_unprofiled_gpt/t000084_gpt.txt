People just see that the kids are out the door by three or four and think teachers have it easy, but they’ve no idea. Classroom teaching is really stressful and hard work, especially when you’ve got disruptive pupils you’re not allowed to exclude. You’re stuck managing that behaviour day in, day out, and there isn’t enough support to help deal with it properly. That’s why people get so fed up when others say teachers have short days – they just don’t understand what it’s actually like.
