To make a three-wagon connection between Amsterdam and Essen, you first need to have three yellow cards. You pay those three matching yellow cards to the bank. Then you place three of your blue train pieces on the route between Amsterdam and Essen. For completing that connection, you gain four points on the score track. Throughout the game, this is how you score: you collect coloured cards, then when you have enough matching ones for a route, you pay them to the bank, place your trains, and receive the corresponding points.
