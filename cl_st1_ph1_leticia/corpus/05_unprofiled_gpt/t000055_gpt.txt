We were in the car park and I had him on that stupid extendable lead, which I really don’t like anyway. I hadn’t managed to clip it in time and then he suddenly spotted these three dogs, all off the lead. That was it – he just bolted. Because it was that extendable thing, he had enough momentum to yank me straight off my feet. I went headfirst into the side of a car before I even knew what was happening, and then he carried on running off. It all happened so fast, and it was completely because of that wretched lead and those other dogs.
