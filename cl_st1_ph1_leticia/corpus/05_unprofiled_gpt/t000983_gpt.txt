Paul Merson was a big-name player, but he went bankrupt because he lost all his money through betting. He even told this story once: when he got his first professional pay cheque, it was about a hundred quid, and he went straight down to Ladbrokes and lost the lot. After that, he said he ripped his shirt and went home and told his mum he’d been mugged, just to cover up the fact he’d blown all his wages gambling.
