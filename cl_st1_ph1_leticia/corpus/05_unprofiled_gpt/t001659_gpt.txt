I remember sitting in A&E once, after I’d had a miscarriage, and overhearing this young doctor talking about women coming in “pouring with blood” and how, in his view, they exaggerated. He said that even a relatively small amount of blood can look like an awful lot. That really stuck with me. So later, when I was having all this bleeding myself, I kept thinking back to that. It made me wonder if I was just overreacting, if what I was experiencing was actually within normal limits.
