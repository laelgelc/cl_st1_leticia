We went to the Comedy Store and I thought it’d be a laugh, but it started off a bit awkward. I went up to the bloke on the door and sort of stumbled through, asking if there were any seats or any free tickets or anything, and he was just really brusque, barely responded at all. It was that really short, shut‑down kind of answer, and you’re left standing there feeling a bit daft. I came away thinking how disappointing it was that a place that’s meant to be all about fun and comedy could feel so unwelcoming right from the door.
