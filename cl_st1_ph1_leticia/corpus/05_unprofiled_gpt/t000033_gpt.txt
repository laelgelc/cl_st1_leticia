so basically what happened was one of my friends came to me and told me this whole story about my friend's best friend's ex. Apparently he’d gone out drinking with this other girl, and then at the end of the night she gave him a lift home. When they got back he was trying to get her to stay over, and from what I was told he apparently kissed her as well. 

The way it was passed on to me wasn’t in a bitchy way or anything, it wasn’t malicious. It was more like, “look, this is what I’ve heard, you’re closer to her, you decide what you want to do with it.” So it was kind of left in my hands whether I said anything or not.

I really agonised about it because of the timing. This all came out just before Christmas, and I just thought, if I tell her now I’m going to completely ruin her Christmas. Then it was like, well if I leave it a bit, I’ll end up ruining her New Year instead. So I ended up sitting on it for a while, trying to work out when would be the least awful time to drop something like that on her.

In the end I didn’t actually tell her until about the middle of January. By then all the Christmas and New Year stuff was over, and I just thought, right, I can’t keep this to myself any longer, she needs to know. When I finally did tell her, she didn’t really react the way I expected. She just said she’d have to talk to him about it, and that was kind of it. There wasn’t this big dramatic fall-out or anything, just that she’d speak to him.
