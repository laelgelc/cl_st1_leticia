I just think if you genuinely want to help someone, you’ve got to put them in a position where they can help themselves. Just throwing money at a problem doesn’t really fix it. If you gave a load of cash to someone who’s terrible with money, it wouldn’t magically sort their life out; they’d probably just end up in the same mess again. It’s the same with bigger issues, like countries in trouble. The real key is teaching people how to solve their own problems, so they’re not dependent on constant help.
