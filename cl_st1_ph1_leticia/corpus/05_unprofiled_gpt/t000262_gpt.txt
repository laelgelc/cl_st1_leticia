It’s just such a scary situation, really. And sometimes cyclists don’t help themselves either. There was that recent case where a cyclist was killed by a lorry turning left at traffic lights – he’d crept right up the side of the lorry while the light was on red. He was far too close, completely in the lorry’s blind spot, so the driver just couldn’t see him at all. That’s why drivers really must check their wing mirrors, especially when they’re cornering, because they just can’t know what’s there.
