we even took it further than just making little piles of the stuff, there were a few of us that actually made a sort of rifle out of it. we got a short bit of copper pipe, sealed one end up, and packed it with our gunpowder, then put some wadding in and a ball bearing down the barrel. we’d prop the thing up on a fire and then leg it round the back of a tree, peeping round to aim it at this sapling, about six inches thick. and it actually blew a hole clean through it.
