if I get given something and it just doesn’t feel right to me, I really do trust that gut reaction. I don’t just leave it there though. I’ll go and get a second opinion – I’ll ask someone else to look at it, answer a few questions, and see what they notice. That way I’m not just relying on my own sense that something’s off.
