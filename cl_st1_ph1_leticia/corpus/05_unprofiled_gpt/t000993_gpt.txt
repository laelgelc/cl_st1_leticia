I don't actually think people will live as long as they're predicting, but the big issue is what the country will look like in about fifteen years. By then, most people here will be old rather than young, so the whole balance shifts. That means your generation isn’t just saving up for your own pensions, you’re effectively paying for ours as well. There’ll be a relatively small number of young people trying to support a very large, ageing population, and that’s a huge financial burden to carry.
