I think a lot of the reason Labour did so badly before was because so many of their core voters just walked away over Iraq. People felt Tony Blair had blatantly lied about weapons of mass destruction, and that the whole thing was really about oil. That completely shattered the sense that Labour were somehow morally superior. Once that moral high ground was gone, some people basically thought, well, if they’re all as bad as each other, we might as well vote Conservative. It wasn’t just a policy disagreement, it was that feeling of being properly deceived that really did the damage.
