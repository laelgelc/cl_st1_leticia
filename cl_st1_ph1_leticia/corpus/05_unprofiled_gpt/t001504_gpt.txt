we don’t actually do degrees in *playing* sport, as such, but we do have a sport and exercise science degree, and that’s really a science course. It’s very much about anatomy, respiration, diet for fitness, training regimes, that sort of thing, rather than coaching a team. Those students would be the ones who might go on to do research placements. They might work with sports bodies, looking at performance or injury, or with the Civil Service, where their understanding of how servicemen cope under stress is directly relevant to the research being done.
