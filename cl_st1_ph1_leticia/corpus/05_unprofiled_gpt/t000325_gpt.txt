On twenty‑five grand here I just don’t feel like it’s worth it. You lose about twenty percent straight off in income tax, then another ten percent in National Insurance, and another ten percent again in student loans. On top of that there’s council tax and all the other bits, plus twenty percent VAT on what you actually buy. By the time you add it all up, it feels like I’m effectively paying about fifty percent in tax overall. So it’s just not cost‑effective compared to earning and saving elsewhere. Once I’ve got my PhD I can lecture on about forty grand a year, and that’s really the income bracket I need.
