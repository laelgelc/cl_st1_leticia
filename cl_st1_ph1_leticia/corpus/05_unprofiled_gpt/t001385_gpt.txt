I actually quite like watching what my hair’s doing as it goes grey. There’s this one streak that just keeps getting bigger as more of the hairs lose their pigment, and I find it fascinating seeing which ones decide to change next. I used to keep all the interesting ones, like the hairs that were half white and half brown, in what I called my “hair museum”. I’ve kind of stopped doing that now, though, because I find those odd half‑and‑half hairs so regularly it’s not special anymore.
