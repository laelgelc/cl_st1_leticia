I told him, “You used to drink in the lounge, didn’t you? You always had Manns Brown.” Then I said, “And you were always in a suit and tie, really smartly dressed.” I explained I’d worked behind the bar there, so I’d seen him a lot. He was absolutely gobsmacked that I could remember all that about him. He kept saying he was sorry that he couldn’t remember me at all, when I could even remember his drink and how he looked.
