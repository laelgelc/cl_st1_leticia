I felt really naughty after the Great North Run, because I totally skipped this insanely long Metro queue. There were literally thousands of people waiting, and I just thought, there is no way I’m standing in that. So instead of joining the back, I walked right up to the front of the queue area. There was a Caffè Nero nearby, so I nipped in there and bought myself a sandwich, kind of using that as my cover. Then, instead of going all the way back out and joining the end, I just slipped straight into the queue from there. I knew I was jumping in, but I just couldn’t face waiting with all those people.
