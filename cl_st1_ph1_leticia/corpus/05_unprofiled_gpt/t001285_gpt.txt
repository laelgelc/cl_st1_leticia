On the ship where I’ve been teaching, I’ve got to know one of the Filipino barmen who’s really keen on learning languages. We ended up chatting quite a bit, and I started teaching him some Greek and especially Turkish. Every morning I’d greet him with “gunaydin,” and he really enjoyed picking up those phrases. It’s been a fun little routine on board. The ship itself will be going around Southern Africa in the spring, which adds another interesting dimension to the whole experience.
