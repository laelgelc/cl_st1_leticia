As you get older you really do reach a point where you just don’t care what people think anymore. You either accept me as I am or you don’t, and I’m not going to twist myself into knots to please anyone. What really hurts, though, is when people who’ve leaned on me for years – whether it’s for my academic strengths, or the more spiritual and even metaphysical things I seem to have a knack for – suddenly turn round and say I’m egotistical or full of myself. They’ve happily benefited from those gifts, and then act like I’m the problem. That’s very hurtful.
