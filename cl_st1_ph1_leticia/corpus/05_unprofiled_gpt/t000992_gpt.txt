Well remember, the state pension is on top of whatever private pension you’ve got, so your mum should be okay, she’ll have both coming in. It’s your private pension that’s going to be rubbish, really. With private pensions you have to put in an awful lot of money for what you get back. For example, if you had a pot of about a hundred and twenty thousand, you’d only get roughly six thousand a year from it, and that’s without any index-linking. And I very much doubt you’ve actually got that much saved.
