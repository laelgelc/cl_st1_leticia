I just keep coming back to how my parents were by then – they’d already had enough, really, and they just sort of overlooked it. I could pick up tunes on the piano so easily when I was little, and nobody really clocked that it was a proper talent. Later on, when I finally did get into formal lessons as a teenager, I just lost all motivation. There were too many distractions, and it was hard work, and I didn’t push through it. Now it feels like such a deep regret. I get really frustrated with myself about it – it feels almost criminal not to be using that part of my brain. It’s like being illiterate, having this thing and not doing anything with it.
