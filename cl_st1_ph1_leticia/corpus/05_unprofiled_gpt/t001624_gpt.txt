We were decorating this house once, doing all the painting, and the bloke who owned it started telling us about this ridiculous problem he’d had with the gas company. For years he’d been trying to pay his gas bill properly, and they just wouldn’t have it. They kept insisting there was no gas meter in the house at all, as if he was making it up. He actually took us to where the meter was, perfectly visible, and said he’d even offered them the reading over the phone. Still, every time he called, they told him there was no gas meter there, no gas account, nothing to pay, and he just couldn’t get them to accept it existed.
