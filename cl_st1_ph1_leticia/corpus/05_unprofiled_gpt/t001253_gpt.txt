I did find his ideas genuinely interesting, actually, and I’m thinking I might try to put a couple of them into practice at the summer school. One of the main things he suggested was building in certain consistent elements to every lesson. For example, having each teacher always write a list of ten vocabulary words in the same place on the board. And then, right at the beginning of the lesson, putting three clear lesson aims at the top of the board. He also talked about colour uniformity, so we always use the same colours for the same kinds of things.
