Overly warm temperatures really are a problem for me. In my last place I didn’t even pay any bills, but we had no control over the heating at all. It was all centrally managed for the whole building, and they kept it very high. I actually prefer it when the air is cold and I’m just warm under the duvet. Instead, all winter I had to sleep with a fan on and only a sheet over me, because it was just much too hot all the time.
