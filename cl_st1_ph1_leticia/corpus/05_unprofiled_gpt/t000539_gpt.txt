so the story goes that in Woolpit these village children were out and they suddenly came across this brother and sister who had green skin. they brought them back, and the boy was really sad and never settled, and in the end he died. the girl, at first, would only eat green beans, nothing else at all, but gradually she started to eat normal food. as she did that she lost the green colour in her skin, and she began to learn to speak like everyone around her. once she could talk properly she explained that they’d come from a place that was always dark, and they’d followed the sound of music and somehow come out into the sunlight. eventually she grew up and even married someone local.
