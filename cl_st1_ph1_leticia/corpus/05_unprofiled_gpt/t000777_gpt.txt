It went on for ages, this general assembly, and I only sat through part of it before I escaped. Anyway, one of my colleagues had decided he was going to make this big principled stand, protesting about the organisation taking four, four and a half million from the tobacco companies. So, knowing what he was planning, I left a couple of little messages to be read out in my absence, very polite but absolutely dripping with sarcasm. I thanked him profusely for pointing out to us all that smoking is, in fact, unhealthy, as though none of us could possibly have guessed without his noble intervention.
