He's a bit better than he used to be, I’ll give him that, but the other day his room was just ridiculous again. There were clothes piled all over the floor, stuff scattered across his desk, just a complete mess. I told him to tidy it up, and when I looked in later it actually seemed like he had – from the doorway it looked much neater, like he’d done what I’d asked.

But then you said to me, “Have you actually looked inside?” and I realised I hadn’t gone any further than the landing. So I went back and had a proper look. He hadn’t put anything away at all. He’d just shoved the whole lot into the corner of his bed up by his pillow and then pulled the quilt over it so it looked fine from the hall. From where you walk past, you couldn’t see a thing, it just looked like a made bed.

So basically, he’d just lifted everything straight off the floor and hidden it in his bed, instead of actually tidying it.
