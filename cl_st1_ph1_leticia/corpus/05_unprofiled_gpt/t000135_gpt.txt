I get to this hotel and it’s all smoking, like properly a smoking hotel, and my room just reeks of smoke. It’s horrible. So I think, right, I need to get out, get some fresh air and find some food. I head out and it’s just this totally empty industrial estate, no one around, just warehouses and car parks. I’m walking and walking, starting to wonder where on earth I’m going to eat. 

At one point I pass this petrol station and I’m actually considering just grabbing some crisps or a sandwich from there, because there’s literally nothing else. But I decide to keep going, end up walking two, maybe three miles through this weird mix of residential streets and commercial units. Still no proper shops, nothing that looks like somewhere you’d actually want to sit down and have dinner. 

Then finally, out of nowhere, there’s this one Italian restaurant, just sitting there on its own. No little high street, no other cafés or anything, just this lone Italian place in the middle of all these houses and random businesses.
