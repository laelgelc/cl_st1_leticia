Miami was the worst for it though. We were down on South Beach when Radio One were out there doing some big thing, so it was all kicking off, music blaring, loads of people about. We’d been drinking pretty much all day, just the two of us to start with, and we got properly, properly drunk. By the time the others came and met us we were in a right state. I ended up being so bad I was just projectile vomiting on the beach, like full-on, not subtle at all, just sick everywhere on the sand. It was one of those nights where it was brilliant fun but also completely grim by the end.
