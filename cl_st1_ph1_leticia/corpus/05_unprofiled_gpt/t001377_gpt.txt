it was actually really straightforward, there was no zoom involved at all. I’d pulled into this wee snowy car park at the start of Loch Luneck, just north of Callander. I parked up, grabbed my camera bag and tripod, and wandered down to the waterfront to set the tripod up and see what I could get of the loch in the snow.

While I was faffing about getting everything sorted, this robin just appeared out of nowhere and landed on the wall right next to my bag. It was really close, just a couple of feet away, so I just froze and stayed as still as I could. Because it was that close I didn’t need any zoom, I just quietly lifted the camera and fired off a couple of shots. Managed to get those close-ups you saw before it eventually decided it’d had enough and flew off.
