I really do wonder how big an issue these refugee‑related sexual assaults actually are. My Polish acquaintance and my student both talk as if it’s this massive, almost universal problem, like the majority of refugees are rapists, and I just don’t buy that at all. When I look at the UK media, the emphasis feels very different: there’s far more focus on how to welcome refugees, how to integrate them, how to support them in settling in. And then you’ve got some of the Scandinavian countries – I think Denmark is one – that are actively looking for young refugee workers, trying to bring them in because they need people. So it really seems to me that different national media choose particular emphases, highlight particular aspects, for their own political purposes, rather than because that’s the whole reality.
