yeah so they actually got us all on the plane and everything, and then suddenly they were like, “right, everybody off.” We had to get off and they shoved us into this tiny little waiting room. We were in there for about five hours, no toilets, no water, no food, no information, nothing. And you could still see all these men in orange overalls crawling all over the outside of the plane, just carrying on working.

People started getting really fed up, and it turned into this huge mutiny, everyone shouting and demanding basic stuff, like, “we need water, we need to go to the toilet, what’s going on?” We were basically stuck there for about twenty hours in total, just waiting around and being told nothing.

Eventually they finally let us out, gave us free food and all that, and then in the end they just cancelled the flight anyway. And I’m convinced the only reason they did anything at all was because everyone complained so loudly together. If we’d all just sat there quietly, they would’ve just left us.
