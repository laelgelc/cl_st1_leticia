well I suppose it all comes down to people’s needs and personalities really. Some people are absolutely focused on being near good transport links, like an airport or a mainline station, because that convenience really matters to them and they want to be able to get places quickly and easily. Other people, though, don’t see it as such a priority and are quite happy to live further away, even if it means a longer journey now and then. So transport does have an impact, but it isn’t the deciding factor for everyone.
