if it says dialects then they're almost certainly talking about how it's spelt in different dialects, not about the accent as such. When people are talking about pronunciation properly they usually switch to the International Phonetic Alphabet. That's just one big phonetic chart that covers all the sounds in every language. Each sound has its own symbol, some of them look a bit like Greek letters. The point of it is, if you know that alphabet you can look at those symbols and pronounce any language in the world.
