if you were trying to do this work in a totally new field, I think you’d need to be really deliberate about how you throw yourself into it. You’d have to actually put yourself in situations where you can ask the right questions, not just sit at home guessing. I’d be looking to pull together a small, unfamiliar but carefully chosen team, even if I didn’t know their world that well yet. Then I’d give them one clear, simple task and a tight timeframe – like, “let’s get this done in a week.” And I’d want everyone in the same place, working side by side, so I could watch, listen, and join in. In that one week you’d soak up a huge amount of knowledge just by being there with them.
