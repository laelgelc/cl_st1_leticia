I honestly think some of these different levels in the system need to be taken out. After working for the NHS for years, including at a hospice, I was forever being dragged into these strategic health authority meetings. We had to justify everything using this traffic light system. If you’d got things in place with your trust, you were either on a red light, an amber light, or a green light. It was all about service improvement, so I’d go along, sit in front of a panel, and be questioned about what we were doing.
