Loads of people have golden retrievers in houses way smaller than ours, though. Think about Nan and Grandad’s big dog – and their place isn’t exactly massive. Space-wise I don’t think it’d be a problem. If you really want a Sussex spaniel I’m not against it at all; my only worry is how boisterous it’d be. I’d be a bit concerned about it playing rough with an older, smaller dog, especially once a golden retriever got big and maybe started making the older one grumpy.
