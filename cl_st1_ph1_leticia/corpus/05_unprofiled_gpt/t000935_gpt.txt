The idea is that you sit with a participant and ask, “Who helped you understand academic integrity?” Then you get them to talk about those people and the relationships involved. After that, you ask whether one of those people might be someone we could approach for an interview. If they say yes, the story moves forward; if they say no, it stops there. So the story either progresses or it doesn’t, but it’s always driven by the people themselves.
