I used to absolutely love Lidl when I was in Northern Ireland. It was amazing there – they always had really good new stuff coming in, and the quality was excellent. I’d go especially for the organic vegetables, they were so good and really affordable. But the Lidl here just isn’t the same at all. The things in that middle section now are mostly really cheap, poor-quality stuff. Every time I touch anything it seems to be polyester, and it gives me static shocks, which drives me mad. So I hardly ever buy anything from those middle-section offers anymore. In contrast, I think Biedronka is brilliant. I always call it the “Polish Lidl” because it kind of fills that role for me here. Their products are such good quality, and lots of them are actually made in Poland, which I really like.
