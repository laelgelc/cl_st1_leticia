So I finally got to go to this place I’d been wanting to go to all summer, right, and I went with my boyfriend. It was just us, something we’d been meaning to do for ages. Then my mum comes back, hears about it, and instantly kicks off that we should’ve waited so we could all go “as a family.” She acted like I’d somehow wronged her personally by going without her, like she was actually offended that I’d done something as a couple.

It’s like she can’t stand the idea that I have my own life with him that doesn’t automatically include her. She reacts as if every single thing we do should be opened up into some big family outing. And it makes me think, what exactly does she expect? Is she supposed to be invited along to everything we do together? Even our private, intimate moments? The way she behaves, it’s as if she’d be put out if we didn’t. It just feels completely over the top and suffocating.
