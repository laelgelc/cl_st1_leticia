I’ve just got so close to these friends I met travelling. We were together every weekend for months, and we’ve done Japan, the Philippines, Malaysia, Ecuador, the States – all of that side by side. Now they’re coming to England to stay with me for three weeks, and it feels like we’ve built this whole other level of friendship I never really had here before. It’s made me think of it as almost a new “class” of friend. Because I don’t plan on living in England, it changes how I see my friends back home a bit, since our lives are heading in such different directions.
