When I was hostelling in New Zealand I met this really passive‑aggressive, arrogant guy from Essex, and he had this massive motorbike. His whole thing was that he’d rock up to a hostel and, before anything else, tell the managers he snored really badly. He’d say it in this way that made it sound like he was being considerate, like, “Oh, you don’t want complaints from other guests.” But the whole point was to get himself a private room at the shared‑room price. I just thought it was so selfish, trying to get something better for nothing. The annoying thing was, it usually worked.
