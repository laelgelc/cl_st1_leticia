It was such a good sleep-over, really classic stuff – we were drawing on each other’s faces and we had what we called a “midnight feast”. Basically we’d gone down to the Spa and bought loads of sweets, and then decided to eat them all in the middle of the night. My mum was not impressed, because we’d already brushed our teeth and she didn’t want us eating sweets after that. My parents were asleep upstairs, and every few hours they’d come down and ask us to be quieter, and I’d be all apologetic.
