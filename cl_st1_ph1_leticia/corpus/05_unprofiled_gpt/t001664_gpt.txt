I’m just remembering this whole ridiculous school saga. I’d had this on‑off crush on this boy since about year eight, but I always felt a bit rubbish next to my really slim, athletic friends. Then during Trident work experience in year nine or ten I just went for it – proper crash‑dieting, eating salads, going swimming twice a week. Over about two and a half months I lost something like two stone. When I came back to school he suddenly really fancied me. It honestly felt like one of those cheesy teen films – there was this big swoony moment, and then him and his best friend were both fighting over me.
