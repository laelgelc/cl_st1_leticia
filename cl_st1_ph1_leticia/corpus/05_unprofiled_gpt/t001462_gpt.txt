It's not like a Fox or CBN kind of thing at all; Serial is done by quite a small group of people, not some huge media company. With podcasts, if you want to get them on air you usually either have to pay yourself or get a sponsor to cover it. In Serial’s case they had Dropbox sponsoring it before, so that’s how they managed it. There’s a really short Dropbox ad right at the beginning, before the programme actually starts, and that’s it—just that one advertisement.
