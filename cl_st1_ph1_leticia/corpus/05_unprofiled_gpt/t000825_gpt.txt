I’d be quite reluctant to go in that direction, actually. If you’ve only got a very short slot, I think you’re better off just giving them a really tight teaser about what you can do, rather than trying to run some elaborate exercise. In a twenty or thirty minute session you haven’t got time to do everything, but you have got time to make people curious. You want them going away thinking, “What on earth is he talking about? How does that work?” so that they come back to you afterwards and ask. That way, you’re creating intrigue rather than trying to deliver the whole thing upfront.
