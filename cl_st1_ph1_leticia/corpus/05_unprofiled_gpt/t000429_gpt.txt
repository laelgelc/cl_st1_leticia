I honestly think a lot of it is down to how we live now. We’re all so obsessed with disinfecting everything that people’s tolerance has just dropped, and you see so many more allergies. I mean, I’ve got a really bad dust allergy myself. I used to be so sceptical about antihistamines, but they’ve made a massive difference – I used to be constantly sneezing with my eyes streaming, and now it’s so much better. But there are people now who seem to fall apart at the slightest thing. Look at peanut allergies, for example.
