I got involved because a friend of mine, who’s a PR agent, forwarded an email around asking if anyone was interested in this project. I thought it sounded very interesting, particularly as I’d just been about to apply for an oral history job funded by a Heritage Lottery grant for the Guild of Quilters. That connection really caught my eye. It prompted me to go along to an exhibition and sale they were having, and when I arrived there was this absolute crush of women of a certain age, all in candy‑coloured rainwear.
