I was walking past the other day and she was out the front watering her plants, so I said hello and asked how she was. She said she’d had an absolutely horrific eighteen months and had been in hospital. I told her I had heard she’d been in hospital, but only because the Avon lady had mentioned it when she was delivering, and I didn’t feel it was right to just turn up on the doorstep and say, “I’ve heard you’ve been in hospital,” when she hadn’t told me herself.
