In Kenya I was working in signals, codes and ciphers. I’d been sent out as the vanguard WRAF signals and ciphers officer, really to set up a section there. Before that I’d done a short spell in Egypt, and then I went on to Cyprus during the terrorism. The idea in Kenya was that they were anticipating opening up operations during terrorism, and I was sent ahead in that context, but in fact that particular plan never actually came to anything in the end.
