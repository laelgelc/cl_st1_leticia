I was there for what felt like forever, calling and calling, trying to coax the kittens out with a bit of milk and food, and I was honestly about to give up. Then, finally, one tiny kitten crept into my hand. We ended up keeping that one as our own cat. Once that first kitten came out, the others slowly followed. We gathered them all up and put them into a basket, and then the rest of the kittens were found really good homes.
