I did most of the planning for that weekend myself, really – all the travel and what I was going to do – but I didn’t sort out the accommodation. When I first looked, I just thought, this isn’t worth it, because everything I could find was about a hundred and fifty quid for one night. So I kind of parked it. Then someone emailed me a suggestion, a place they’d found, and I just sort of glanced at the website. I didn’t really pay proper attention to where it actually was, I just went, “Yeah, fine,” and booked it.
