I was sat in this office when I went to see the navy person, and I’m always the only girl there, so I’m just looking out the window at people walking past in Peterborough. They’re rough as fuck. The women especially – these stupidly high heels, tiny little skirts, like they’re going to work dressed like that, and their hair’s all bleached to death and broken. It’s actually quite shocking to look at; it just doesn’t look nice at all.

The mad thing is, that’s what was acceptable where I grew up. That was just the normal standard, and you didn’t even really question it. But I’ve been living round here for three years now and it’s made me soft about what I think is the general standard. Peterborough’s more like what I should be used to, really – that properly rough standard.

Here, yeah, you do get rough people, but you’d never see that kind of really extreme behaviour or that level of roughness. I actually feel relatively safe where I am now compared to what you see going on over there.
