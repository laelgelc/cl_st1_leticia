I did go back, you know, and once I was back there I stayed another seventeen years in the end. Funny thing is, I actually felt more when I left that place than I did the one I’d been at for twenty-five years, and I think it was because the others were still there then. There were four of us who were really close, and we all ended up going within about two years of each other. One of them, bless her, she died not long after. She had cancer, in her early forties, and I went to see her just before she died. That really stayed with me.
