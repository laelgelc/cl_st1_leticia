There were technically five places I could’ve gone to for Spanish and Chinese, but it never really felt like five. I ruled one out straightaway because I just didn’t want to live there at all, and there was another where I had family, but I kind of just ignored that option for some reason. So it basically ended up being a choice between two unis. I remember sitting in my friend’s living room with all these prospectuses spread out everywhere, just feeling completely unsure – where to go, what to study, what actually mattered most.
