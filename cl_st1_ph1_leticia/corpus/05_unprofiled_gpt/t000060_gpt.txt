well she sent me this message on Facebook saying she’d got a problem, because her fiancé wants his mum down in Essex to make all the cakes for the wedding. He wants a fruit cake, a chocolate biscuit cake and a sponge, and of course she was in a panic about how on earth to get them all here and stacked properly in time. So I said to her, look, the only way we can really do this is if his mum makes the fruit cake first and sends it up to me early, so I can ice it properly and get that one ready as the main tier. Then the chocolate biscuit and the sponge can come up the day before the wedding, I’ll ice those as well and we’ll use them as cutting cakes. To make it look like a proper tiered cake, I’ll put polystyrene tiers on top of the fruit cake so it all stacks nicely without worrying about the others being too fresh or too soft. She just came back saying I was amazing and she was so relieved it was actually possible.
