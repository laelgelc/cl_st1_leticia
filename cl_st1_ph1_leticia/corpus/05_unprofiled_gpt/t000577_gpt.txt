I do like yoga, but I use that P90X yoga video that’s about an hour and a half, and it just gets a bit boring and slow. It’s definitely harder than a standard yoga video, but I still end up speeding it up to one-and-a-half times so it only takes an hour. Twenty seconds is enough for me, so we just kind of speed it up a little bit.
