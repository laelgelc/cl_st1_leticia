Years ago I was on a train, going past these blocks of flats just around teatime, and I found myself staring into all the kitchen windows. I was wondering in real detail what everyone was actually cooking. Had they planned it earlier in the day, or decided on the way home? Had they taken the meat or whatever out that morning, or picked things up at the shops on the way back from work? I wouldn’t actually go and knock on their doors, of course, but that sort of curiosity about what and why people are cooking is just how my mind works.
