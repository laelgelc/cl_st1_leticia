I did a proper classic dad thing the other day. I went down to the shop without my glasses, bought a paper and then sat on a bench, but I could only read the headlines. I cycled all the way back home and then started panicking, thinking I’d lost my glasses because they weren’t in my bag. I’d completely forgotten I hadn’t taken them with me in the first place. It’s not like me at all, I’m usually really good at finding things.
