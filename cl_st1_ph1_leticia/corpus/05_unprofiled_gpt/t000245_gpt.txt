On my very first Avon round I was quite careful about how I did it. With every book I dropped through a door, I also left a little note saying I was just starting out. I put on it exactly which day I’d be back to collect the book, and which day I’d be delivering the next one. I added that if they didn’t want to be disturbed, they could just leave the book outside. I got all sorts of responses: some left the book out with a note saying no more, others said they didn’t want anything this time but to keep leaving the books.
