They also arranged a biopsy for me at the dental hospital, for something they’d found. If I’d had to go through an ordinary dentist, it would have cost an absolute fortune, so I was really grateful they sorted it that way. The result itself was fine, nothing sinister there, but the whole thing left me in a bit of a state. One cheek swelled right up afterwards, so my face was completely uneven. At the same time I was totally bald, so I felt like I looked absolutely ridiculous. Bald I could probably cope with on its own, but bald and with this lopsided, swollen face as well – that was just too much. I really didn’t feel I could go out on the high street looking like that.
