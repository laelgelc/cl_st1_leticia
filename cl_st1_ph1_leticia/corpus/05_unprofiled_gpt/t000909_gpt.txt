I took it first to that jeweller next to the YMCA shop, the one by Greggs. I only chose it because the sign outside mentioned watchsmiths, so I thought they’d know what they were doing. But when I handed the watch over and watched him examine it, I really didn’t feel confident. He didn’t seem to know what he was doing with it at all. Then he said it would be twenty pounds, and that actually made me more suspicious, because I thought that was far too little for the work needed. So I told him I’d take it somewhere else instead.
