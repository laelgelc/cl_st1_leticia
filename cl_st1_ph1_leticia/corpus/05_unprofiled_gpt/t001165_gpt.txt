She’s got all these food issues, so she has to have special bread, and for years she basically lived on this Co‑op fine cut lemon marmalade. She gets through about a jar every week or two. Then, of course, they stopped doing it. So I said to Mum, “Leave it with me.” I contacted the Co‑op, then tracked down the original producer and asked them to send me the full list of ingredients. I told Mum that once I knew exactly what was in it, I could try other marmalade makers and find something as close as possible so she could carry on having it.
