I was literally standing at this auction, sorting through someone’s dad’s belongings, when my phone kept buzzing with these huge, emotional texts from him. He was going on about how the Pope was lucky to have someone who truly loved him at his deathbed, and then asking who would be there for him when he dies. Mixed in with all that, he was calling me naïve and having a go at my faith, my religion, my beliefs. It was horrible, because while I’m trying to focus on this really sensitive task, I’m getting these intense messages that attack the core of who I am.
