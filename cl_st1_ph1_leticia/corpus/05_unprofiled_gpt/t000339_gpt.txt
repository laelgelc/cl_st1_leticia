well during the war they were in eastern Poland and the Russians came and took them all prisoner and they nearly shot my father because he was a land agent and he was in uniform and it was only because my mother begged and begged for his life that they didn’t shoot him and they were all sent off to Siberia and it was awful they had no shoes and there was a baby that died on the train on the way there and then later on when the Russians became allies the Polish men were allowed to form an army so my father went off with that and they were sent to North Africa and then they fought their way up through Italy and he was at Monte Cassino and the women and children were sent a different way my mother went through Kazakhstan and then down to North Africa as well and she worked in a hospital there and somehow in all that chaos with everybody scattered all over the place and so many displaced people they did eventually find each other again the Red Cross helped to track people and bring them back together and that was how my parents were reunited after all that time and all those journeys
