I remember that winter when the snow was really deep, I had to come down to milk the goats and I just couldn’t get the car out at all. I was walking down regularly then, and I’d often meet the doctor on his rounds, because he was walking everywhere as well. We used to joke about it when we passed, both of us trudging along in all that snow. When I got down near the water tower, there’s that treeless stretch of road, and the snow on the road there was about fifteen feet deep. It was incredible, you just couldn’t believe how much had come down.
