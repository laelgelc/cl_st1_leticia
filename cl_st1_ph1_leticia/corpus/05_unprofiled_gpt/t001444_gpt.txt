So I went home early, and then the next day I ended up walking about five miles into town to the police station to ask about my missing bag. I get there, and there’s this policewoman at the desk, and you can hear people in the office behind her kind of laughing. She looks at me and goes, “Oh yeah, we know who you are. You’re the ones who were skinny-dipping on the beach and got all your clothes stolen.” So clearly the whole thing had already gone round everywhere, like it had really widely circulated as this funny little local story before I’d even turned up to report the bag.
