We won that goldfish at a fair, you know, one of those you bring home in a little plastic bag. We popped it into a bowl and kept it on the sideboard for a while. When we moved, we really didn’t want to cart a goldfish around with us, so we gave it to my uncle, who had a proper fish pond in his garden. The cat was hilarious with it – he’d sit there pretending he wasn’t interested at all, then when he thought no one was looking he’d have a sneaky go at catching it, never successfully. In the end the pond was absolutely the best place for it; keeping a goldfish in one of those tiny round bowls is really quite cruel.
