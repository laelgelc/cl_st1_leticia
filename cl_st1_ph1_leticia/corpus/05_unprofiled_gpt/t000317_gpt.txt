For me the fundamental point is that all people are simply people. Wherever you go in the world, humans basically want the same things for themselves and their families: safety, warmth, comfort, shelter, food, community, relationships, and that sense of belonging to something bigger than themselves. Those are such basic, shared human needs. And yet there are so many things that divide us, or are used to divide us. I really feel that this division works as a kind of “divide and conquer”, keeping people at odds with each other. While poor people are busy fighting each other, they’re not able to focus on the issues that really matter.
