I don’t really mind living with my mum and dad, but I’m on my own a lot. They’re either away or asleep because of their shifts, so it’s just me rattling around the house. I end up going a bit stir-crazy, just sitting there applying for jobs over and over again. It gets to the point where I feel this really strong need to get out of the house, any excuse will do. 

So when my friend came down on Friday with her uni friends, I went out with them because they live nearby. That was such a relief, just to be out and around people for a change. It made a nice break from the whole being-stuck-at-home-and-job-hunting routine.
