I've never sold this house, and I’ve no intention of selling it, I don’t need to. If I ever did decide to, I’d have to do a lot of work on it first. Over the years I’ve had bits and pieces done by people who know what they’re doing – the roof was done, I’ve had double glazing put in, some of the floorboards sorted, an outside wall done, and the chimney as well. But I’m not a DIY expert at all, so there’s a limit to what I can realistically tackle myself. I’m quite aware that if you were seriously thinking of putting it on the market you’d probably want everything looking really smart, but as it stands I just haven’t gone down that route.
