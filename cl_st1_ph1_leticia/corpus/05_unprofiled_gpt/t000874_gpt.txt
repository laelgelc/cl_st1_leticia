I just find it unbelievable really, and very hard to accept, the way the church as an institution seems to have separated itself from Christianity. That fashion show in the church still amazes me when I think back on it. I remember walking along those boards over the pews, all the clothes being sold, and me buying this locally made patchwork skirt. I wore that skirt for years and years, it was such a good buy, but even so I kept thinking how odd it all was in a church. I still often ask my husband, “Would they really have allowed that at your church?” And it leads me on to thinking about other things with the church.
