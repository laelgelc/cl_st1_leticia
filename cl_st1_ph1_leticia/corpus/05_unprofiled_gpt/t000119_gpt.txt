At the beginning it really felt like a partnership, you know? We were all getting on, working together, and that made the job enjoyable. Then they changed the management, changed my role, brought in different people with very different characters. After that, a big part of the job became just adapting, fitting in with whoever you were thrown together with. When you’re actually at work you stay professional, you get on with people, you do the job and mostly it’s fine. But it’s only when you get home that you really let off steam about it all.
