After my bank account was hacked, I had a lot of trouble for a while, it was just ongoing hassle. The only good thing was the way the bank handled it. They were actually monitoring everything really carefully and blocking payments and standing orders that weren’t legitimate. Somehow they managed to work out exactly which transactions to stop, so nothing actually went through that shouldn’t have. Then I started getting all these letters, several every week, from the bank. Each one was asking for my signature to authorise a standing order I’d never even tried to set up. It was all a bit unsettling, but at least they kept checking before letting anything happen.
