When we finally moved, I sat them down and said, “Right, this is home now, this is where we’re going to be.” I really wanted them to feel a bit more settled. I also told them, “Look, you can think about going to the local school if you want.” I wanted them to have some say this time, because before it had all been, “Now we’re off to this relative’s, now we’re going into this refuge,” and they’d just get told, “Now you’re at this school.” They’d had so many sudden moves and no control, so I thought, at least here, they should have a choice.
