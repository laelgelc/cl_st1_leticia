In that tiny courtroom there were only four of us, so it was really obvious where everyone’s eyes were going. It wasn’t like a big crowd where you could pretend you’d misread things; you could absolutely tell when someone was staring. And that sort of behaviour, you’d expect it in pubs or clubs, where people are drinking and being flirty or whatever, but not in an office. Not anywhere professional, really, where you’re supposed to act professionally. You just wouldn’t do that there, and you certainly wouldn’t expect it in a court.
