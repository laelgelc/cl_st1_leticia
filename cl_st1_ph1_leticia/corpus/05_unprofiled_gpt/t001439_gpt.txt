In hospitals I find that visitors often come and talk to me, I think because of the environment and everything that’s going on. There was this one woman who really stuck in my mind. Her husband had died on the first of May, and it was only the second week of May when she spoke to me. She was so full of praise for the staff, saying how wonderful they’d been. She’d already decided she was going to raise funds by doing this garden event with paintballs and a white sheet.
