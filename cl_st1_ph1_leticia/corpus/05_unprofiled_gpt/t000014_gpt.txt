Well, it’s very much a recreational course rather than a formal, traditional art programme. We run it here at the Creative Innovation Centre, and the emphasis is on creativity and innovation instead of a standard “beginners learn to draw” kind of structure. It’s open to anyone, but in practice most of the people who come are mature students, and quite a few of them already have a fair bit of art education behind them. We’ve got about twenty people doing painting and drawing, and another twenty in the life drawing sessions. In feel, it’s probably closest to a foundation-type art school course.
