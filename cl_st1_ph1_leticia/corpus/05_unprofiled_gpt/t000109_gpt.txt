We had the film Beethoven's Fifth on over New Year, I think it was. You know, the one where the male St Bernard meets the female St Bernard, and then they have puppies that end up staying in the house. Holly was sat on the sofa between us while it was on. At first she was just sort of there, but once she realised that all the little whining puppy noises were actually coming from the television, she just became completely fascinated. She fixed on the screen and didn’t look away, properly watching it right up until the end of the film when the puppy sounds stopped. It was really sweet.
