I was watching this interview with Jason Robinson the other day, and the bloke interviewing him comes out with this absolutely daft question. He goes, “Do you remember missing that tackle on Jonah Lomu in the World Cup?” as if Robinson’s going to be sat there dwelling on one missed tackle from years ago. And Robinson, being really polite, just smiles and says, “Erm… yeah.” I was sitting there thinking, how on earth is he supposed to remember one tackle like that? It was such a stupid thing to ask.
