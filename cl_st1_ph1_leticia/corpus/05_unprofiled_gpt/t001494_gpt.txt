There was this thing online about supermarkets and returns that really took off. Someone had bought a packet of chocolate biscuits from Aldi, or maybe it was Lidl, and when they opened it they found that one of the biscuits didn’t have any chocolate on it. They were absolutely devastated about this single plain biscuit. They put a post up on social media about the whole thing, and it went completely viral because of how over-the-top and dramatic their reaction was to that tiny surprise.
