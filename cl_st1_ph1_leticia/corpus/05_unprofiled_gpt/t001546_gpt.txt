There really isn’t a shortage of houses, that’s the thing. I honestly don’t think people should be allowed to own more than two. Like, you have your main home, and then if you really want, a summer home or whatever, but that should be it. If there was that kind of limit, there’d be far fewer people forced into renting because there’d be more places actually available to live in, rather than just being owned. I know most people don’t have more than that, but there are definitely some who do.
