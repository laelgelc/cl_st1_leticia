it was actually a bit dangerous, you know, it wasn’t just inconvenient. I was already really annoyed from having to slog back and forth in that first typhoon, and then the next week, first day at my new school, it happens again. Another one – Tembin this time – just decides to roll in. 

I’m standing there thinking, brilliant, fresh start, and I can’t even get a taxi because of the storm. So I end up having to walk, like half an hour, in this ridiculous wind and rain, just being battered the whole way. Kept having to stop random people on the street to ask for directions because I didn’t really know where I was going yet. 

By the time I finally got there I was absolutely drenched, like completely soaked through, hair, shoes, everything. I was kind of hoping they’d take one look at me and be like, “Oh my god, here, have a change of clothes or something,” you know, just a bit of sympathy. But no, nothing like that at all.
