I mean, I’ve never had this illness while I’ve been in a proper work situation before, so I’ve got nothing to go on. When I went to see the specialists, they were actually really surprised that I’d had no help at all. They said they’d have to properly review everything at the end of this little programme I’m on – it’s only three appointments, and then they’ll look at my situation again and see what needs to happen next.

What really hit me is how different everyone else at the clinic seems. A lot of them aren’t working at all, some of them can barely move, and there are people who are literally bed bound. For some of them, just going to see the doctor once is enough to completely wipe them out and make them really ill.
