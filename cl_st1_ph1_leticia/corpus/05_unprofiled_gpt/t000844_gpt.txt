I was thinking about a friend of mine who was a civil servant in a government department. She was always the first to know what was going on, and it wasn’t because of her job title. Ministers would wander into the smoking room for a cigarette – even the secretary of state and other very senior people. She’d be in there too, having a smoke and chatting away, and she’d pick up all sorts of information that was way above her pay grade. It made me think that sometimes it’s definitely worth being a smoker.
