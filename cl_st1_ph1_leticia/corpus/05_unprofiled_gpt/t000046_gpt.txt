I think this idea could work really well because it shifts the focus from people just arriving with problems they want solved. Instead, they’d be coming with experiences they want to develop by sharing their stories. That feels much more constructive and engaging. Once those stories are out there, other people—because we’re naturally social—would want to get involved, contribute, and add layers to what’s already been said. It becomes a kind of collaborative story-building, rather than a one-way problem-dumping exercise.
