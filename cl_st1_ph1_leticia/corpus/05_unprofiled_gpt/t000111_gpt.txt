When my older one was very small, and I probably had the baby in a sling, I actually let her wander off in British Home Stores. I stayed close, secretly following and hiding behind the clothes rails, just watching. I was waiting for that moment when she’d suddenly go, “Oh, mummy,” turn around and realise she couldn’t see me. You could see the fear starting to appear in her face. Just before it tipped into absolute terror, I came out from behind her and said, “Did you lose mummy?” She spun round and threw her arms round my neck. It really taught her a lesson about wandering off, even though I did feel quite mean.
