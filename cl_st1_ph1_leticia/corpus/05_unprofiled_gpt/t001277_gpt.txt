when individual words are spoken in a stream, it suddenly becomes much harder to understand than when you see them written down in isolation. So I use this little activity to make that point. I take a sentence that was actually misheard in a real meeting, and I put up on the screen what someone thought they’d heard. Then I show them a series of different “misheard” versions in sequence, and I ask them to guess what the original speaker really said. We go through these alternatives, and only at the end do I reveal the correct final version of the sentence.
