I’d actually heard about those public beheadings before, from one of my patients’ daughters. She was a young air hostess, and she told me this story that really stayed with me. On one of her trips she’d gone to the marketplace to buy some gold, just doing a bit of shopping, and suddenly there in front of her a woman was brought out and beheaded, right there in the street. It happened right in front of her, this young girl, and it disturbed her so much she said it unsettled her and she never wanted to go back there again.
