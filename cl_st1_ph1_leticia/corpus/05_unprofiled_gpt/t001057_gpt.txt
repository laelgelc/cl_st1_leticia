I really do feel very strongly about this now. I just don’t think a servicewoman should be having babies while she’s serving, because you simply can’t put the service first if you’ve a young child, and that means your colleagues end up carrying an unfair share of the work. In my day there was that rule that you couldn’t join if you had a child under fifteen, and I always thought that was fair enough. Younger children need a parent to put them first, and your career ought to come second to that.
