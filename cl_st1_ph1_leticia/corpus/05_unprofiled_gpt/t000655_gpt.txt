When I was writing it all up, I went back to this idea I’d had of “cyber provincialism”. At first I’d thought of it as these game worlds being totally closed off from reality, like little sealed provinces where nothing from outside really gets in. But then I had to rethink that, because you do get things like Occupy Wall Street and Occupy London turning up in Minecraft. So real-world stuff does actually enter the game universe, which complicates that whole neat separation I’d imagined at the start.
