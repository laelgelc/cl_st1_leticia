It was this funny sort of van, really – it didn’t even have proper seats, more like a little living area in the back that we all piled into. Our driver was absolutely huge, Viking‑sized, and he spent so much time ferrying the band around that his girlfriend hardly ever saw him. He’d always turn up to pick us up to go out for dinner, totally reliable. And the band were so sweet – they invited me along and covered everything, so I basically got to go for free, all expenses paid.
