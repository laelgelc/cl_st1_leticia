They actually had to move out of their old place on either New Year’s Day or Christmas Day, I can’t remember which. I said to them, you know, “Just ask the landlady if you can have a few extra days,” because no one’s going to be moving in on those days, are they? It just seemed obvious to try and get a bit of overlap so they didn’t have to rush everything.

But in the end they didn’t do that at all. They went ahead and moved everything on New Year’s Eve and New Year’s Day. I went over and helped them shift stuff, lugging boxes and bits of furniture back and forth. It was all a bit mad timing-wise, but we got it done.

Their new place is lovely, though. It’s in this really tiny little settlement, only about three houses, just beyond the village. It feels very quiet and tucked away.
