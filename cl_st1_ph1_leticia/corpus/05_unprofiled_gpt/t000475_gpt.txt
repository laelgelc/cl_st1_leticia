I saw this bloke about a year ago when I was standing in H&M, and he had this tattoo on his arm. It was just like a fairly ordinary landscape, nothing mad in terms of subject, but it was absolutely flawless. It looked completely painterly, like it had literally been painted onto his skin. There was no outline at all, it just sort of blended in, and I couldn’t stop staring at it. I honestly couldn’t tell if it was real or not because it looked so perfect.
