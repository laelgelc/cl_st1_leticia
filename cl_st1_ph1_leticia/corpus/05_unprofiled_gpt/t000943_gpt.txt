They’ve really got quite a fixed idea about how lessons should work. I was thinking of that Korean friend of X’s, you know, the one who’d been here eight years and still didn’t speak English. She didn’t want proper lessons at all. What she wanted was for me to sit down and write out all these model answers for her, like stock replies for typical situations. So if a customer at work said, “How much is this?” she’d have a ready‑made answer on a bit of paper instead of actually learning the language.
