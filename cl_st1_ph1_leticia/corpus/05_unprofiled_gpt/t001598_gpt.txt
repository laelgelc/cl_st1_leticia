I do think alcoholism comes from all sorts of different directions, not just one neat cause. Take her, for example – she stayed with that bloke who treated women badly, and she just didn’t seem to have any self‑respect left. You look at that and you think, why on earth would you stay, what’s going on in your head to make that feel normal? And then I look at myself and my own drinking, and I can see a similar sort of pattern, really. I was drinking because I thought somehow it would give me a bit of self‑respect, or at least blot out the feeling that I didn’t have any. But of course that’s the mad thing, isn’t it – the more you drink like that, the more you actually lose whatever self‑respect you had.
