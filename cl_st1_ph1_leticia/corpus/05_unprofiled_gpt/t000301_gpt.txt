I remember this David Attenborough programme where he went into this cave that was just absolutely full of guano, like really thick on the floor, and he was talking about the bats’ sonar. He was explaining how, even though there were hundreds of bats flying incredibly close to his head, their sonar meant they wouldn’t actually hit him. And then, as he was saying all this, one of the bats just flew straight into his face anyway, completely contradicting what he’d just been describing.
