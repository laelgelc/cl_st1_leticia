And he just read and read, like literally all the time. By the time the three weeks were up he’d got through about twelve books. I only managed five, and then I started secretly taking his when he’d finished and reading those as well. He never minded, he was just so good-natured about everything, always really happy, just a really cheerful kid to have in the bunk.

Then there was this other boy in our bunk who was chubby, and he was also just ridiculously happy. Like, he never got angry, never seemed stressed about anything the whole three weeks we were living together. So you had these two lads, totally different in some ways, but both just completely content and easygoing the entire time.
