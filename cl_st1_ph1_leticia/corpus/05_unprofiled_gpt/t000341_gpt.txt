When I was helping the German teacher at my old school, we tried something a bit different with the students who’d actually chosen to do German. I used to take them out of the classroom one by one and sit with them, just going through the exam questions together. It was interesting, because as soon as they were on their own with me they’d start off saying they didn’t know anything at all. But then, as we went through the questions, they gradually realised they did actually know the material after all.
