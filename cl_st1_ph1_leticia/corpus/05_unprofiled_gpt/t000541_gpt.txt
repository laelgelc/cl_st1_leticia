yeah I do really like MasterChef, it’s fun to watch, and I like that you don’t really know what they’re going to come up with next. The cooking’s exciting because they’ve just got that invention thing at the start and you’re thinking, you know, what are they going to do with it. But I’m not so keen on what they’ve done with the new series. 

They’ve changed it so that straight after that first invention task they’re suddenly shoved into some really posh restaurant kitchen. It just feels like too much of a jump. One minute they’re doing something really basic, like beans on toast sort of level, and then the next they’re expected to be turning out foie gras and all that fancy stuff. It’s a bit ridiculous, going from really simple home cooking straight into that kind of hardcore professional cooking.
