It reminds me of that interminable dot-com presentation I sat through, where they showed this photo that really stuck with me. It was of a woman’s daughter and her friend in a kitchen somewhere in North America. The two girls were both completely hunched over their mobile phones, just texting away. What struck me was that their backs were actually to each other, so there was absolutely no real interaction going on between them at all. They might as well have been on another planet, given how cut off they seemed from each other despite being in the same room.
