We were both really ambitious at school, you know, like actually enjoying working hard and getting good marks for ourselves. But our parents kept turning everything into this massive competition. Every set of exam results, it was all, “What did she get? What did you get?” and constant comparing. It stopped being about us being proud of what we’d done and became about who was “better.” I ended up feeling really frustrated, like I had to apologise for not being perfect, or for doing well. It made me feel guilty that my marks were causing tension, especially when it started affecting my friendships, which just really upset me.
