I had this proper little panic at the petrol station the other day with the new car. I’m stood there filling it up and it just keeps taking more and more fuel, and the pump’s not clicking off at all. I’m watching the numbers go past fifty litres and thinking, hang on, my old car’s tank was only forty-five litres and I usually only ever put about forty in. So I got to about fifty-two litres and just stopped, because I was convinced something was wrong and I was paying for loads of fuel I didn’t actually need. It really threw me, I had no idea what was going on with it.
