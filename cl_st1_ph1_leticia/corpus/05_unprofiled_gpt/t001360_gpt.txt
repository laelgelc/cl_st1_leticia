I put this toffee sponge in the microwave for about twenty minutes, way longer than you’re meant to, so the toffee went properly molten and was bubbling. When I took it out, this big blob of toffee just dropped straight onto my hand and basically encased three of my fingers. It hardened on the outside almost instantly, but the inside was still completely molten. I ran to the tap and shoved my hand under freezing cold water, trying to get it off, but the water just made the outer layer go even harder.
