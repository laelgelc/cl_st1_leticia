I was so jealous of him, you know, because he’d started teaching me piano and he was just naturally brilliant. In music class I was really proud and showed her this piece I was slowly learning, that “River Flows in You” one. It was hard for me, I was taking ages with it. Then the next week she comes in and suddenly she can play the whole thing perfectly. Everyone was like “wow, that’s amazing,” and I just sat there feeling completely deflated.
