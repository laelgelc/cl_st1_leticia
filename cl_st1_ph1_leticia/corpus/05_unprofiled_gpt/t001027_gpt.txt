I had that really weird moment the other week when everyone went bowling and then on to play pool, and I just felt… boring. I ended up leaving early because I couldn’t get into it at all. It’s not that I don’t know how to have fun sober – I can, I’ve done it before – but right then it was like I just wasn’t able to. I think it was probably part of the whole detox thing, you know, my head and body still adjusting.

What I’m noticing more and more is that I just don’t care about the whole drinking scene anymore. Especially that point in the night when it all escalates – someone orders a tray of shots, then another, and suddenly it’s not even about being together, it’s just about getting smashed. That’s the bit that really puts me off now.

In a funny way, drugs seem better on that front, because at least you don’t wake up with that horrible hangover. With booze, it’s like you’re punished twice: once during the stupid bit of the night, and then again the next morning.
