So the idea was that the two sisters and the two brothers‑in‑law and me would all share this little holiday place in France. The arrangement was that my brother‑in‑law would actually stump up the money to buy it, and I’d put in the work doing it up, so it became a sort of shared project between us all. 

We were sitting in the estate agent’s office, waiting around because the farmer who owned the place we were meant to see was busy and couldn’t get there until six. While we were hanging about, the agent said, well, since you’re here, would you like to see another place? He described it as a farm with a water mill, some barns, a house and a stream running through it. We thought, why not, we’re here now, so we agreed to go and have a look at it.
