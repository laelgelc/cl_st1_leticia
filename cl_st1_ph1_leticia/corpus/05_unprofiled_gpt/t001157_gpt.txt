I knew you’d like the paella, and I thought it’d be great because there’d be enough left for the next day as well. So I went up to the fish counter and said I was making paella, and that I’d already thought of a couple of obvious things to put in it. Then I asked what else I should add. The guy there got really enthusiastic and started suggesting all sorts of things, including octopus, and he even began taking some out for me.
