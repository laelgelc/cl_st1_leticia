I mean, I always say I’m a really bad cook, but for a while I felt like some kind of master chef just by comparison. A mate of mine had just come over from South Africa and literally everything had always been done for him there, so he’d never learned to cook at all. When he moved over, I ended up showing him really basic stuff in the kitchen – like how to do simple things with a pan, or add a few bits to make something taste better – and he was absolutely amazed by it. It was quite funny, because I still thought I was useless, but next to him I suddenly looked like I knew exactly what I was doing.
