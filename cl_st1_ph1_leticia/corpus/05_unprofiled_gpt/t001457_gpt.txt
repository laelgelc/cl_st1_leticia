My next–door neighbour was vegetarian for, what, ten, fifteen years, really strict with it, and then one day we were all at this barbecue and she just suddenly said she really fancied a sausage. So she had one, just like that, and from then on she was back on meat, no big announcement or anything. Nobody said a word to her about it, nobody had a go or questioned her, she just changed what she was doing and carried on. I do think it’s up to each person whether they’re vegetarian or not, but it did seem a bit odd how quickly she switched.
