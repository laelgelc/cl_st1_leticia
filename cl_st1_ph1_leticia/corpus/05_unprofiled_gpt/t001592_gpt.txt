I remember when I used to stay at Grandma and Grandad’s during the air raids, just lying in bed. They’d call us to go to the air raid shelter, and I’d be up and down, up and down, before finally deciding I’d just go over to my mum’s instead. Over there, the houses were absolutely full of black cockroaches. You were literally walking on black bugs. Everybody had those black bugs in their home; it was really awful, everywhere you went.
