It's a really tricky situation, because if you think about it, how many landlords are actually going to say, “Oh yeah, I’ll happily rent my nice two–bedroom flat to somebody who’s getting free housing?” They’re going to assume that person’s unemployed, probably got problems, and they’ll just worry the place is going to get trashed. So that puts them right off offering decent properties. Instead it kind of pushes things towards these rogue landlords, who are happy to let out really substandard places because they figure it’ll get wrecked anyway.
