from about October through to March I was working in this shop and it was absolutely freezing, because we had to keep the door open all the time. my boss was really obsessed with having the door open, like it was all about getting customers in, so even when it was properly cold we just had to stand there with the icy air coming in.

then we had that period when it was snowing, and it finally got so bad that we were actually allowed to shut the door. we even put up this little sign saying “we’re just keeping warm,” which we’d copied from somewhere else. it felt like such a luxury just to have the door closed and not be shivering the whole time.

it was around then that I got completely hooked on this baked potato from the place nearby. they did this topping that loads of students ordered, and I was told they all went for chicken curry and cheese. so I tried it, and it was just amazing. you’ve got the hot potato, loads of butter, the melted cheese, and then this mildly spicy chicken curry on top. not too hot, just a nice bit of spice. it all sort of melted together – cheese, butter, hot potato and the curry – and it became my absolute favourite combination.
