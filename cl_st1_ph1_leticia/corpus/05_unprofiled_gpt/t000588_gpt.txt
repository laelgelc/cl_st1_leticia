The guided tours up into the hills were just way beyond what we could afford. They wanted twenty‑five dollars for the guide, and then another twenty‑five dollars for every extra person, even though you’re only getting the one guide. It just felt ridiculous when you think about what we were actually living on day to day. Our whole daily budget was about fifteen to twenty dollars, so blowing fifty or more just to walk up a hill with someone didn’t make any sense for us at all.
