I actually thought The Boat That Rocked was really funny, even though everyone kept saying it was rubbish. It’s got such a good cast in it – you know that guy who’s having a baby with Sienna Miller is in it, and Chris O’Dowd as well. It’s all set in the sixties, and the music is just brilliant all the way through. They’re on this pirate radio ship where they literally just drink, take drugs, and play music all day. I was watching it thinking, I want my life to be like that.
