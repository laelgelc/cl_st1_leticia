I saw this pie chart online about reasons why women get raped, and it literally just had one big section: “the existence of rapists.” The whole thing was basically saying it’s entirely the man’s fault, end of story, which, on one level, is obviously true – if there weren’t rapists, it wouldn’t happen. But it bugged me a bit, because it felt too simple. I do think girls don’t help themselves sometimes, and that part gets completely ignored in that kind of message. It’s like there’s no room to talk about any other factors at all.
