Yeah, it was really good television, I loved it, though I don’t think we always watched all of Scooby-Doo. It is funny, I’m not really sure why we went round to my gran’s, because my mum wasn’t working then. Maybe it was just sort of arranged that my gran would look after us a couple of nights a week. We literally just went there, watched TV, and then went home, we didn’t even have dinner there. Maybe my parents just hadn’t paid their TV licence.
