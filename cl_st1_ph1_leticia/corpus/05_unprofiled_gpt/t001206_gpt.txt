There’s this thing they give you after meals in India that I absolutely hated. It’s this really common seed-and-sugar digestif, tastes really strongly of aniseed, and you get it everywhere, all over the country. They serve it in this kind of plant-like container at the end of the meal, and I’ve never seen anything like it outside India. The sugar’s often got ants in it, so they pour some into your hand and you’re there blowing the ants out before you eat it. It just completely put me off, that whole combination of the aniseed taste and the ants and everything.
