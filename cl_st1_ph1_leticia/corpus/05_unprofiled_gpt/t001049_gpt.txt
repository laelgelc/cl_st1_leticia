when I first went to Spain I didn’t know any Spanish at all, and they put me straight into teaching this English class of six and seven year olds. At the beginning it was actually fine, because they sort of assumed I understood them. But as soon as they twigged that I didn’t speak Spanish, they just started playing up and messing around, because they realised I couldn’t follow what they were saying or doing. It got pretty chaotic for a bit. Eventually though they worked out that, even if they misbehaved, I was still going to respond in English, which they didn’t understand either, so there wasn’t much point in trying to take advantage of it.
