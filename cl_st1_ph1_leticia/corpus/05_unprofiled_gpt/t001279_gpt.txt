But what she was saying, which I found really striking, is that in so many conflicts you simply cannot get the two sides in the same room. And yet the standard conflict resolution model always starts from “bring everyone together round the table”. So she and people like her have had to work out ways of doing it where you only ever work with one side. Which is a bit like what I always say – if one person just refuses to keep arguing, the other person can’t actually carry on the argument on their own.
