Living abroad’s really made me rethink how friendships work. It feels a bit like plate spinning: you’ve got all these different friends and family members and you have to consciously keep each plate going, or it just wobbles and falls. I’m always aware I need to keep in touch with this person, then that person. But then, even here in England, it’s not that different. My mum’s got someone who lives just round the corner and she hasn’t actually been round since Christmas.
