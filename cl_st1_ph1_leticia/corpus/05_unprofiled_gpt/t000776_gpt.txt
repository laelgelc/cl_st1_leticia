yeah I got some odd looks on parents’ day, but most people just called me by my nickname anyway. A lot of people at camp end up with really strange nicknames the kids give them. There was this one boy in my bunk who everyone called Skittles. He got it because we were playing this basketball game called knockout, and he was wearing loads of different colours. He ended up winning the game, and after that everyone started calling him Skittles, like the sweets, because of all the colours he had on.
