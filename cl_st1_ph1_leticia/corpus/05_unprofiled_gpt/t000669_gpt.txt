We literally just went in, found them, and said, “Have a lovely day,” and explained about my dad, that he wasn’t there because he was ill. I felt awful about that, but there was nothing else we could do.

We didn’t hang around at all. A couple of people said, “Look, just go home, don’t worry about staying,” and they were saying not to fuss about the food either, not to pay for what we weren’t going to eat. So we took them at their word and left quite quickly.

On the way back I stopped off and got a Chinese for the boys, so at least they were sorted and had something decent to eat. I didn’t have anything myself; I just wasn’t in the mood to eat by then. That’s how it all ended up that night.
