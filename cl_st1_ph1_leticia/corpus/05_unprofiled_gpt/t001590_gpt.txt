we'd been in the shelter and it had gone very quiet, no more anti-aircraft guns, no bombers about, and you could tell the German plane had gone. then suddenly there was this huge explosion, nothing like what we’d heard before. we just stayed put till the all-clear went, listening, wondering what on earth it was.

once the all-clear sounded, me and this other lad, he was about fourteen and I was eleven, decided we’d go and see what had happened. we thought it must’ve been right down at the end of the avenue, the way it sounded. when we got out, there wasn’t shouting or anything, just this sort of low babble, people talking very quietly, almost in whispers, as we walked towards where we thought the blast had been.
