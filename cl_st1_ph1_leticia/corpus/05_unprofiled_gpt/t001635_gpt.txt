well I think the core of it is what kind of society you actually want to live in. There are some basic things that are just fundamental requirements of life where people ought to have an equal start and a fair footing. For me, good healthcare and good education are the big ones. Those shouldn’t just be another set of things the rich can buy more of, like a bigger car or a nicer house or a fancier holiday. Those are luxuries, but decent healthcare and education are about everyone having the same basic platform in life before individual effort comes into it.
