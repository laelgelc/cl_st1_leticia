there was this older chap, one of the guests, and while everyone else was hauling stuff down all those steps, he just quietly set himself a different job. the path had got completely lost under the grass, really overgrown, and instead of joining in with the carrying he started clearing it. he said, “it’s not my job, but I like to do something when I arrive,” and he decided that “finding the path” would be his task for the day. by the time he’d finished you could actually see and use the path again, all because he’d taken it on himself.
