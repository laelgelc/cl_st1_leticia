It is really complicated, yeah. The weirdest thing is the way the narration keeps switching. So you’ve got this guy Lockwood, and he’s renting Thrushcross Grange, and some of it is him talking. But then you’ve also got Nelly Dean, who’s a servant and actually grew up with Catherine and Hindley and works in one of the mansions. Technically people might say she’s not the official narrator, but really she is, because she’s the one actually telling all the stories that Lockwood then writes down.
