I keep thinking I should just walk that parcel down to the post office instead of asking someone to pop it in the car and take it for me. But I can already see what’ll happen: I’ll get home, have some lunch, and then I’ll think, “Oh, I’ll do it tomorrow.” And then tomorrow comes and I’ll do exactly the same thing again. It’s like I’m stuck in this loop of putting things off. It feels almost like a sort of depression, the way I keep postponing it instead of just getting on and doing it.
