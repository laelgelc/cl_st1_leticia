If I was the only immortal while everyone else still died, that would be incredibly hard, because I’d have to watch everyone around me pass away again and again. That’s the really difficult part of the idea for me. You see it in a lot of science fiction as well: these very long‑lived or immortal people who eventually just get bored with everything. They’ve seen it all, done it all, and nothing seems new anymore. So they end up choosing to go into these virtual worlds, or into hibernation, asking to be woken up thousands of years later when things get exciting again.
