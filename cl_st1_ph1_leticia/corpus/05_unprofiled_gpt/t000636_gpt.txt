The children just think their mum is completely normal, even though she used to be in the circus, and they don’t know anything about that past. Ashad, on the other hand, thinks his own mum is really cool. Teejay and Katy really admire Ashad’s mum too – she’s quiet and tidy and does lots of baking. She’s based on my student Sumi. Teejay and Katy imagine it would be lovely to have a mum like that, even though they do actually like their own mum. So there’s this contrast between how Ashad sees his mum and how Teejay and Katy see her, and how they all see their own families versus other people’s.
