My drumming teacher actually plays there once a month now, a regular little gig at the Olympic Park. It’s funny, after the Games you sort of imagine it all just sitting there empty, but it’s not like that at all. They still use it loads for sport, and there are restaurants and pubs and different events going on, so it’s a really lovely place to go.

Taking you lot out to restaurants there is definitely easier now than it used to be, but it’s still a bit stressful when you start getting bored and wriggly. It always reminds me of when I was little, having those endless roast dinners at Granny’s house. We had to just sit there for ages, and I absolutely hated it. You weren’t allowed to get down or run around, just sit and sit. Kids don’t really want to sit in restaurants, do they? It’s not exactly designed for you, it’s for grown‑ups who like talking for hours.
