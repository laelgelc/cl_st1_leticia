I get really irritated by the language they use in BBC news headlines about war. All that stuff like “remembering the fallen” just feels like a ridiculous euphemism to me – they’re people who died in the war, and it’s as if they’re dressing it up to make it sound nicer or more heroic. It really grates. When I was on my French weekend, I met this woman who’s done loads of conflict resolution and peace‑building work in war‑torn areas, proper on‑the‑ground stuff in places that have been through serious violence.
