it's an Indonesian rendang, that’s probably my favourite curry ever. I do it with about a kilo of beef steak, and I really tenderise it first, then cook it off on a high heat and take it out. The sauce is actually really easy – it’s quite like a green curry. I use onions, red chillies, and then ginger or galangal, kaffir lime leaves, lemongrass, and tamarind. It ends up a bit sweeter than some curries. Then there’s a lot of cinnamon in it, and cardamom, which gives it that lemony flavour, and quite a bit of clove too.
