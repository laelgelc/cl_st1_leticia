I just find the whole idea really troubling that from about four years old a child has to go into this state school system I don’t agree with. The state decides what they learn, who they’re with, how their day is structured, right up until they’re sixteen or eighteen. That level of control over such a long stretch of their life just doesn’t sit well with me. It’s not the kind of environment I’d want for my own child at all. I don’t like the thought of them being shaped so much by a system I don’t believe in, rather than by us and by a broader range of experiences.
