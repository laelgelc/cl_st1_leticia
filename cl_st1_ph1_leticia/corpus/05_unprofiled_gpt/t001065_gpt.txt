I'm literally the loudest person in our flat and even I couldn’t compete with this lad’s iPhone alarm. Our beds were on either side of the same wall, and he’d put his phone right against it, so every morning his alarm would just blare and vibrate straight through into my room. I didn’t even need to be up that early, but it woke me every time. I’d be there, banging on the wall, shouting, “Wake the fuck up, you stupid prick!” and he’d sleep right through it. I was absolutely fuming. One day I just snapped and went, “Did you have a good sleep? What do you think? I woke up with that fucking alarm.”
