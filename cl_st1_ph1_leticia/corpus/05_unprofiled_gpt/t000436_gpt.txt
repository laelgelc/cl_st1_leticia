Ely is on the A10, but it’s not very far up it at all. The big issue they had was this plan to build, I think it was ten or twenty thousand new houses up there, and yet there were no plans at all to do anything about the A10 itself. It’s already really busy and congested, and all those people would be commuting straight down that same road into Cambridge or heading on to London.

There was talk at one point about building a new commuter station somewhere south of Cambridge. The idea was that people wouldn’t have to come all the way into Cambridge itself just to get on a train; they could pick it up before that and avoid adding to the traffic and congestion in town. I don’t know if that’s actually going to happen, though. I haven’t heard anything more about it since it was mentioned, so I’ve no idea whether it’s still being considered or if it’s just one of those ideas that’s quietly disappeared.
