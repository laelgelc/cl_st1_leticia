I really didn’t want to go on that Wales trip at first. Four days just felt far too long, and I did try to wriggle out of it. But I’d enjoyed helping out before, and in the end it sort of fell to me because I was the only one with a car that could take four people and four bikes. So we worked out a way to do it: we got all four bikes standing upright on the roof, like you see in a race, and that made it possible.
