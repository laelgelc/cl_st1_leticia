It is lovely talking to you like this, you know. I actually went to the library once and suggested they run some “art of conversation” classes, where people would just sit and really talk about something chosen, because I think a lot of people are longing for proper conversation. It’s very different from being in a long-term partnership, where, after a while, the conversation isn’t always that stimulating. The library said, “Oh, we’ve got knitting and nattering,” but that’s not what I meant at all. I was thinking of serious topics – what’s going on at the Conservative conference, women priests in the church, anything like that. Of course we’d have to charge a bit to cover the room. Anyway, the idea rather fell on stony ground; they preferred origami and knitting.
