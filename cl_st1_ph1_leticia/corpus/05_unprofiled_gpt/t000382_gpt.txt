When you were just a few months old, we started talking about how nice it would be to move. It felt like the right moment to think about a change. We were actually able to do it because Daddy, who’d become disabled quite early, could cash in a pension he had from when he worked in London. That made a huge difference. It meant that, instead of it just being a vague idea, within less than a year of us first discussing it, we suddenly had the real opportunity to move.
