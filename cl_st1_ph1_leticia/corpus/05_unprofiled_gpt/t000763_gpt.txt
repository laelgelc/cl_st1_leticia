I was driving in one day when a colleague suddenly said, “Slow down, there’s average speed cameras here now.” That was the first I’d properly registered they’d gone in. I had this horrible moment of realisation, thinking, hang on, I’ve been bombing through here every day since about the second of January. It’s my usual route home in term time, not school holidays, so I’d just been on autopilot. I’d probably been over the limit past those cameras almost daily without even noticing.
