Over the years we’ve just learned how this game goes. They’re meant to pay that last fifteen percent, but instead they start throwing all sorts of things at you – demurrage charges, saying there are pipes missing, or that the pipe numbers don’t match up. And you sit there thinking, hang on, we’ve already agreed the total number of pipes received. It’s all been counted and signed off. But they keep coming back with another angle, another supposed issue, and it just goes round and round in circles instead of them paying what they owe.
