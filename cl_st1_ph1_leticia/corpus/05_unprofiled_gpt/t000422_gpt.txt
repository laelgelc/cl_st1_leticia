During the war, when all the men went away to fight, women had to step in and do all the jobs the men had been doing. They worked in factories and on farms and in all sorts of places they hadn’t been allowed into before. A lot of them actually really enjoyed it, because it was exciting and it gave them a new sense of freedom and independence. But then, when the war ended and the men came back, the men wanted their old jobs back. Women were told to go back home and look after the children and do the housework again. That made many women very upset, because they wanted to keep working.
