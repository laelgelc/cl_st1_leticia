In Norway there isn’t really much incentive to work exceptionally hard, because there’s no real meritocracy or clear reward for being especially innovative. It’s more like you’re expected to serve the system, fit into it, rather than stand out or push for something new. That whole cultural expectation is quite strong. It was so strong in practice that someone I know actually left Norway to study in America and England, partly for that reason, because they felt stifled by it and wanted to be somewhere that rewarded creativity and individual achievement more.
