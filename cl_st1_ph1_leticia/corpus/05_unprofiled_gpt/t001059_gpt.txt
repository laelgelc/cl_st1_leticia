People often talk about the Primpt Restaurant under the Angel Hotel as if it’s something exotic, but it’s actually an undercroft, part of that same sort of structure.

Ours, by contrast, was built as a shop, sometime between about twelve fifty and thirteen hundred. You can still see bits of the original fabric: there are parts of the old stairs surviving, and one original window. There’s also a very small arched doorway, which they think would have led to a spiral staircase going up to the house above. The staircase itself is ruined now, of course, but you can trace how it must have worked from that doorway and the remaining features.
