The train into Birmingham was absolutely heaving, and then the shops were rammed as well. We tried to get into Café Rouge, but they said all the tables that could take a pram were already reserved because of half term. The way they said it, though, made other people think there were no tables at all, so a few of them actually started to leave. The owner then rushed after them, saying there were plenty of tables, just none that could fit a pram. He even said he’d have carried the pram upstairs if there’d been a lift, he was that keen to get people back in.
