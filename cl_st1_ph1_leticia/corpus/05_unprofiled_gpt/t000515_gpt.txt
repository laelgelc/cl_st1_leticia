I can actually imagine spending a few years in Northern Ireland again, just because it’s so beautiful there. If I did move back – and I might, you know – there are loads of things I didn’t really take advantage of before. Like there’s this local sailing club, and on Wednesday nights people can just sign up as crew. You don’t have to know anything about boats, you just go along and learn while you’re sailing up and down the loch. That kind of thing makes it feel like there are all these opportunities I never really explored last time.
