I remember that employment project offer that came up, the one that was supposed to start in April. I told him straight away it sounded too good to be true. They were talking about a full year’s grant being paid in advance in November, and the space being held for them until then. I said it just wasn’t right, that sort of arrangement doesn’t add up. But he was absolutely convinced it was all above board. He kept saying the woman who’d put it forward was so sure of herself, so persuasive, that it must be genuine. I couldn’t shake the feeling it was wrong, though, and I stuck to that.
