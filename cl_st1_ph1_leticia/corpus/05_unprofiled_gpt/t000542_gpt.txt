I would quite happily skip mashed potatoes, I just don't like them at all. I do sometimes wonder if that goes back to junior school, actually. We used to sit at these big tables, and instead of everyone queuing up at a hatch to get their food from dinner ladies, the oldest kid at each table would dish everything out to the younger ones. It’s a bit of an odd system when I think about it now, and I can’t help thinking that might be why I’m so anti-mash.
