I’ve got this little row-counting app on my phone that I use for my knitting instead of scribbling on bits of paper. I’d got up to forty-four rows, feeling quite pleased with myself, and then I just left the phone perched on the arm of the sofa while I carried on. The cat decided that was exactly the right moment to leap up onto the sofa arm. She settled herself there, just sitting and staring at him, completely ignoring me and my knitting, and I didn’t think anything of it at first, because the phone was just sitting there next to her.
