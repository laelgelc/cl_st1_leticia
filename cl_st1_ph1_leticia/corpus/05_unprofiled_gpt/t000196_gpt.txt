oh no, that’s really sad, never getting stockings. We still do, actually – all of us still get them from our parents. It’s sort of evolved as we’ve got older though. When we were little we’d leave out biscuits and a carrot and some brandy for Father Christmas, and then my parents would sneak into our rooms in the night and fill the stockings while we were asleep. 

Now it’s more like we give the biscuits and the brandy straight to them, and we take our stockings into their room and leave them there to be filled overnight. So the whole magic Father Christmas thing has kind of shifted, but the routine is still there. And the stuff in them is very practical now – lots of little things like underwear and pyjamas rather than big toys or anything.
