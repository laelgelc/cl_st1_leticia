oh now that was entirely my stupid husband’s fault. He decided to tie our great lump of a dog to one of those little café tables, on a really windy day as well. Of course, a gust comes, a chair falls over, the dog gets spooked and bolts. Off he goes, dragging this flaming table behind him, straight through the gap in the outdoor seating, out onto the high street. Somehow he managed to cross without hitting anyone, just this table clattering along after him. He finally stopped when the table smacked straight into the Body Care shop window and smashed it. Honestly, you couldn’t make it up – I was furious with him for tying the dog to something that actually moves.
