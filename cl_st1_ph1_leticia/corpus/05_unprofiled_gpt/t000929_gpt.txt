What I really like about this framework is that it becomes an evidence base for people who’ve got very little or no work experience. They can actually point to real collaborative work they’ve done when they’re applying for jobs. It’s like what photographers do with portfolios, or filmmakers with their showreels – you don’t just say you’re good, you show it.

So we could deliberately design this as a product for young people: “Here’s how you sell yourself.” Not just relying on the stereotypical CV, which is really hard to make look appealing, but using concrete examples of what they’ve actually created with others.
