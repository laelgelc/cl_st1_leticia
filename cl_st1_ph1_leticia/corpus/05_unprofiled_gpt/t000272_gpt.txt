I was working with these really disturbed children at this school, and they kept telling me I was doing really well there. Then we had this absolutely terrible winter. I didn’t have a car, I was living in Nottingham, and my tutor – the one the pupils couldn’t stand – decided to send me for my main secondary teaching practice to Derby. So I had to get a bus into Derby, and then another bus out to Mickleover, every day in that weather, while all the people who actually had cars were able to do their teaching practice locally.
