I mean fifth of January, not December. I saw part of the garage door open and called out, and eventually found the mechanic round the back, fixing storm damage to his roof. I asked him if it was his garage and if he had any jump leads. He said they were actually closed and he didn’t have any leads, but then he brought a battery pack over instead. He hooked it up and jump-started the car for me. That really saved me – I was stuck there, worried about being on a hill and everything, and he just sorted it out even though the garage was shut.
