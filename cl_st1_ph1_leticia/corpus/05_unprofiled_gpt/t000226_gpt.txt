I honestly don’t know whether to believe her about being strangled. Part of it is because she’s said things before that I *know* aren’t true – like saying someone pushed her up against a wall, or that she paid for my university tuition when she didn’t. Her memory of events is often really different from mine. 

And then I found that poem and the anniversary note, about thirty‑seven years, pinned on their care calendar. The poem talks about them drifting apart for twenty years, then meeting again, liking each other, but the time not being right, so they left it a few more years. It makes it pretty obvious she’d been emotionally involved with him for years, so I wonder if this incident became a convenient reason.
