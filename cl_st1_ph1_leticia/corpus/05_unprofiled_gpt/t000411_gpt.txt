I used to have this job where students would constantly come up to me with all sorts of questions about essay writing and referencing. They weren’t all asking the same thing, so I’d end up giving different answers depending on what each person actually needed, really tailoring it to the individual. Later on, I moved into working more directly with academics. The idea there was to embed this whole notion of “academic literacy” into their courses, so instead of bolting it on separately, we’d just slightly upskill them and build it into what they were already doing.
