It was quite entertaining, honestly, they were mad. I remember this one picture so clearly: the entire outdoor area was just covered in these big square storage units, filling the whole back room. They went right up, must have been ten, twelve feet high, and they were for the kids to put all their clothes and belongings in. There were strict rules that children were not allowed to climb them at all. And yet at one point, they came back and there was this one child, completely on his own, right on top of the thing. No one had any idea how he’d managed to get up there.
