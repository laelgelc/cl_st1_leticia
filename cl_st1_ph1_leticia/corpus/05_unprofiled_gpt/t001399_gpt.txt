when I was three and had my adenoids out, I honestly thought the whole operation was just them putting this special gas on a mask and me having a little sleep. that was exactly how all the adults had described it to me, so in my head that really was the entire thing, nothing more to it. I didn’t question it at all and just carried that version around. it wasn’t until I was about nine or ten that I realised there must have been more involved than simply breathing in gas and dozing off for a bit.
