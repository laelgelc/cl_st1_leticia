so the exams are split into a few different parts, and they all work quite differently. the reading bit is the easiest to mark, because it’s just right or wrong – there’s no real judgement involved, you just check the answers. then there’s the writing part, where they have to write a short essay answer to a question, and you mark that, which obviously takes a bit more thought. the speaking exam is more complicated again, because you actually have to go in and do the speaking tests with them, face to face. on top of that, there’s the listening side, where they write summaries of what they’ve heard and you have to mark those as well. altogether, the speaking component ends up being quite difficult.
