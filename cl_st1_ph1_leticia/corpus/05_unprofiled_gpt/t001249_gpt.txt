It really is lovely, honestly. When you were little, we used to make this thing you absolutely adored. We’d mix tuna with mayonnaise and a bit of ketchup, then add finely chopped spring onions and sweetcorn. Then we’d spread that mixture all over crumpets – well, we always called them muffins – so it covered the top nicely. After that, we’d put cheese on top and pop them under the grill until the cheese was melted and bubbling. It was really tasty, you know, you used to gobble them up.
