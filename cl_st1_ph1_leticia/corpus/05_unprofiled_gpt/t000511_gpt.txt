So the idea was they’d all go out, and I’d stay here and just have a pizza delivered, which sounded blissful after I’d just finished work. I was really looking forward to ten minutes’ peace before they all turned up. Someone asked if she’d be all right in the house on her own, and I said, yeah, she’ll be fine, as long as we don’t have any extra candles lit or anything. But afterwards I started thinking about it and realised I really don’t want pizzas delivered when she’s here alone. If a delivery driver genuinely asked to use the toilet, he might realise she was by herself, and that worried me.
