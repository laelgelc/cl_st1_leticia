I showed her this handwritten letter from this very elderly scientist, and it was all about how he keeps writing to Dad wanting to measure him, and especially his children. He’d listed this whole range of things he wanted to know about us, all these different measurements and features he was interested in. I asked her, slightly nervously, whether she’d actually done any of these things he’d been going on about. She said that yes, she had done some of them over the years. Then she mentioned, quite casually, “droopy eyelids” as one of the things she’d measured.
