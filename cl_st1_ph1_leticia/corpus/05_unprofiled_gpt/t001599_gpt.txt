I'm actually glad I don’t remember everything I did or said when I was drunk; the bits I do recall are dreadful enough. But, you know, I also blame the society we were brought up in for some of the bad things we learned. We actually gave a sadistic man the power to beat a six-year-old child. It’s not so different, in a way, from what Jimmy Savile ended up in prison for, even if that wasn’t specifically beating. And there are still people who want corporal punishment back. All it really does is make you hate the institution that dishes it out.
