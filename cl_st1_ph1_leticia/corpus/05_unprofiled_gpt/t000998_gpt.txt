it's this Indonesian slash Malaysian curry that I honestly think is like the best curry in the world. You do these big chunks of beef, sear them off first, and then you cook them really, really slowly. The sauce is made with tamarind, kaffir lime, lemongrass, a little bit of brown sugar, and loads of coconut cream. You just let it go for ages until it really reduces down, so instead of a runny sauce it turns into this thick, dark paste that clings to the meat. It ends up being very sweet for a curry, but in a good way, like all the flavours get really concentrated.
