I’d arranged to visit my aunt on the bank holiday Monday. I’d rung her the week before, when I suddenly realised, oh, it’s bank holiday, so I checked if she was free. She said she was all right in the morning, but she had something on in the afternoon, so we decided I’d go over and we’d just go out for coffee. Then on the Sunday she rang me back and said, could we make it lunch instead of coffee? She wanted to fit in her antibiotics, which had to be taken an hour before a meal, so lunch would work better with her routine.
