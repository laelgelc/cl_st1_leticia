I did say to him afterwards, “I’m really sorry there wasn’t any reading‑in work for you,” and that I’d actually thought he was going to help herd people in. Then I heard from someone else that he’d just been happily socialising while “meeting and greeting,” chatting away with everyone. So when I explained that, he turned round and sarcastically thanked me for taking his job away, like I’d somehow stolen this grand role from him, and said that was the only reason he’d come down in the first place.
