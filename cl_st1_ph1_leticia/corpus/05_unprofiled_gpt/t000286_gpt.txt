It was the roof right near the windscreen that was completely caved in where the deer hit, just crushed in. Other motorists stopped straight away, and one of them kept on asking the driver, “Are you sure you’re alright? Do you want an ambulance?” He was really insistent, properly worried. But the driver kept saying he was fine, that he just had a few cuts and didn’t need anything. Looking at the state of the car, though, it was unbelievable he was actually okay and walking around.
