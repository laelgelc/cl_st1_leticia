We built ours right between our driveway and next door’s, sort of straddling the two. When we’d finished, I texted their daughter to warn her it might be in the way if she needed to park, and said she was totally allowed to kick it over if it was blocking her. She texted back saying she couldn’t possibly do that, which made me laugh.  

Over the next day it did the job for her anyway. It started off looking brilliant, then gradually slumped. First the head tilted, then the eyes and nose just sort of slid off and disappeared into the slush. By the following day there was just this small, sad little heap where our magnificent snowman had been.
