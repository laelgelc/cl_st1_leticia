Using computers for your cartoons makes everything so much easier. You don’t have to worry about running out of space on the page, and if you make a mistake it really doesn’t matter, because you can just go back and change it. It’s completely different from how it used to be. When we had typewriters, if you made a mistake you were stuck with it. You either had to get out the Tippex and try to cover it up, or you had to start all over again and rewrite the whole thing from the beginning.
