Loads of people round there just don’t work and they just steal all the time. I was upstairs having a fag out the window and I saw this bloke park up, knock on next door, then he comes over asking if I know them. I say no, and he says they’d ordered forty quid’s worth of food and nicked it. They’d pretended to live in this empty corner house, climbed over two garden fences, told the delivery guy they were getting cash, then gone back over into their real house. They got seen and caught.
