Well, they might not actually get made, you know. It all started with that poem Nicola wrote, and Jackie read it and got really inspired. Then loads of other people joined in, drawing chairs and sending their pictures in. The whole idea is to welcome the refugee children who are coming here, to make them feel wanted when they arrive in this country. So the chairs are really a sort of message, a way of saying, “You’re welcome here.” And who knows, maybe one day an artist will look at all the drawings and decide to make a real chair based on some of them.
