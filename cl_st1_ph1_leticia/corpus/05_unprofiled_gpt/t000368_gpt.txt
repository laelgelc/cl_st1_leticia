When I finally went back after those three years in the shop, it was such a change. I found it really mentally engaging and I absolutely loved it. I was constantly busy, always doing something. I’d be going to the theatre a lot, and through a friend who worked there I’d end up meeting people backstage, which made it all feel even more exciting. It really did feel like this incredibly exciting life, always on the go, always something new happening. And then I finished, moved to Cambridge, and it just… stopped. That whole exciting lifestyle just came to an abrupt end.
