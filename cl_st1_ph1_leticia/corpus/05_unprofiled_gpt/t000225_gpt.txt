I've just been thinking a lot about Mum and Dad and the whole divorce thing recently. I honestly think now that Mum tried to convince us all that Dad was an alcoholic, just so she could use that as the only legal ground for divorce that applied. I can still remember Dad crying and saying she was lying, and I just didn’t understand what was going on back then. It didn’t make sense to me at the time, but looking back, it feels like that was exactly what she was doing, trying to make that story stick.
