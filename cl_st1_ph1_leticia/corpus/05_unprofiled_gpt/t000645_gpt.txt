So when they told us on the course to get completely obsessed with one particular theme, I picked Minecraft. I just threw myself into it, learned everything I could, spent loads of time on different servers, and started noticing patterns. One big thing was how often people built Mario monuments; they were everywhere, like this recurring motif in the Minecraft world. That really stuck with me. I’d already been thinking about that idea of the garden gnome blown up to the size of the Statue of Liberty, and I thought it’d be fun to mash all of that together. So I decided on a statue that combined a garden gnome, the Statue of Liberty, and Super Mario in one big Minecraft build.
