I was up early, about half six, going out to a car boot sale, and as I was leaving I saw this car pull into our street and just sit there. It stayed parked up while I went off, which struck me as a bit odd. Then I noticed another police car coming in at the bottom of the street, so I thought, something serious must’ve happened here. I kept watching for ages, really looking to see what was going on, but I never actually saw anything or anyone come out of that house.
