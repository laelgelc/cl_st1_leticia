At work we had a huge stock transfer, so loads of council properties went over to a housing association. After that, the chief executive really went after one big, very valuable house where a family of eight were living. He just wanted them out so he could sell it off, and he pushed it really hard. I had to step in and say, look, you can’t just evict them like that, it’s illegal, and that stopped it. But that’s the point: once those houses are in the hands of a chief executive instead of an elected councillor, it all becomes about the financial bottom line.
