I remember walking up those stairs absolutely terrified, just bracing for this massive punch from that huge jock I’d just threatened. I was fully expecting him to come after me, like any second I’d feel a fist in the back of my head. And then… nothing. He just didn’t follow, didn’t attack, nothing happened at all. Next thing I know, I’m outside, and my friend’s completely losing it at this hot dog lady, screaming at her, and we’re just kind of on this ridiculous little rampage together.
