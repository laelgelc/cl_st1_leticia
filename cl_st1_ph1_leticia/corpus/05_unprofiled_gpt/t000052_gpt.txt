Sometimes the phrases you mishear do sound a bit like what they’re actually saying, but not really enough to justify how wrong you’ve got it. It’s because so much of hearing is done with context in your brain, so you’re sort of filling in the gaps. I end up having to listen again and again until I can understand everything that’s being said properly. 

There was this one business-related meeting term on some audio I was working with, and I just could not pin it down. I wasn’t sure how to spell it at all, so I couldn’t look it up properly online. I tried searching different versions, and nothing came up that looked right. What made it worse was that other people didn’t seem to spell it correctly either, which just confused me even more.
