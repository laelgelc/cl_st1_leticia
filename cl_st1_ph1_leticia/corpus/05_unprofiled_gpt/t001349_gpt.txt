So on Valentine’s Day me and my sister were thinking of going Nando’s, yeah. The one near her house is tiny and always full, so we didn’t even bother with that. Then there’s another branch, but that one’s just too crusty, I don’t like it at all. There’s also a big one, but we knew that would be rammed with people and couples, so that was a no as well. In the end we picked this more remote Nando’s in this almost empty area, just because we thought it would actually be quiet.
