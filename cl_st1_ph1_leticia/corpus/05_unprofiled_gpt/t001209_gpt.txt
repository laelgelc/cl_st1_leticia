They still have cannibals there, and completely uncontacted tribes – like those videos you see of people throwing spears at helicopters. I was swimming on this beach that was just unbelievably remote and beautiful, absolutely idyllic, no one around at all. And then later on I read that someone had actually been eaten by a saltwater crocodile on that exact same beach, just three months after I’d been there. It was such a strange feeling, realising how close I’d been to something like that.
