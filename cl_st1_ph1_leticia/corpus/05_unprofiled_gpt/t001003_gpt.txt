I think a parent should be properly educated so they can teach their child all the important simple things themselves, not just rely on schools. They should know how to cook quite well, so the child grows up with good food and basic life skills. They should be able to manage money very well, so there’s stability and security. They should also be social, able to help the child learn how to interact with people. And there are lots of other qualities too, which most people only really develop gradually, often doing better with a second child than with their first.
