You can't just let things slip; that’s what struck me in those early Blair years. In that first year, people like Blunkett looked absolutely exhausted because so much work was being pushed through in such a short time. Whatever you think of it, there was a huge effort and it showed on their faces. Compared to that, this current lot are frenetic but don’t seem to produce anything positive or properly thought through. Back then there really was an attempt to bring everyone in, that whole stakeholder approach, involving all parties before decisions were made.
