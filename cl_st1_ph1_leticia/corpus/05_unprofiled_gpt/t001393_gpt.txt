Another way we could connect this is by really homing in on what employers are actually saying. You hear this line all the time that uni graduates aren’t what employers want, or that Gen Y is somehow not measuring up. I’m not even sure if that’s genuinely new or if every generation gets that complaint, but it’s definitely part of the narrative out there. So why don’t we deliberately go and talk to a group of employers and ask them directly? Get them to spell out what they think is missing, what they wish students could do better. That could give us a really solid base of evidence and language to build from, and it would tie our whole project more concretely to the real-world expectations students are walking into.
