Some of them just can't really follow the music at all, so we've had to make it very simple. Their notes are all colour‑coded, so they know which ones belong to them at a glance. We also put little marks on the music to show which hand they should be using for each note, so they don’t get muddled up. And then there are extra markings to tell them whether they’re meant to damp the sound quickly or let it ring on for a long time.
