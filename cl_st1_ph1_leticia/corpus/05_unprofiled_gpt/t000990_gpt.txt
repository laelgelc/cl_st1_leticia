Well I was chatting to this lady at the wedding, she came up to me and said, “I don’t know anyone here,” so I said, “Well you know me now,” and we got talking. It turned out we both had sons getting married, so of course we started comparing all the arrangements and dates and everything. Then we realised we’d actually both tried to book the same church date, the fifth of September. She’d been told she couldn’t have it, but I’d managed to get it, which was a bit awkward really when we worked it out. We were laughing about it though, because it was just one of those odd coincidences you only discover when you start chatting properly.
