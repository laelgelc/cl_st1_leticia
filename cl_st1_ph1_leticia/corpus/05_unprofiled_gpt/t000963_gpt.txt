They were supposed to be helping me all along, but they just assumed everything was all right until I actually said, “look, there’s something wrong.” I’d been getting these funny “warpy” dizzy spells whenever I bent down, and they just put it down to my blood pressure being low, when in fact it was too low. It’s really this year that it’s got worse. The morning I finally rang the doctor I just couldn’t think straight at all, didn’t know what to do, couldn’t decide anything. That’s what finally pushed me to phone and say something wasn’t right.
