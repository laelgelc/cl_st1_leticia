I really don’t think this is about your personality, or that you somehow “caused” it by not having some kind of spiritual outlook. People love to pin it on that, but it’s just not that simple. There are so many outside factors that pile up and make you feel constantly upset and worried – it’s not just something inside you being wrong or you choosing to be this way.

I know it feels like this is permanent now, like this is just who you are and it’s never going to shift, and that really sucks. But feeling permanent isn’t the same as actually being permanent; it doesn’t have to stay like this forever.

When you were at home over summer, did it feel less stressful because there weren’t the same responsibilities? Or was it actually harder because of pressure from your family?
