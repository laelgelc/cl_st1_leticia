yesterday it just really struck me at school, actually. I had my class in, they’d been really badly behaved, so I had to keep them in, and it was during the time when I was supposed to be on break duty. So I went to this Year 6 teacher and said, you know, “Can you cover my duty?” and he just straightaway said yes. No complaint, no sighing, nothing, just immediately agreed to do it.

And I was thinking, if it had been the other way round, I would’ve been really reluctant. I’d have been thinking, oh for goodness’ sake, I’m going to miss my coffee, I don’t want to go out in the cold, and I’d probably have been a bit irritated about it. I’d have done it, but it would’ve been with that sort of “fine, okay, I’ll do it, fine, fine, yes, fine, no worries” kind of grumpy attitude running underneath. Whereas he just did it, and it really highlighted to me how much nicer he was about it than I would’ve been.
