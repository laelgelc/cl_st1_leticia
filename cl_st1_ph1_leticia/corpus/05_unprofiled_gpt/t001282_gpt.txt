I just think, especially in first year, it’s more about getting your life together than anything else. You’re basically starting a new life, learning a whole new way of living, and that’s a lot on its own. It’s about getting used to the whole experience, figuring out how everything works here, and giving yourself time to settle in. Meeting people, making friends, joining different things and getting involved in stuff – that’s a massive part of it too. The academic side is important, but it doesn’t have to be the absolute main focus right now; you can push that more in later years once you’re more settled.
