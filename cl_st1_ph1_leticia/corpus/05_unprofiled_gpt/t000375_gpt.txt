After her dad died, she just didn’t know what her life was about without him. He’d always been central to everything and, on top of that, he’d helped keep a bit of a lid on her drinking. Once he was gone, even though her daughter really did care for her, she didn’t properly understand what was going on with the drink. So there wasn’t anyone there who really grasped the situation or could step in the way he had. She just carried on drinking heavily, and it got to the point where it started to make her a bit nasty with people.
