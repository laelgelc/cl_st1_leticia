yeah it really is like that, all or nothing with them. When I was working at Disneyland Paris I saw it first-hand. We were having this massive Fourth of July party in the university residences, loads of American students, really loud, people hanging out of windows, music blaring, all that. Someone called the police, you could see people on the phone, but nothing happened at all for about an hour, no sirens, no normal police turning up to tell us to turn the music down or anything.

Then suddenly the CRS riot squad arrived, full gear, shields, helmets, the lot. They came in, spread out, and just stood there, watching us. They didn’t say anything or try to stop the party, everyone just carried on drinking and shouting, and they just waited. It was really odd, like they were biding their time rather than doing the usual “move along” kind of thing.
