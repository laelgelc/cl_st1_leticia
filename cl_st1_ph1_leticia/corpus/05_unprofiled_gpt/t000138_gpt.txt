The problem is really how they’ve sold the houses. They’ve released a batch of plots all at once, but they’re actually selling them off at different times and different stages. So you end up with houses that are supposed to be built together being bought at completely different times. Ours went later, so we’re stuck in the middle of that. It means certain bits of the build just get held up, because they’re either waiting on information from the people who’ve bought, or they’re deliberately dragging their feet for some reason. So whole parts of the process get delayed when they should all be moving along together.
