I told him as clearly as I could that there was absolutely no hope of a relationship, but he just wouldn’t take it on board. He kept saying I must be unhappy, pointing out that I’m always out and doing plays, as if that somehow proved something. When I was ill, some anonymous flowers turned up, and at first I honestly thought they were from other people. Then later there were more flowers, and a Valentine’s card delivered to work with a Shakespeare sonnet in it about unwavering love. I just assumed that had to be from him, and I decided to ignore it. I didn’t respond at all, because I didn’t want to encourage him or give him any sense that there was still a chance.
