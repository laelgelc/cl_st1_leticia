As I’ve got older I’ve really started to notice things changing. Physically, for a start – the rheumatism and the joints hurting, and just not being able to go out two nights in a row like I used to. I feel it so much more now. And then there’s the whole technology side of it, which has really knocked my confidence. I used to feel like such a confident person, but now I find it harder to understand things or remember how to use them. It’s not that I never manage, but the increasing difficulty in taking it all in and making it work makes me feel different, somehow less like the person I once was, and that’s quite a strange feeling.
