It's funny, I do think times have really changed. I started work at eighteen, straight out of school, never went to university, and it just would never have crossed my mind to ask my parents for money if I was a bit short. They simply didn’t have it, that was that. Whereas now, my daughter rang yesterday saying, “Mum, I’m really going to struggle to pay the rent this month, could you pay this month’s?” I’d already paid last month’s and I’m her guarantor as well. I did say to her, at twenty‑seven, I’m really fed up with this and don’t want to keep doing it.
