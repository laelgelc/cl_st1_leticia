I saw this thing on Facebook that really stuck with me, saying “stop the glorification of busy.” It hit home because I used to be exactly like that. When I was working on that small industrial estate, I’d actually feel guilty for taking a proper lunch break. There was this bloke from the little business centre nearby, and we’d grab a coffee together sometimes. We both had this mindset that the truly dedicated, hard-working people were the ones who didn’t stop for lunch at all, who just powered straight through the day without a break.
