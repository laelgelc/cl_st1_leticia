I woke up one morning and my hands were just completely different, like overnight. The skin on the palms was suddenly peeling off, really dramatically, almost like a dead man’s hand. It was all just coming away and it was absolutely terrifying, really freaky, seeing all the skin on my hands like that. I went to the doctor, and they said it was contact dermatitis, which I’d never really thought about before. They gave me some cream, and thankfully it went away, but it really scared me.
