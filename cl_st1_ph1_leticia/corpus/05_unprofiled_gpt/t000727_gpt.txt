I said to her, well, I really liked that group, so I was happy to stay with them until they actually finished. I thought, if they’ve started with me and they want me to carry on, then I’ll see it through with them. So I did that, and when they finally finished, I thought that was it, really. But then later on they rang again and asked if I could cover more classes because someone else was off having an operation. So I went back in and covered those as well.  

I do actually enjoy it there, the whole atmosphere at the college, the people, the teaching side of it, all of that. But it’s changed a lot. The funding cuts have really bitten. Where before you could just go and get what you needed, now there’s hardly anything. You used to be able to have all the basic teaching materials without thinking about it, and now it’s all really restricted. It’s gone from feeling like there were plenty of resources and you could do what you wanted with the lessons, to feeling like you’re constantly having to make do with very little.
