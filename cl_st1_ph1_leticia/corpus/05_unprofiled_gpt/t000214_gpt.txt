I’d do it in a Word document first, just keep drafting it till it says exactly what you want, really simply, nothing complicated. The whole point is to make her stop and think properly about what she’s doing and what it’ll mean later on, not just now. I’d start it by saying something like, “We were talking the other day about you renting a place,” and then, “I’ve now looked into it and there are several places you could rent.” That way it links straight back to the conversation and shows you’ve actually checked things out.
