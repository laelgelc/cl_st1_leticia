she was literally grumpy the whole weekend, and it felt like the entire trip turned into this weird competition about who knew Paris better. Every time I said something, she had to correct me, like at the Louvre – I’d say one thing and she’d jump in with “no actually it’s this,” like she needed to prove she knew more.

then at Galeries Lafayette it all kicked off again. I sprayed some perfume, just trying stuff out, and she got all stroppy, saying “you’re not allowed to do that.” I was like, that’s ridiculous, of course you’re allowed to test perfume, and I pushed back a bit because it was such an outdated thing to say. she really didn’t like being challenged.

later on, we were in this café and she suddenly comes out with, “I’m really pissed off with you.” apparently she felt I’d snapped at her earlier. from my point of view I hadn’t snapped at all, I’d just disagreed with her, but she’d clearly been stewing about it and decided to confront me there. it just put this cloud over everything.
