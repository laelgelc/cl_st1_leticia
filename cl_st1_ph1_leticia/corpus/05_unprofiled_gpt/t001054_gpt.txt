That Facebook memory popped up from four years ago, when you were just over two and we went to watch the diving at the Olympic Stadium. It reminded me how we had to be absolutely silent in there, because the divers on the ten-metre board needed to concentrate and any sudden noise could really put them off. You absolutely hated having to be quiet, though. And it didn’t help that the diving building was really, really warm. You were just so hot and cross, stuck in this boiling place where everyone had to be silent, and you were not impressed with that at all.
