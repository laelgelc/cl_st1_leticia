I emailed her about a few different books, just chatting about what we’d been reading, and I said I’d just finished this one that was possibly the worst book I’d ever read. It was so bad it was almost mesmerisingly bad, you know, where you keep reading because you can’t quite believe sentences like that have actually been published. And then she replied saying she thought it was quite good, quite interesting, as a sort of portrait of people living in this particular place. At that point I just thought: she has no taste or judgement whatsoever.
