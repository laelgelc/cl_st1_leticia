What I didn’t realise at first was that there was this bloke in a car behind us who’d clearly decided we knew what we were doing, so he was following along. We’re trundling along and the road’s getting narrower and narrower, turning into this little track by some farms, and I’m thinking, this can’t be right. In the end we had to turn round, and when we got back I wound the window down and said to him, “We’ve absolutely no idea where we’re going, you know!”
