When I’m sat there in traffic having this really stressful little internal monologue, I keep thinking, if I had this really important job it would all feel more justifiable. Like, if I was heading in for big meetings, with proper responsibilities, where people were depending on me and I absolutely had to be there, then maybe the horrible commute would make some kind of sense. But instead I’m just stuck there, inching along, and I’m thinking, realistically it probably doesn’t even matter if I get to work today or not.
