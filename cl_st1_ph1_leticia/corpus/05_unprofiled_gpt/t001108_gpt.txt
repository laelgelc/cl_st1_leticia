So in the evening we decided to go to that big light and fountain show, you know, the one in front of that massive grand building that used to be something else but is now the national museum. It was really impressive when we finally got there, all lit up and everything, but it was such a long walk. We hadn’t really thought through how far it actually was, we just sort of set off.

And then, of course, we got a bit lost on the way, because none of us had been there before and we didn’t really know where we were going. I’d only suggested going, but somehow I ended up being treated like the leader, like it was my responsibility to know the route. It felt a bit unfair, because I was just as clueless as everyone else and it wasn’t like I’d been there before.
