I let them all go off on this proper run and I just stayed put on the baby slope, still trying to get the hang of moving and holding onto that rope. After a bit I thought, right, I’ll have a little break, so I went and sat in the café. I’m looking out the window and I can see her, this lass from our group, actually climbing down the side of the slope through the snow. She kept throwing her skis down in front of her, clambering after them, over and over, till she finally got to the bottom and just said, that’s it, I’m never going skiing again.
