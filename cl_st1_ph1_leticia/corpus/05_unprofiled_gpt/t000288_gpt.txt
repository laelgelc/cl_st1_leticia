Well, from what I know – which isn’t much – daffodils and tulips are actually really hardy. In my family we’re generally terrible with plants, but those always seemed to survive whatever we did to them. We used to dig the bulbs up and chuck them out every year, and they still came back. My dad would routinely dig them up before winter, even though my mum kept telling him not to, and somehow they survived all that and carried on growing anyway.
