When I was about thirty, my next-door neighbour went through this. She was very highly strung, very anxious, had OCD, and for years doctors just wrote her off as hysterical and neurotic. All the symptoms she kept going with were interpreted as part of that. Eventually they discovered she had stage four ovarian cancer, and by then she was basically done for; she died. I find that bloody terrifying. With breast cancer you’re told to look for a lump, with cervical cancer you go for a smear. But I only recently even heard there was such a thing as vulval or vaginal cancer, and I still don’t know the symptoms. Ovarian symptoms are so vague they get labelled IBS or “her age” instead of triggering blood tests or ultrasounds.
