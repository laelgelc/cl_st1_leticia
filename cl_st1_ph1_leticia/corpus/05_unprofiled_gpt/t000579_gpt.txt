It starts off really weird, like straight away it’s unsettling. You just get these strange sound effects, no proper music or anything, and it’s all in black and white, really slow-motion. There’s this big tree, and the whole family are there, literally tied up in hangman’s nooses from one of the branches. There’s this saw on a stick, and it’s kind of slowly cutting through the branch they’re all attached to. Eventually it cuts all the way, and that pulls on the rope so the whole family, including the children, are suddenly lifted and hanged.
