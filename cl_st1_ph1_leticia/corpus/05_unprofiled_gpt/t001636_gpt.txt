I just remember coming away from that documentary thinking, even if I can’t recall the exact details, it’s pretty shocking what they’re letting happen. Bits of the NHS being sold off, treated like a business, really. Mental health especially stuck with me – all these cuts so loads of people just aren’t getting treated, and the services that do exist are on such a shoestring. It sounded like things are really bad in that area. But then, on the other hand, when you look at the benefits side of things, there are problems there too. It’s not all straightforwardly good; that whole system has its own set of issues.
