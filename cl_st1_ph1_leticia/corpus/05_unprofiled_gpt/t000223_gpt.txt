for me one of the biggest problems with having kids is that when they’re about four you have to hand them over to the government, basically. You put them into the public school system and from that point they’re in that system until they’re eighteen. It feels like the state then controls their education and a huge chunk of their daily life, and that really worries me. I don’t trust the way that system runs or the effect it could have on the relationship between a parent and a child, when so much of their time and experience is shaped elsewhere. There are alternatives, of course, but realistically private teaching just isn’t something I could afford.
