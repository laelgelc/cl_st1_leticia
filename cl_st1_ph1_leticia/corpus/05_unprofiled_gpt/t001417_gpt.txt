So we’d got him those vouchers, didn’t we, for his birthday and Christmas, with the idea that we’d go and do a refresher skiing lesson together in January before the trip. I thought that’d be quite nice, just the two of us brushing up a bit. Then I said to the boys, you know, if they fancied it they could come along as well, but maybe just do one of the cheaper weekend options rather than the full thing, because it all adds up, doesn’t it.

Anyway, later on he’d clearly been looking online, and he comes back to me all pleased with himself and says he’s found this over-fifties session. It’s three hours in the morning and you get a cup of tea included and everything. I was like, oh right, that’s what we’re doing now then, is it?
