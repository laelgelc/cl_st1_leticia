I’d never actually met her before, but she acted like we already knew each other. She started asking all these really friendly questions, like “how are you?”, “when are you back to work?”, and “did you have a nice summer break?” It all came just from her having seen my email address, which I’d written down to get included on this group email about baked goods. It felt a bit strange because there was no real introduction, just this instant familiarity based solely on that email contact.
