I had such a good reputation that some of the classes were actually quite scared of me. There was one time I decided to have a bit of fun with it. I hid behind the classroom door before the bell went, so they didn’t know I was there. The bell went, they all came charging in, shouting and carrying on as usual, thinking there was no proper teacher about. I just stood there watching them for a moment, taking it all in. Then I stepped out from behind the door with this big smile on my face. The whole room went absolutely silent, everyone just sat down straight away, and in this little chorus they all went, “hello.”
