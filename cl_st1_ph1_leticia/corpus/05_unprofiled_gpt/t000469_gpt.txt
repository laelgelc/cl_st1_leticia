I keep hearing them all on the radio and that, moaning about low wages and blaming the unions, and I’m sitting there thinking, I feel like phoning up and saying, “Have a look where the unions were strong.” Look at the car industry, places like Ford, they’re on proper money there. That’s where you see the difference. It’s the shops where they’re getting buttons, hardly anything for what they do. People go on and on, but they never seem to look at that side of it.
