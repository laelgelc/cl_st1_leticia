I was talking to this girl a couple of weeks ago, and she actually grew up bilingual. At home they always used two different languages – say, something like German and Italian – right from when she was little. Then on top of that she learned English, or at least really improved it later, and she also studied French at school. She said that sometimes, when she’s trying to find a word, all those languages sort of get jumbled up in her head, and whatever comes to mind first just pops out, regardless of which language it is. And then there’s this other person we know who’s similar in a way: his English is really advanced now, and he also speaks both Catalan and Castilian Spanish.
