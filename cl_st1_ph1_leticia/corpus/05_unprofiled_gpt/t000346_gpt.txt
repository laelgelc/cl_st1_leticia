I used to collect plants quite seriously, you know. I’d go out and pick them, bring them home and press them, and then sit and identify them properly. I even used to go out on fieldwork, along the streams, looking for snails and things, tagging along while the proper work was being done. People would stop and talk to us, very friendly, just curious about what we were doing. But later on, African friends – our driver and the lab assistant – told me I really mustn’t walk about on my own anymore, it just wasn’t safe.
