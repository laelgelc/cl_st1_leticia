when I used to go to work I’d have to leave the house at about five to seven in the morning, so around six fifty‑five, and it was always in the dark and freezing cold, usually with snow about as well. I’d get the seven o’clock bus, and then after that I’d be stuck waiting for the next one for about half an hour, just standing there in something like minus eight degrees. once that second bus finally turned up and got me near work, I still had a ten to fifteen minute walk, all uphill, from the bus stop to actually get to the place.
