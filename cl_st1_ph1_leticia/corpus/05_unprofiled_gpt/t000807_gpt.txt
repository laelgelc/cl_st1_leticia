So after I’d sent all that stuff – the frying pan in heaven, the ridiculous wedding message, and starting it with “hi duck” – I finally got a reply back. This person texts, “sorry duck, wrong number,” and even apologises about the frying pan, like, “sorry about your frying pan.” For a moment I was like, oh my god, I’ve actually got the thief, this is them! Then it slowly dawned on me it wasn’t you at all. Turned out it was someone from Plymouth, completely random, and their number was saved in my phone under some other name. No idea how that even happened. So I’m sat there thinking I’ve uncovered this big mystery, and actually it’s just my phone being really weird and mixing up people’s numbers.
