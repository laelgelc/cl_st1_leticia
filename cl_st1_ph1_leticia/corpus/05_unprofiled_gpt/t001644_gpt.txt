It really depresses me when I hear these stories about young people, sex and pregnancy. You get this picture of girls not actually enjoying sex, just going along with it because the boys want it. And both boys and girls are learning about sex from the internet and pornography, which is such a completely unrealistic picture of what it’s actually like. I feel that whole portrayal is profoundly unhelpful for girls and for their chances of having a healthy sex life. Instead of it being something mutual and pleasurable, you’ve got girls sometimes having sex under a kind of duress, not really enjoying it at all, and that just feels so wrong after everything that was supposed to have changed.
