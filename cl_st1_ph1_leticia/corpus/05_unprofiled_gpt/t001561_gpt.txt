There was this big, fat American bloke, must’ve been about fifty, in the audience, and he just kept interrupting the compere. Every time the compere said something, this guy would shout, “That’s me!” really loudly. At first everyone was just confused, like, what’s he on about? Then people started talking to him, trying to work it out. In the end the compere clearly decided he was a complete nutter and tried not to engage, but the bloke kept standing up and saying loads of stupid things.
