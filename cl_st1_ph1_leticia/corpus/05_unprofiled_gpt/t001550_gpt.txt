I really don’t know how he survived, to be honest. He looked like he’d been on the street for a long time, really worn down, so it’s possible he just moved on somewhere else. What made it worse for me was knowing we had that spare apartment sitting there, completely unused, and I was the one responsible for it. I got so close to just saying, “Look, you can stay here,” but I held back because of that responsibility, and it left me feeling really torn.
