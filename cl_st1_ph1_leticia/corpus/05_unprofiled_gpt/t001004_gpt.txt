I don't have other adults around me, really, and that’s a big part of why I think the way I do about having children. It probably sounds completely crazy, but I honestly believe everyone should be sterilised by default, and then you’d have to apply and meet certain criteria before being allowed to have a child. 

Look at how it works with other things: you need to pass tests and prove you’re responsible to drive a car, and the process for adopting a child is insanely rigorous, with endless checks and assessments. Yet to have your “own” child, there’s nothing like that at all. I find that absurd. The only reason it’s like this, as far as I can see, is because it’s just so easy, biologically, to have a child.
