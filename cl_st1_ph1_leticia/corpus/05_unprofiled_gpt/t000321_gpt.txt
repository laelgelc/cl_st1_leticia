The Buena Vista Social Club is this really famous music place in Cuba, and also a film, all about these incredibly talented Cuban and South American musicians. The story is that they were taken over to perform in this really glamorous city in the States, even though most of them had hardly ever left their own hometowns before. The documentary they made about it is really, really good – the music is just fantastic, proper soul‑lifting stuff, and you get a sense of their lives as well as the performances. 

And actually, that farm in Austria we were looking at is apparently full of South American people. From what I’ve heard it’s probably mostly South American‑owned as well, which kind of ties in nicely with all this Cuban and Latin American music talk.
