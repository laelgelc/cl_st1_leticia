Well the rapids come before the waterfall, that’s the first bit where the water’s all fast and bumpy. When people get badly hurt or die it’s mostly because it’s such a long way to fall, and they end up smashing into jagged rocks at the bottom. Even if there aren’t rocks, the sheer force of the water can really hurt you. Think about high diving: if you hit the water badly from high up you can break bones, just from the impact.
