I was thinking about that other site we’d been to before, the free one, and how there’s no kiosk or little man selling anything there. When we were at Priory, you know, the manned place nearby, I’d seen the guidebooks in the shop and thought, actually, that would be a really good idea for the free site. The guidebooks always tell you far more than the odd notices dotted about. I kept meaning to go back and get one, and just never did. Then we had this absolutely beautiful day, so we finally went, bought the guidebook, and visited the site again. It’s very close to that fruit farm, so it made sense to call in there on the way back.
