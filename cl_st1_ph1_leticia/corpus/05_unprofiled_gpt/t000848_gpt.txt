It was good fun the other evening, and really quite worthwhile as a sort of reconnaissance, because the place has such a wide range of things, but all the information’s scattered about. The staff were brilliant, actually: they took a board off the wall so we could see it properly, and even moved a table and a chair out of the way for us. There weren’t many people there, which made it easier to look around and get a feel for it all. We even had a fire going, though it wasn’t an open one, so it was cosy without being smoky or anything like that.
