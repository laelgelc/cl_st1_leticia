Even if he flat-out refuses a pay rise now, you should still push him on it. Ask him, “Okay, what do I need to do—X, Y, and Z—to get more money in the future?” Otherwise there’s literally no incentive for you. They can’t just set you all these big performance targets for the next twelve months and expect you to smash them with nothing extra in return. That’s not how it should work. You need to go in there with your fighting face on.
