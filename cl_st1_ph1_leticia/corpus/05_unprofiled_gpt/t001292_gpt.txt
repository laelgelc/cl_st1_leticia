Acting out of fear feels like a really poor motivation to me, and I do feel quite conflicted about people begging for money. I generally don’t give money in those situations. Instead, I do this sort of spiritual or energetic practice: I silently “word” through the person, almost like a Namaste, acknowledging their presence and the equality between us. I imagine and project this pink, loving energy, and I consciously intend the highest and best outcome for them. It may even be that, in their string of incarnations, they’ve chosen this lifetime to experience lack.
