I remember when I finished my first degree, my very last exam was in Quranic Arabic. For that, I’d had to memorise an extraordinary number of languages and dialects, and I actually managed to do it. But straight after the exam, I went into the common room and picked up a copy of The Times. I suddenly noticed I was automatically memorising the articles, almost word for word, without even trying. That was the moment I realised something was wrong, that this compulsive memorisation wasn’t normal at all.
