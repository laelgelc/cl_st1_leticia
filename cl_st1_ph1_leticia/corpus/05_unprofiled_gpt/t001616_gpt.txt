I had to scoop all the broken glass out of the car before I could even drive it home, and I ended up cutting my hand doing it. I remember driving back with the wind just whistling through where the window had been smashed. They only repaired it because the car was so new, and luckily the machete hadn’t hit the engine or any of the really important bits underneath. But the damage to the top was awful – the roof and the roof lining were so badly wrecked they had to replace the entire roof in the end.
