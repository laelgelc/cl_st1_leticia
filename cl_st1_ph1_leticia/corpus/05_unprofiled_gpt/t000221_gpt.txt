Campo Santa Margherita sounds lovely. It says here it’s this really cheerful, picturesque square, with lots of different architectural styles all around it. It’s dedicated to Saint Margaret of Antioch, and apparently her emblem is a dragon, which I quite like. It’s really popular with kids on bikes and skates, so it must feel quite lively and relaxed. And the reason it’s so big is because in the nineteenth century they enlarged it by filling in some of the canals at its southern end.
