in a relationship I’d really expect the motivation to be that you actually want to see the other person, that you’re drawn to them. Not in a needy, dependent way, but because you’re both whole people in your own right. Each of you should have your own life and your own interests, and then bring that to the relationship. For me, that’s the ideal: two complete individuals who come together and create something bigger than either of them alone, something beautiful and beneficial and expansive, but also challenging in a good, stretching way for both partners.
