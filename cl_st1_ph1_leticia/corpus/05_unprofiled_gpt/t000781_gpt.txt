Those are really good theories, but in the end we can’t ever really know if that’s what they were actually thinking. What interests me more is how every creature experiences the world through its own bodily scale. An ant, for instance, must experience everything as absolutely enormous, whereas for a giant the same things would feel tiny and insignificant. That’s why I keep coming back to “scale” rather than just size – it’s the relationship between large and small that really matters, not the absolute measurements.
