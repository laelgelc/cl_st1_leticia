I remember coming out of the Country Girl and realising my car had gone, and I was so shocked I started looking in the daftest places, like over the fence, as if it might somehow be there. I was really thrown by it, just wandering about trying to figure out what had happened. What really got to me, though, was my mate’s reaction. He was supposed to be getting a lift home with me, but instead of sticking around or helping me look, he just said he’d get the bus and disappeared.
