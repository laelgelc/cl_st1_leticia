That woman the other day really got to me. She’d been in before, never ordered anything, then came back wanting wallpaper. So I sat down with her, went through all the books, helped her find exactly what she wanted. While I was writing the order out, she’s copying all the details down. Then she says, “I’ll phone the order through,” and off she goes with the book, having taken up a couple of hours of our time. Of course, the call never comes. Later I look online and there it is – the same wallpaper, twenty‑five percent cheaper than the twenty percent discount I’d already offered her. So she’s just gone off to save a few extra quid, not bothered at all about the time we spent helping her.
