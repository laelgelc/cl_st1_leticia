I had this lovely little travel alarm clock in the bedroom for years, just a small, neat thing, and then it finally broke. I thought I’d just replace it with something similar, so I bought another little one, same sort of size, nice and neat. But the ticking on it was absolutely unbearable. The very first night I had it, I just couldn’t stand it at all. It was so loud that it drove me mad, and I realised there was no way I could sleep with that racket going on beside the bed.
