so what I did with the arena was I had to make sure the monsters would actually spawn inside it, right, because if there’s too much light they just don’t appear. I ended up enclosing the whole redstone arena so that the sunlight couldn’t get in, which meant you’d get all these monsters popping up on this dimly lit floor that still reacted when you stepped on it. The idea was that players could go in and fight them on this kind of glowing, dangerous surface. 

The build itself was really simple, just a dome on a circle, nothing fancy in terms of architecture, but because it was right near a bunch of other people’s stuff it drew loads of attention. People were really up for helping out with it. From the outside, in the middle of this big sandy desert biome, it just looked like this huge stone dome rising out of the sand, so we ended up calling it the “desert dome.”
